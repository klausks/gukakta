## Character Overview

Igga Bevneros is a lyre-playing adventurer—possibly a drow—who was taken in by [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) at the Vistani encampment in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). According to [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}), his destiny is intertwined with the party's.

## Backstory

Unknown. He is not a member of [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) but was welcomed into their encampment under [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) care.

## Goals & Motivations

Unknown; follows his destiny as foretold by [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}).

## Connections

- [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) — his benefactor and the one who revealed his connection to the party's destiny
- The Party: [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}})

## Key Events

### Session 17
Met the party upon their arrival at the Vistani encampment. Greeted them enthusiastically, carrying his lyre, and declared that [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) had foretold their destinies are connected. Shortly after this introduction, the entire group entered [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) cabin together. Appears in [Session 17]({{< ref "/sessions/session-17" >}}).

### Session 21
Used *Detect Magic* on the dolls and rats: confirmed the dolls carry **enchantment** magic and the rats carry **divination** magic. Tested whether the dolls could transmit speech—they cannot, only touch and pressure. Was drained of vital energy by a white-skinned creature that ambushed the party; the creature was killed, dissolved into dust, and [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) verified no magical aura remained in the dust. Cast his mending spell on [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) damaged doll, fully healing [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}); the same effect was confirmed on [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}). The party also recognized that [Igga's]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) mending applied to a doll fully heals its linked bearer. Appeared in [Session 21]({{< ref "/sessions/session-21" >}}).

### Session 22
Recognized the path to the old mill. Remarked that he had never seen the iron gate closed before. Noted the nervous stranger [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) bore a Vistani-style hat. When [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) reached for the key, [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) stepped in to claim it for the party instead. Identified that the dolls near the monolith structure emitted divination magic—different from the enchantment aura on the party's own dolls—and suggested they were being used for observation. Confirmed the magical red candle on the skull inside the cave (no wax melting). Detected strong necromancy energy emanating from deeper in the cave. Appears in [Session 22]({{< ref "/sessions/session-22" >}}).

### Session 24
Created a containment for the voodoo dolls when [Bela's]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) scream caused them to fly out of the party's bags. Rushed inside the mill alongside [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) after [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) knocked [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) unconscious. Cast Mending on [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) doll, restoring [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to full HP. Cast Healing Word on [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), reviving him to consciousness. Appears in [Session 24]({{< ref "/sessions/session-24" >}}).
