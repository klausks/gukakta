## Character Overview

Igga Bevneros is a lyre-playing adventurer—possibly a drow—who was taken in by [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) at the Vistani encampment in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). According to [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}), his destiny is intertwined with the party's.

## Backstory

Unknown. He is not a member of [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) but was welcomed into their encampment under [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) care.
