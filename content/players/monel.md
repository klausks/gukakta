## Character Overview

Monel is a member of the party and a leader figure. He is pragmatic, decisive, and willing to make personal sacrifices for the safety of his companions. He demonstrates strong tactical thinking and a protective instinct toward the group.

## Backstory

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) was born into a good family without major hardship. His father was a master brewer, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) spent much of his youth drinking excessively and engaging in foolish behavior. At some point in his life, he enlisted in the military and adopted a more disciplined, peaceful lifestyle.

However, [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) past contains a profound unexplained mystery: he once found himself transported to a domain consumed by never-ending war. He spent what felt like an infinite number of years in that war-torn land, yet he could never understand what was happening or discover any explanation for how he arrived there or what the domain was. As suddenly as he was transported to that domain, he was inexplicably returned to the normal plane. **[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) has no understanding of this episode and remains deeply frustrated by its incomprehensibility.**

## Key Events

### Session 10 - The Challenge with Strahd
[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) accepted [Strahd von Zarovich's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) challenge to combat in an abandoned house, motivated by a desire to protect the rest of the party from greater harm. His reasoning: if anyone had the best chance of surviving such an encounter, it would be him.

**Combat Performance:**
- Attacked [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) twice, dealing mediocre damage
- Received a devastating critical hit from [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) that nearly killed him instantly
- Was saved by [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) after the rest of the party intervened

**Strategic Insight Gained:**
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) believes [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) was attempting to grandstand and display his power
- The encounter became a learning opportunity: "It was good for us to start understanding the challenge that awaits if we have to face him directly"
- **Key Tactical Discovery:** [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) will only enter a dwelling when explicitly invited. The party can use this as a defensive tactic by entering houses and simply not inviting him inside

Appears in [Session 10]({{< ref "/sessions/session-10" >}}).

### Session 11 - Recovery and Repairs
[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) purchased pies with [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) from a local vendor in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). At the inn, he spent the evening attempting to repair the armor damaged by [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) in the previous session's encounter.

Appears in [Session 11]({{< ref "/sessions/session-11" >}}).

### Session 15 - Backstory and Tactical Response

As the party traveled along the river, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asked [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) about his origins, prompting [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) to reveal his backstory. In turn, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) shared his own deeply troubled past, revealing his origins in a good family, his period of dissolution and drink, his military service, and most significantly—the mysterious war domain from which he cannot explain his transportation or return. When [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) asked [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) why this transportation occurred, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) responded with frustration that he never understood this episode in his life.

Appears in [Session 15]({{< ref "/sessions/session-15" >}}).

### Session 19 - Forest Master
Fought alongside [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) in the first Forest Master duel against [Yakov]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}) and a flaming magic skull. Before the fight, he recalled a phrase from his private session with [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}): *"Battle is my destiny. Pain, my guide. Victory, my truth."* He killed [Yakov]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}) with a shield bash to the face and was formally recognized as a **Forest Master** by [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) after the duels concluded. Appears in [Session 19]({{< ref "/sessions/session-19" >}}).

### Session 20 - The Forest Master's Covenant
Was summoned by [Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}), who emerged from a tree carrying a magical painting. The figure within—[Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}})—commanded [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to enter. Inside, [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) granted him the full privileges of the Forest Master title and revealed that [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) was likely a failed attempt by a war entity to secure a human servant—an explanation connected to the mysterious war domain from his past. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) will receive an item through a forest informant to make contact with this entity. He tested the tree-concealment ability upon returning to his companions and confirmed it works. Appears in [Session 20]({{< ref "/sessions/session-20" >}}).

### Session 21 - Strahd's Invitation
Confirmed to be physically linked to one of the enchanted dolls, likely as a result of eating the suspicious pies. When [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) handled his doll, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) felt the sensation; he later had his doll mended by [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and was fully healed. Upon finding [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) carriage at the road fork, he used his Forest Master tree-concealment ability to scout it from a nearby tree, confirming it was empty. He explained [Lord Balkor's]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) promise about the item to the group and argued against accepting [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) invitation, citing the party's current weakness. Appears in [Session 21]({{< ref "/sessions/session-21" >}}).
