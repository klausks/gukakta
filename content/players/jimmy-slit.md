## Character Overview

Jimmy is the party's rogue. Born into poverty and shaped by violence and loss, he is a seasoned assassin who operates by a personal moral code: he only takes contracts on those he deems worthless. Beneath a hardened, emotionally muted exterior, Jimmy carries the weight of a traumatic past and an inner demon — quite literally — that he has not yet found a way to silence.

## Backstory

Jimmy grew up surrounded by filth and poverty. His father was an alcoholic who terrorized the household, beating not only Jimmy but also his mother Annette and his sister Camila. His father spent nearly all the family's money on drink, leaving them in misery and hunger. To keep his family fed and bring some small comfort to his mother and sister, Jimmy turned to petty theft from a young age.

At fifteen, Jimmy spotted what seemed like an irresistible opportunity: a strikingly ornate talisman sitting unattended on a merchant's counter. Certain it was too good to be true — though he only understood just how true that was later — he stole it without hesitation and brought it home.

He tried to hide the talisman from his father, knowing the man would seize it and sell it for drink without leaving a coin for the family. What Jimmy didn't know was that losing the talisman would have been the best possible outcome compared to what was about to unfold.

His father found the talisman and beat Jimmy savagely for hiding it. Then, entranced by its red glow, he put it around his own neck.

That night, deep in drink, Jimmy's father rose from bed with a dark, hollow look and strangled his wife. Camila woke to the sound of her mother struggling and screamed, but Annette was already still. Jimmy woke moments later to find his father's hands already around Camila's throat, the talisman pulsing red. His father wore a man's face, but his aura was demonic. A paralyzing terror gripped Jimmy, rooting him in place until it was too late to save his sister. The terror gave way to uncontrollable fury. Jimmy seized a lantern and brought it down on his father's head, then kept striking until he lost the strength to continue and his father's face was nothing but a blur.

In one final act of rage — blaming himself for having brought the cursed talisman home — Jimmy smashed the talisman apart. A red aura rose from the shattered jewel like vapor and entered his body. A voice began to torment him in a tongue he had never heard yet somehow understood. Overwhelmed by grief over what had happened and by the voices now screaming inside him, Jimmy collapsed.

When he came to, the world — already grey — had lost all color entirely. The only things he could feel were anger and hatred, and the spirit bound to the talisman seemed deeply satisfied with that.

Alone and aimless, he continued surviving through theft. Not long after, in a fit of rage, he killed a drunk man who threatened him. He felt no pleasure in it, but noticed that killing was one of the few things that quieted the spirit that haunted him.

Operating under the alias **Jimmy Slit**, he built connections in the criminal underworld and began taking assassination contracts, accepting only those targeting people he judged to be worthless. It was through this work that he crossed paths with [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), a con man with whom he completed several jobs.

By the age of twenty-two, Jimmy had become a successful criminal. But the existential void he had long grown accustomed to was now impossible to ignore. He needed to find his place in the world — and a way to rid himself of the demon that tormented him.

## Goals & Motivations

- Find a way to free himself from the demonic spirit bound to him through the talisman.
- Understand what the talisman was, where it came from, and why it was left out in the open for him to steal.
- Find a reason to keep going beyond mere survival.

### Session 3
Heard his inner demon's voice again in the attic of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). The spirit [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) demanded he take his hand; Jimmy refused and suffered damage. ### Session 11
Purchased pies with [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) from a local vendor in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). Took shelter at the inn with the party. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) sensed an unusual chill from [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), which [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) observed and investigated. His demonic companion manifested, urging him to make physical contact with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to discern her condition.

**Critical Discovery:** Through a touch and the demon's perception, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) learned that [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) has been **enchanted by [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}})**. The enchantment presents two possible magical effects:
1. She may be cursed to never awaken from sleep
2. She serves as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) remote eyes, ears, and nose from a distance

Appears in [Session 11]({{< ref "/sessions/session-11" >}}).

### Session 15
[Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) attempted to remove the magical hat from [Pepino Falstaff's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) head, but the hat would not come free and became stuck. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) responded with a sharp slap and a firm warning against attempting this again.

[Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) shared a deeply personal confession with the party: he revealed that his father was a chronic alcoholic who tormented the household, responsible for killing his entire family. However, he survived by killing his father.

Appears in [Session 15]({{< ref "/sessions/session-15" >}}).

### Session 21
Confirmed to be physically linked to one of the enchanted dolls carried by the mice on the bridge—a consequence of eating the suspicious pies. When [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) handled the doll, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) felt the sensation. He proposed using the dolls as a form of communication. During the rest, he observed his doll appeared damaged in a way that mirrored his own injuries and suggested [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast his mending spell on it—resulting in [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) being fully healed. He also scouted [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) carriage stealthily and retrieved the letter inside. Appears in [Session 21]({{< ref "/sessions/session-21" >}}).

### Session 22
Experienced flashes of children running and a tingling in his feet near the iron gate, exchanging a glance with [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}). Approached the nervous stranger [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) and offered him a rope to scale the gate. Shortly after passing through the gate, suffered unexplained psychic damage, emerging with –6 temporary HP. Raised the possibility that overusing the mending-on-doll healing may be causing their ongoing ailments, suggesting restraint. Identified drag-line tracks on the road as likely belonging to the old pie woman. Appears in [Session 22]({{< ref "/sessions/session-22" >}}).

### Session 26
Searched the mill before the party departed and found a hidden chest beneath a bed containing **6 pieces of jewelry**, **200 gold pieces**, and an **adoption document** listing three children — one named **Kaselyn** — referencing an institution under the name "Valak," possibly tied to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}). Later opened the colorful leather pouch looted from [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}), releasing a spirit that his demon immediately consumed. His demon used the opportunity to warn him: *"The stronger I grow, the closer your death becomes."* When [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) dismissed him, the demon dealt 15 damage in retaliation, demanding respect. Privately warned [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) that [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) headache may signal she is acting as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) eyes and ears — which would explain why [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) avoided her. Appears in [Session 26]({{< ref "/sessions/session-26" >}}).

### Session 30
In Vallaki's town center, assessed the tense crowds and public punishment at the Stockade and noted it would provide many opportunities for a thief. Ordered elegant clothing from a tailor for upcoming political meetings. Appears in [Session 30]({{< ref "/sessions/session-30" >}}).

### Session 32
Guards searched the party, confiscated Jimmy's vial of poison, and stated he would be jailed at the Stockade for at least one day. They also demanded the party decide on a group name at Izek's request. Appears in [Session 32]({{< ref "/sessions/session-32" >}}).

### Session 33
Could not avoid punishment: in Vallaki, bail was treated as bribery. Jimmy was hooded and taken through quieter streets into custody, where Izek struck him with an unnaturally burning punch.

Jimmy was brought before [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) and questioned in a torture room about whether the party had ties to "the Devil" and which nobles they had spoken to. After another search, Jimmy was placed in solitary confinement and later given food and water.

Jimmy's demon claimed it can always know the correct path to take and can access a person's memories if Jimmy touches them. Appears in [Session 33]({{< ref "/sessions/session-33" >}}).
