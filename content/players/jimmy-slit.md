## Character Overview

Jimmy is the party's rogue. Born into poverty and shaped by violence and loss, he is a seasoned assassin who operates by a personal moral code: he only takes contracts on those he deems worthless. Beneath a hardened, emotionally muted exterior, Jimmy carries the weight of a traumatic past and an inner demon — quite literally — that he has not yet found a way to silence.

## Backstory

Jimmy grew up surrounded by filth and poverty. His father was an alcoholic who terrorized the household, beating not only Jimmy but also his mother Annette and his sister Camila. His father spent nearly all the family's money on drink, leaving them in misery and hunger. To keep his family fed and bring some small comfort to his mother and sister, Jimmy turned to petty theft from a young age.

At fifteen, Jimmy spotted what seemed like an irresistible opportunity: a strikingly ornate talisman sitting unattended on a merchant's counter. Certain it was too good to be true — though he only understood just how true that was later — he stole it without hesitation and brought it home.

He tried to hide the talisman from his father, knowing the man would seize it and sell it for drink without leaving a coin for the family. What Jimmy didn't know was that losing the talisman would have been the best possible outcome compared to what was about to unfold.

His father found the talisman and beat Jimmy savagely for hiding it. Then, entranced by its red glow, he put it around his own neck.

That night, deep in drink, Jimmy's father rose from bed with a dark, hollow look and strangled his wife. Camila woke to the sound of her mother struggling and screamed, but Annette was already still. Jimmy woke moments later to find his father's hands already around Camila's throat, the talisman pulsing red. His father wore a man's face, but his aura was demonic. A paralyzing terror gripped Jimmy, rooting him in place until it was too late to save his sister. The terror gave way to uncontrollable fury. Jimmy seized a lantern and brought it down on his father's head, then kept striking until he lost the strength to continue and his father's face was nothing but a blur.

In one final act of rage — blaming himself for having brought the cursed talisman home — Jimmy smashed the talisman apart. A red aura rose from the shattered jewel like vapor and entered his body. A voice began to torment him in a tongue he had never heard yet somehow understood. Overwhelmed by grief over what had happened and by the voices now screaming inside him, Jimmy collapsed.

When he came to, the world — already grey — had lost all color entirely. The only things he could feel were anger and hatred, and the spirit bound to the talisman seemed deeply satisfied with that.

Alone and aimless, he continued surviving through theft. Not long after, in a fit of rage, he killed a drunk man who threatened him. He felt no pleasure in it, but noticed that killing was one of the few things that quieted the spirit that haunted him.

Operating under the alias **Jimmy Slit**, he built connections in the criminal underworld and began taking assassination contracts, accepting only those targeting people he judged to be worthless. It was through this work that he crossed paths with [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), a con man with whom he completed several jobs.

By the age of twenty-two, Jimmy had become a successful criminal. But the existential void he had long grown accustomed to was now impossible to ignore. He needed to find his place in the world — and a way to rid himself of the demon that tormented him.

## Goals & Motivations

- Find a way to free himself from the demonic spirit bound to him through the talisman.
- Understand what the talisman was, where it came from, and why it was left out in the open for him to steal.
- Find a reason to keep going beyond mere survival.
