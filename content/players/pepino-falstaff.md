## Character Overview

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) is the party's con artist and a half-elf whose past is marked by loss and abandonment. Driven initially by necessity and the need for money to survive, he has become a skilled practitioner of deception and manipulation. Despite his criminal background, [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) demonstrates loyalty to his companions and tactical awareness in dangerous situations.

## Backstory

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) is a half-elf orphan who believes he was abandoned by his parents. With no family to support him, he was forced to pursue adventure and criminal work out of sheer necessity—driven by the need to obtain money for basic survival. His childhood was compounded by tragedy: he had a sister, but she died while he was still young, leaving him as a true orphan without familial bonds. These experiences shaped him into someone who survives through cunning and deception.

## Inventory

- **Beautiful Hat** - A magical hat discovered in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) that grants visibility to a floating golden sword. When [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) wears the hat, he can see a golden sword with a swirling design on its hilt. The sword becomes invisible without the hat. The connection and true properties of the hat remain under investigation.

## Key Events

### Session 14

After the combat, [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) used dissonant whispers to frighten a bear that was threatening the party during their exploration of a clearing. He subsequently discovered a golden sword floating in the clearing. Pursuing it, a[Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) stumbled and the hat fell from his head—causing the sword to vanish immediately. Upon replacing the hat, the sword became visible again. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) theorized that the hat grants visual perception of the sword and proposed following the sword's trajectory, which aligned with the party's intended direction toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

During the party's short rest, [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) advised that magic item properties can be discovered through meditation. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) meditated with the hat to investigate its properties. The results of his meditation remain to be determined.

### Session 15
[Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) revealed the story of his origins to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) as the party traveled along the riverbank. He disclosed that he began his adventures out of necessity and the need for money, born from his isolation as a half-elf orphan who believes he was abandoned by his parents. The loss that most deeply marked his past was the death of his sister when he was still young.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) attempted to remove the magical hat from [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) head, but the hat would not come off and became stuck. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) responded by slapping [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) hand and issuing a firm warning: "Never try that again."
