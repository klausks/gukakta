## Overview

Castle Ravenloft dominates the skyline of Barovia from its perch atop the thousand-foot-tall spire of Mount Ghakis.
The castle is ancient beyond memory — part fortress, part mausoleum, part labyrinth — and it pulses with the dark will of its eternal master.
