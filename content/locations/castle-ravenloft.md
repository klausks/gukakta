## Overview

Castle Ravenloft dominates the skyline of Barovia from its perch atop the thousand-foot-tall spire of Mount Ghakis.
The castle is ancient beyond memory — part fortress, part mausoleum, part labyrinth — and it pulses with the dark will of its eternal master. The interior is intensely cold.

## Notable Locations

- **Dining Hall** — where [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) received the party for a formal banquet. Contains a pipe organ decorated with human skulls and human bones. Two large mirrors in the room serve as hidden secret entrances; their destinations are unknown but appear to be interconnected.
- **Main Entrance Hall** — lined with stone statues of dragons; a carving of Strahd looking downward is set into the walls. No secret mechanisms or magical anomalies were detected on first inspection.

## Key NPCs

- [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) — lord of the castle
- [Rahadin]({{< ref "/npcs/rahadin" >}}) — Strahd's trusted attendant

## Restrictions (as told by Strahd)

- The **second floor** and the **crypt** are off limits to guests.
- Visitors may only remain until **midnight**.

## Key Events

### Session 46
[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) arrived at the castle after stepping through the portal at the winery basement. They were received by Strahd at a formal banquet in the dining hall, where a pale-handed figure played the pipe organ. Strahd requested the green gem, answered one question from each party member, and permitted them to explore the first floor until midnight. [Swigas]({{< ref "/npcs/swigas" >}}) appeared briefly escorted by Rahadin. Appears in [Session 46]({{< ref "/sessions/session-46" >}}).
