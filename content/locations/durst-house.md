## Overview

The Durst House is a house the party discovered at the end of the only fog-free path through the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). It appears to be the home of the Durst family, though the parents were not present when the party arrived.

Outside the house, the party met two children — [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) — who told the party they were afraid to go inside because of a monster their parents kept locked in the basement.

## Notable Locations

- **Basement** — reportedly where the Durst family keeps a monster imprisoned.
