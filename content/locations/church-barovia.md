## Overview

The Barovia Church is the primary place of worship in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) and is presided over by the priest [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}). It serves as the center for funeral rites, burials, and spiritual guidance in the cursed land.

## Notable Locations

- **Main Sanctuary:** Where religious ceremonies are held
- **Locked Door:** A sealed door that emits disturbing whispered screams, as witnessed during the Burgomaster's funeral

## Key NPCs

- [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}) — the church's priest

## Key Events

### Session 9 - The Burgomaster's Funeral
- The party brought the Burgomaster's coffin to the church for burial ceremony.
- The funeral was presided over by [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}).
- **First Interruption:** Whispered screams emanated from behind a locked door within the church.
- **Second Interruption:** The same disturbing whispered screams occurred again, interrupting the ceremony.
