## Overview

The Svalich Road is a named road in Barovia. The party has traveled it while moving between major points of interest.

## Notable Locations

- **Aligned Tree(s)** — a named route/landmark referenced by a sign at a fork in the road. Near here, the party later observed beetles burrowing into the wood and collected black liquid as a potential poison sample.
- **Berez** — a signed destination at a crossroads (south). See [Berez]({{< ref "/locations/berez" >}}).
- **Mysterious Temple** — a surprisingly clean temple/dungeon discovered off the road. See [Mysterious Temple]({{< ref "/locations/mysterious-temple" >}}).
- **Forest archway trail** — a fork where an arch leads into a forest trail; from this route the party identified a castle within Lake Baratok.
- **Uncle Billy’s Orchard** — a named orchard on the way to the winery. See [Uncle Billy’s Orchard]({{< ref "/locations/uncle-billy-orchard" >}}).

## Key NPCs

Unknown.

## Key Events and Discoveries

### Session 41
The party follows the Svalich Road as the mist thickens near the Waschter Forest and encounters mercenary wagons and a sudden fork in the road. Appears in [Session 41]({{< ref "/sessions/session-41" >}}).

### Session 42
After the ambush at the fork, the party investigates strange changes around the Aligned Tree(s), sees Vallakian mercenaries finishing off similar creatures, and takes shelter in a padlocked temple. Appears in [Session 42]({{< ref "/sessions/session-42" >}}).

### Session 43
After exploring the temple’s sealed tombs, the party returns to the road, notes dangerous spore-heavy mushrooms, and reaches a new fork leading toward Lake Baratok, snowy mountains, and the road onward to Krezk and the winery. Appears in [Session 43]({{< ref "/sessions/session-43" >}}).
