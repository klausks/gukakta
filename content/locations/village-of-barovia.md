## Overview

The Village of Barovia is the first settlement adventurers encounter after crossing through the mist and the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). Its residents are hollow-eyed, despairing souls who rarely venture outside after dark. The village is characterized by decadent, shuttered buildings and an overall atmosphere of decay and abandonment. The village is ruled by [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), a vampire lord who has cursed the land, trapping all inhabitants in a perpetual state of despair.

## Notable Locations & Establishments

### The Inn
The primary lodging establishment in the village. The innkeeper maintains the establishment, offering room and board to travelers.

### Street Vendor (Pie Seller)
An **old woman** operates a makeshift food stall in the village streets, selling pies. Her goods are exceptional in quality—consumption of her pies grants a notable health restoration. The woman is hospitable and provides directions to travelers.

## Key NPCs

### Ismark
[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) is the son of the Burgomaster and serves as a de facto leader of the village. He was the mysterious benefactor who paid for the party's stay. His father died recently to wolf attacks and requires burial. He is deeply concerned about his sister [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), who is the target of [Count Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) personal interest.

### Arik
[Arik]({{< ref "/npcs/arik" >}}) is the primary staff member at the village inn. He handles all tavern operations, though he is not the owner.

### Donavich
[Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}) is the village priest. He performs funeral rites and provides spiritual guidance to the community.

### The Pie Vendor (Old Woman)
An **old woman** operates a makeshift food stall in the village streets, selling pies. Her goods are exceptional in quality—consumption of her pies grants notable health restoration.


### Session 8 - Morning in Barovia & New Allies
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) woke feeling "Well Rested" after pleasant dreams. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) required waking.
- The mysterious benefactor was revealed to be [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), the Burgomaster's son.
- The party met [Arik]({{< ref "/npcs/arik" >}}), the tavern's primary staff member.
- [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) provided breakfast and wine, then shared critical information:
  - His father died recently in a wolf attack and requires burial.
  - [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) is a cursed land ruled by the vampire lord [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}).
  - [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) has taken a personal, obsessive interest in [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) sister, [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}).
  - He requested the party escort [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to safety outside the cursed land.
- The party agreed to help but prioritized gathering information, meeting with a merchant, and assisting with [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) father's burial.
- The party met [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}), the village priest, who was assisting with funeral arrangements.
- Appears in [Session 8]({{< ref "/sessions/session-8" >}}).
## Key Events

### Session 7 - Emergence from the Curse
- The party arrived in the Village of Barovia after the haunted magic of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) dissipated and its surrounding fog lifted.
- Encountered the **old woman selling pies**. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) purchased 4 pieces and consumed 1, receiving a health boost from the exceptional food.
- Sought lodging at the **inn** following the old woman's directions.
- A **mysterious man** paid for the party's entire stay to the innkeeper.
- The party received water and cloth for washing and rested comfortably overnight, each in their own bed.
- Appears in [Session 7]({{< ref "/sessions/session-7" >}}).
