## Overview

The Baron's Mansion is the residence of [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}), the Burgomaster of Vallaki. His son [Viktor Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/viktor-vallakovich" >}}) maintains a secret study in the mansion's attic.

## Notable Locations

- **Attic** — used by Viktor as a private workshop filled with magical scrolls, enchantment circles, and dolls. The magic present is of ancient Fey origin. A magical portal from the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}) leads here.

## Key NPCs

- [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) — owner and Burgomaster of Vallaki
- [Viktor Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/viktor-vallakovich" >}}) — occupies the attic

## Key Events

### Session 47

Part of the party arrived in the attic through the magical portal from the winery. Viktor confronted them and, after being convinced they were there by accident, suggested they wait until nightfall to leave quietly. Appears in [Session 47]({{< ref "/sessions/session-47" >}}).
