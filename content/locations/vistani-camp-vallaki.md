## Overview

A Vistani camp of approximately 30 people situated near Vallaki. Initially introduced as an expected stop where the merchant [Tolmul]({{< ref "/npcs/tolmul" >}}) would be found, the camp was visited by [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) the day before Vallaki's festival.

## Notable Locations

- **Central tent** — a large tent from which smoke emerges, accompanied by the sounds of cries and whipping. The party learned that this is where members who fail in their duties are punished.

## Key NPCs

- [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}) — leader of the camp; knowledgeable about regional politics and the Abbey of Saint Markovia
- [Louvash]({{< ref "/npcs/louvash" >}}) — Arrigal's brother; father of Arabelle
- [Tolmul]({{< ref "/npcs/tolmul" >}}) — merchant operating from the camp; deals in armor, weapons, and rare goods
- [Irmno]({{< ref "/npcs/irmno" >}}) — Vistani who accompanied the party during the search for Arabelle

## Key Events and Discoveries

### Session 52
Jimmy and Monel arrived at the camp and were escorted to Tolmul, who delivered Monel's promised item and traded with both characters. Tolmul mentioned having sold a unique map — possibly connected to [Rudolph van Richten]({{< ref "/npcs/rudolph-van-richten" >}}) — to an unknown adventurer. He directed the party to Arrigal for information about restoring Jimmy's severed hand. Referenced in [Session 52]({{< ref "/sessions/session-52" >}}).

### Session 53
Jimmy and Monel met Arrigal, who was occupied with the disappearance of his niece [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}). The party tracked her to [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}) and recovered her from the fisherman [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}})'s house. Upon her return, the party was rewarded with a detailed map and invited to the Vistani feast, which they attended briefly. Arrigal shared intelligence about Vallaki, Krezk, and the Abbey of Saint Markovia before the party departed. Referenced in [Session 53]({{< ref "/sessions/session-53" >}}).
