## Overview

Wachterhaus is the residence of Fiona Wachter, a noble figure in Vallaki. The party visited the house for an arranged introduction.

## Notable Locations

Unknown.

- [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}}) — butler
- [Stella Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/stella-wachter" >}}) — Fiona’s daughter

## Key Events and Discoveries

### Session 35
The party was received by Halik and met Fiona Wachter. They witnessed Stella Wachter behaving strangely, and a brief fight broke out with an [Invisible Fiend]({{< ref "/creatures/invisible-fiend" >}}) inside the house before it escaped through a glass window. Appears in [Session 35]({{< ref "/sessions/session-35" >}}).

### Session 36
The party dealt with a captured “diabrete” at Wachterhaus, caging the wounded fiend for questioning as tensions rose within the household. Fiona Wachter spoke candidly over dinner about Vallaki’s politics, her network of watchers, and her belief that the Baron’s festivals might be ritualistic and could attract “the Devil.”
