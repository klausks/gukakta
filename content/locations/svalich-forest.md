## Overview

The Svalich Forest is the dense, fog-laden woodland that surrounds and borders the land of Barovia. The party first became aware of its name from [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}), who identified it as the forest the party had been travelling through before the mists pulled them into Barovia.

The forest is perpetually shrouded in thick mist. The party discovered that the ground itself seemed to be alive and hostile — objects left near the earth were slowly pulled downward and absorbed. Their horses disappeared without a trace during the night they camped here.

## Notable Locations

- [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) — a house that emerged from the mist along the one fog-free path.

## Key Events

### Session 1 - Discovery
The party was travelling through this forest when the mists pulled them into Barovia. During their travels, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) detected that the ground was absorbing objects and slowly pulling them downward. He alerted the rest of the party, preventing the loss of most belongings, though their horses disappeared without a trace.

As the party travelled deeper into the forest, they found that aside from a single clear path, the entire area was covered in thick fog. This path eventually revealed the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}), where they made their first contact with Barovia's inhabitants.

### Session 14 - Ambush and Discovery
Approximately 30 minutes into the party's three-day journey toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) from [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), two furry creatures ambushed and challenged [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) to a duel, claiming forest rules mandated the challenge. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) identified the creatures as impostors and the challenge as a trap, prompting immediate combat. During the confrontation, ranged creatures attacked the party with arrows before withdrawing. A raven bearing parchments was observed overhead during the initial confrontation.

After defeating the creatures, the party discovered a fork in the path. Following [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) intuition, they selected the right path, which had less fog and more vibrant vegetation. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) encountered a clearing where a bear was feeding, with a golden sword (featuring a swirling design on its hilt) and a beautiful hat nearby. The sword vanished when the hat was removed from the area. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) discovered that wearing the hat made the sword visible. The party decided to follow the sword's direction, which aligned with their intended path toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

### Session 15 - The River and Second Challenge
Continuing deeper into [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}), the party heard the sound of a body of water ([Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) suggested following it). The party consulted their map and identified the water source, finding a marked Vistani encampment nearby that [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) confirmed contained Vistani who wish them well. The magical sword continued to float in a direction aligned with the Vistani camp's location on the map.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) became disoriented while attempting to navigate the forest and the party chose to follow the magical sword instead. As they traveled along the riverbank, the party members engaged in deeply personal conversations, sharing their backstories and pasts.

A second ambush occurred when a large armored man named Hoge emerged from the forest and challenged [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) to a duel, claiming the party had violated "the forest's rules"—using the exact same phrasing as the two furry creatures in the previous encounter. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) immediately recognized the ambush pattern and initiated combat with Hoge without waiting for further dialogue, having learned from the previous false duel encounter.
