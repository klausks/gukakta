## Overview

The Burgomaster's Mansion is the principal residence of the Burgomaster and his family in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). It serves as the de facto seat of authority in the village and houses [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) and [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), the Burgomaster's children.

## Notable Locations

- **Main Hall:** Meeting and dining area where [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) provided supplies to the party

## Key NPCs

- [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) — the Burgomaster's daughter
- [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) — the Burgomaster's son
