## Overview

Lake Zarovich is a body of water located a short distance north of the [Vistani camp near Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vistani-camp-vallaki" >}}). The lake is known locally and is frequented by the fisherman [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}}), who has a house on its shore.

## Notable Locations

- **Bluto's house** — a dwelling on the lake shore; home to the fisherman Bluto.

## Key NPCs

- [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}}) — fisherman; lives on the shore

## Key Events and Discoveries

### Session 53
[Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}), the missing daughter of the Vistani Louvash, was tracked to this lake. A light spotted on the water led [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to Bluto's shore house, where she was found locked in a sack. Referenced in [Session 53]({{< ref "/sessions/session-53" >}}).
