## Overview

The Wizard of Wines Winery is a winery operated by Urwin Martikov's family. Wine has been in short supply in Vallaki for weeks, and Urwin claims the disruption is not due to natural causes.

He suspects druids and corrupted creatures may be involved, possibly under the influence of Strahd.

## Key Events and Discoveries

### Session 31
Urwin asked the party to travel to the winery and investigate why deliveries have been failing for weeks, noting the location is roughly eight hours from Vallaki. Appears in [Session 31]({{< ref "/sessions/session-31" >}}).

### Session 33
Urwin explained that three magical gems once empowered the winery. The first was stolen around ten years ago; the second was taken about three weeks ago without signs of a struggle; the third was taken about five days ago, with fighting that the family could not withstand. With the winery overrun by blights and plant growth, the family abandoned the site and asked the party to help clear it. Appears in [Session 33]({{< ref "/sessions/session-33" >}}).
