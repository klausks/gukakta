## Overview

*Wines of Barovia* is a book found at or near the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}). It contains viniculture lore and, notably, a poem that serves as the activation phrase for the magical portal in the winery's basement.

## The Portal Poem

[Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) discovered in [Session 47]({{< ref "/sessions/session-47" >}}) that reciting the following poem opens the portal:

> *"Let this wine, sweet and bold,*
> *Warm our hearts and soothe our souls.*
> *With each sip, let us find delight,*
> *And banish all worries with its might."*

## Significance

- The portal appears to lead to different destinations depending on the circumstances. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) ended up at [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}), while [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) and the others arrived in the attic of the [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}) in Vallaki.
- The book's full contents and any other significance are unknown.
