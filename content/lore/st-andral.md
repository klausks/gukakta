## Background

St. Andral is a saint venerated in Vallaki. His crypt is said to lie beneath the altar of St. Andral’s Church, but it is not visibly accessible.

## Significance

The church is associated with the bones of St. Andral, described as a powerful holy relic. A feast in St. Andral’s honor is held in Vallaki.
