## Background

St. Andral is a saint venerated in Vallaki. His crypt is said to lie beneath the altar of St. Andral’s Church, but it is not visibly accessible.

## Significance

The church is associated with the bones of St. Andral, described as a powerful holy relic. A feast in St. Andral’s honor is held in Vallaki.

According to [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}), St. Andral once drove Strahd from the sanctuary through the force of faith after repeated attempts by the Devil to enter and take revenge on those sheltering there. So long as the saint remains in that sanctuary, Lucian believes those inside remain protected from evil.

## Current Status

The bones of St. Andral were stolen from the crypt on the eve of the festival by gravekeeper [Milivoj]({{< ref "/npcs/milivoj" >}}), who delivered the skull to [Henrich Van der Hood]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}), Vallaki's coffin maker. In [Session 55]({{< ref "/sessions/session-55" >}}), the party recovered the relic from Henrich's house, though these notes do not yet confirm it has been restored to the church.
