## Background

While imprisoned in Vallaki, [Udo Rikalova]({{< ref "/npcs/udo-rikalova" >}}) described a pro-Strahd cult operating in the town. According to Udo, the cult was founded by Lovina Wachter an unknown number of generations ago and is currently led by [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}).

Udo claimed that each member wears a specific medallion and uses ceremonial clothing: robe and mask.

## Significance

If Udo's account is accurate, the cult represents an organised pro-Strahd faction inside Vallaki and may be connected to other plots unfolding on the eve of the festival.
