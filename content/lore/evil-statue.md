## Background

A statue of extreme malevolence dwells in the southern basement of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). Its nature and origin remain unknown, but its presence radiates overwhelming evil that affects even the most stalwart of souls.

## Significance

During [Session 6]({{< ref "/sessions/session-6" >}}), [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) detected the statue while using Divine Sense and was so overwhelmed by its evil aura that he vomited. [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demon familiar issued an explicit warning: any direct contact with the statue would result in immediate death ("turning to dust").

The party chose to avoid the statue and continue their search for the bodies of the Durst family.
