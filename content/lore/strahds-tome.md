## Background

The party recovered a remarkable magical tome from the top of the old mill after their return from the Ethereal Plane.

Later, the party learned the tome is [Count Strahd von Zarovich's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) personal diary from his childhood.

## Significance

The tome is treated as dangerous and politically compromising. The party agreed to keep it in absolute secrecy, believing that even spoken words about it could reach unwanted ears.

The tome is also a personal turning point for [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}): continued reading brought mounting pressure from darkness and a crisis of identity, culminating in Pepino adopting the name **Vedas**.

See also: [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}).
