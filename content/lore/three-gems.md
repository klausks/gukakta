## Background

The Martikov family of the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}) previously described **three magical gems** as the source of the winery’s magic, and noted that all three have been stolen over time.

In [Session 44]({{< ref "/sessions/session-44" >}}), [Adrian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/adrian-martikov" >}}) reports that a **green gem** is currently empowering the hostile forces occupying the winery grounds.

## What Is Known

- The green gem **empowers** the attackers at/around the winery (reported alongside vine blights and forest humanoids).
- As of [Session 44]({{< ref "/sessions/session-44" >}}), it was said to be in the hands of **two wild women** and **a bearded man** carrying a **staff**.
- Adrian claims the gem-bearers **came from the swamp at Berez**. See: [Berez]({{< ref "/locations/berez" >}}).
- The staff is described as a priority objective, and taking it would be a significant affront to [Baba Lysaga]({{< ref "/npcs/baba-lysaga" >}}).
- [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) has requested the party deliver the green gem to him. He claims it is needed to **resurrect his mother**, who died in an accident. The party sensed he was telling the truth when he said this.

## Significance

If this green gem is one of the winery’s missing magical gems, it may explain both:

- why the winery’s defenses/vines have withered without them, and
- why the invading plant creatures are unnaturally hardy and coordinated.

The gem’s reported connection to Berez and Baba Lysaga suggests the theft and use of the gem may be part of a broader Strahd-aligned effort to cripple the Martikovs and their ability to supply wine.
