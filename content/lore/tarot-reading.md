## Background

During [Session 18]({{< ref "/sessions/session-18" >}}), [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) read the party's destiny using a Tarot deck. She drew five cards The sequence in which the cards were drawn is likely the order in which events will unfold. [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) noted there is no strictly prescribed order, but that the party will "probably follow the order in which the cards were presented."

## The Five Cards

### 1. The Charlatan — 7 of Glyphs
*Speaks of the past.*

Seeking secret knowledge will help the party better understand their enemy. The party must seek **"knowledge about the direct enemy, from the devil's own mouth."** This knowledge is to be found at **a mill on the heights**, where a treasure also awaits.

**Known:** [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) confirmed the mill lies on the road to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

---

### 2. The Avenger — 1 of Swords
*A powerful force, in service of good and protection. A sacred symbol of great hope.*

The treasure is located in **the house of a dragon** — a place described as *"once clean hands, and now corrupted."*

---

### 3. The Hooded One — 7 of Swords

A **faceless god** awaits the party at the end of a long and winding road. There is a treasure hidden in **a small castle beneath a mountain**, guarded by **giants of a certain mineral**. The treasure was created by hands that sought not only knowledge, but something more. The party will find no answers there—only a **horrifying power**.

---

### 4. The Temptress — Queen of Swords

*Reveals a candle in the devil's darkness.* This card speaks of who will help the party in the battle against the darkness: **a Vistani woman searching for her powerful mentor**. She is to be found at the **Abbey of Saint Markovia, near the mists**.

---

### 5. The Mist — King of (?)
*Speaks of the enemy.*

When the time comes—whether the party is prepared or not—**the lord of darkness will find them at [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}})**. He is nowhere and everywhere at once. He is a creature of darkness with powers beyond mortal bonds, and predicting the exact moment of his defeat is as uncertain as predicting where the lord of darkness stands at any given moment.

After **one or two visits to [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}})**, the final confrontation will take place.

## Significance

This reading provides a rough map of the party's journey and the obstacles ahead. The five cards point toward five distinct destinations or encounters, culminating in a confrontation with [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) at [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}).
