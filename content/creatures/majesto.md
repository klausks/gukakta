## Overview

Majesto is an **imp** serving as [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}})'s familiar. It moves through Vallaki while remaining invisible to most observers, acting as Fiona's eyes and informant. Only Agares has been able to perceive it.

## Abilities & Tactics

- Invisibility or a similar effect that prevented most observers from seeing it.

## Encounter Notes

### Session 35
Only Agares perceived the imp. During the fight, Igga marked it with red ink, Halik struck it with a distinct dagger, and Agares heavily wounded it. The imp escaped by crashing through a glass window, badly injured and frightened by magic. Appears in [Session 35]({{< ref "/sessions/session-35" >}}).

### Session 36
The imp was captured and placed into a cage. Its wings were torn and it did not appear to regenerate. It repeatedly insisted, “I serve the Devil,” and stared at [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}}). Appears in [Session 36]({{< ref "/sessions/session-36" >}}).

### Session 38
While the party tours Wachterhaus, a common name is given for the captured imp: **Majesto**. It is also heard repeating *"My darv, my darv"* (Infernal for "my lord"). Appears in [Session 38]({{< ref "/sessions/session-38" >}}).

### Session 39
The party learns that Majesto is a **familiar** connected to [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and Halik, and that this has been kept secret for political reasons. Majesto is later described as healed and able to fly while remaining invisible. Appears in [Session 39]({{< ref "/sessions/session-39" >}}).

### Session 51
Majesto was spotted by Agares peering through a window during the party's breakfast meeting with [Baron Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}), observing in secret while remaining invisible to everyone else. This suggests Fiona Wachter could already be aware that the Baron has tasked the party with investigating her. Appears in [Session 51]({{< ref "/sessions/session-51" >}}).
