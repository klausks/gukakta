## Overview

An unseen fiend entered Wachterhaus alongside Fiona Wachter and moved through the room while remaining invisible to everyone except Agares.

## Abilities & Tactics

- Invisibility or a similar effect that prevented most observers from seeing it.

## Encounter Notes

### Session 35
Only Agares perceived the fiend. During the fight, Igga marked it with red ink, Halik struck it with a distinct dagger, and Agares heavily wounded it. The fiend escaped by crashing through a glass window, badly injured and frightened by magic. Appears in [Session 35]({{< ref "/sessions/session-35" >}}).

### Session 36
The fiend was captured and placed into a cage. Its wings were torn and it did not appear to regenerate. It repeatedly insisted, “I serve the Devil,” and stared at [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}}). Appears in [Session 36]({{< ref "/sessions/session-36" >}}).

### Session 38
While the party tours Wachterhaus, a common name is given for the captured fiend: **Majesto**. It is also heard repeating *"My darv, my darv"* (Infernal for "my lord"). Appears in [Session 38]({{< ref "/sessions/session-38" >}}).

### Session 39
The party learns that Majesto is a **familiar** connected to [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and Halik, and that this has been kept secret for political reasons. Majesto is later described as healed and able to fly while remaining invisible. Appears in [Session 39]({{< ref "/sessions/session-39" >}}).
