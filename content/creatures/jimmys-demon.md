## Overview

This entity was bound within a stolen talisman that [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) brought home as a teenager. When the talisman was shattered, the demon transferred into Jimmy's body, where it resided for years — communicating with Jimmy in a tongue he had never heard yet somehow understood, feeding on his anger, and growing stronger over time. It could extract memories from people through Jimmy's touch of his right hand.

For the duration of its residence in Jimmy, the demon served as a constant presence of psychic surveillance — Jimmy unconsciously felt observed at all times. It warned him: *"The stronger I grow, the closer your death becomes."*

The demon appeared to be partially lodged in Jimmy's soul as well as his body, with its main anchor points in his head and right hand.

During an exorcism performed by [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) in the crypt of [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}), the demon was forced into Jimmy's right hand via holy water, and expelled when Jimmy severed the hand. The creature emerged as **black smoke**, partially coalescing into a form described as a disturbing hybrid of demon and aberration. It was not yet fully manifested, but escaped through the crypt gate — wrenching it from its axis — before it could be contained. It is now loose and actively regenerating its physical form.

## Abilities (Observed)

- Memory extraction through physical touch (via Jimmy's right hand)
- Psychic torment and communication
- Forced partial possession / influence over Jimmy's actions
- Partial physical manifestation in smoke form sufficient to break a reinforced gate

## Connections

- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) — former host; severed the bond by removing his right hand

## Key Events

### Sessions 3–50 (within Jimmy)
Resided within Jimmy Slit from the night the talisman was shattered. Communicated with him throughout the campaign, offering information and threats. Used Jimmy's right hand to extract intelligence from targets. Tormented him psychically and dealt direct damage when defied. Referenced across multiple sessions.

### Session 51
Expelled from Jimmy's body after the Iron Vigil rite and the severing of Jimmy's right hand. Briefly took partial form as black smoke with a monstrous silhouette, then escaped through the crypt gate of St. Andral's Church, breaking it from its axis. Now free and regenerating. Appears in [Session 51]({{< ref "/sessions/session-51" >}}).
