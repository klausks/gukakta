## Overview

Vine blights are described as small, spiky humanoid pests that resemble roots. Year after year they attempt to drain life from the Martikov vines.

## Abilities & Tactics

As described by Adrian:

- Do not speak
- Attack at range or restrain targets with thorns

## Encounter Notes

### Session 44
Adrian describes the blights as the most persistent threat to the winery and vineyard. Appears in [Session 44]({{< ref "/sessions/session-44" >}}).
