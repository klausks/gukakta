## Overview

Hianag Darastrix was a High Elf (Solar Elf) born into nobility on an unnamed green island, whose life was defined by tragic enslavement to multiple powerful forces. His tale survives through a letter attached to a gold ring left behind on a forest path near Barovia. 

## History

In his childhood, Hianag was captured and enslaved by a Black Dragon named Nithhogr Nightwing. Decades later, he was "rescued" by a drow patrol, only to become a slave for House Do'Urden in Menzoberranzan, the City of Spiders. During a century of slavery among the drow, he was forced to assist priestesses in childbirth and help the Matron Mother sacrifice male babies. 

He retained his sanity through the kindness of a male drow, Drizzt Do'Urden, who secretly taught him the way of the warrior. Thanks to Drizzt pleading on his behalf to his mother, Matron Malice, Hianag was spared from sacrifice to the Spider Queen. Instead, he was sent on a suicidal mission to kill Nithhogr.

When Hianag arrived, he found the dragon already dead. However, its skeletal remains arose to attack him. After a brutal three-day battle, the grievously wounded Hianag wandered the tunnels until he was saved by [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}). His "rescue" merely initiated a new enslavement. For the next 300 years, he served under Strahd's command during the [War with Duke Gundar]({{< ref "/lore/war-with-duke-gundar" >}}) and operated across the continent as Strahd's assassin, sustained by a false promise of eventual freedom. Hianag's letter was signed in the year 650 of the Barovian Calendar.
