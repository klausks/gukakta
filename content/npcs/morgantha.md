## Overview

Morgantha is one of the mill sisters and appears to be the most powerful of the group. She was not present during the party's initial entry into the mill, but arrived during the battle on her own terms — announcing her own coming through the dying words of her sister [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}). Upon arrival, she displayed devastating power: destroying the party's voodoo dolls with a single clap of her hands and putting [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to sleep.

**Note:** [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) named her sisters as Bela and Morgantha. Whether [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}) is a separate individual or another name for Morgantha remains unclear.

## Personality

Composed and deliberate. She arrived after her sisters had already been defeated, suggesting she was aware of the battle's progress and chose her moment to intervene.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown. Appears to be connected to the mill's operations involving captives and harvested body parts.

## Abilities

- **Doll Destruction:** Destroyed the party's linked voodoo dolls by clapping her hands, instantly rendering their bearers ([Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})) unconscious.
- **Sleep:** Put [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to sleep.
- **Ray of Enfeeblement:** Attempted on [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}); failed.
