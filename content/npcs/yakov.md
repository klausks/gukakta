## Overview

Yakov was a dark-haired human who challenged [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to a duel for the Forest Master title. He fought alongside a flaming magic skull as his second combatant and was mentored by [Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}), a figure in fine red armor who was present at the duel.

## Personality

Unknown beyond his role as a challenger.

## Key Events

### Session 19
Challenged [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to a duel as part of the Forest Master trials at the Vistani encampment. His second combatant was a flaming magic skull, which he revealed dramatically during the fight. He was killed by [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) with a shield bash to the face.
