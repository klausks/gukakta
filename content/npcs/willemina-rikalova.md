## Overview

Willemina Rikalova is a woman from Vallaki who approached the party outside [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}) to ask for help rescuing her son, [Udo]({{< ref "/npcs/udo-rikalova" >}}). She is distressed by her son's recent behavioral changes and his subsequent imprisonment by the Baron.

## Personality

Desperate and direct.

## Goals and Motivations

- Rescue her son Udo from the Baron's custody.

## Connections

- [Udo Rikalova]({{< ref "/npcs/udo-rikalova" >}}) \u2014 her imprisoned son

## Key Events

### Session 50
Approached the party outside St. Andral's Church while Jimmy and Father Lucian met privately inside. Explained that her son Udo had stopped attending church, started going out at night wearing dark cloaks, and developed ties to the Wachter family. He was arrested after being caught carrying a sign calling for the Burgomaster to be thrown to the wolves. She offered **50 gp** for his rescue from the Baron's custody. Appears in [Session 50]({{< ref "/sessions/session-50" >}}).
