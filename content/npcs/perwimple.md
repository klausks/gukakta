## Overview

Perwimple is the personal bodyguard of the merchant [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}})). He serves as protection for [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}).

## Personality

Unknown; not directly interacted with by the party beyond presence.

## Goals and Motivations

- Protect [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) from harm

## Connections

- [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) — his employer

## Key Events

### Session 9
Appeared as [Bildrat's]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) personal bodyguard when the party visited the merchant's shop. Appears in [Session 9]({{< ref "/sessions/session-9" >}}).

### Session 16
[Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) was encountered deep within [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) alongside [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) and [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}). His presence in the forest far from [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) is unexplained. [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) made a cryptic suggestion that it would be beneficial if someone were to challenge [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) to a duel, though the reasoning behind this remark remains unclear. Appears in [Session 16]({{< ref "/sessions/session-16" >}}).

### Session 17
[Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) was revealed as the designated representative and dueling champion of [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}). [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) sent him to duel the party on [Lord Balkor's]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) behalf. He demonstrated absolute, unquestioning obedience to [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}})—when ordered to suffocate [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}), he immediately complied. The duel was averted when [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) offered to fight in the party's place, causing [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) to withdraw. Appears in [Session 17]({{< ref "/sessions/session-17" >}}).

### Session 19
Appeared uninvited at the Forest Master dueling ground alongside [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}), crashing the duel. He was paired against [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) in the second duel, fighting on [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) side. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) killed him with a *Chromatic Orb* (fire). Appears in [Session 19]({{< ref "/sessions/session-19" >}}).
