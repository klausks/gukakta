## Overview

[Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) is a wealthy, athletic, and well-dressed member of [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) who operates as a merchant, diplomat, and negotiator. He travels by a fine two-cabin carriage and carries a stock of goods. He was paid by a Vistani at the encampment—described as "rich in wisdom"—to rescue the party from a forced duel in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}).

He maintains a functional relationship with [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) without being particularly loyal to him, having passed some of [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) tests and gained benefits as a result.

## Personality

Confident, worldly, and pragmatic. [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) is a man of strong beliefs—particularly regarding luck and misfortune—and speaks with the authority of someone who has seen much of the world. He is generous with information when it serves his interests but expects the same pragmatism from those he aids. He is visibly respected enough that even [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) was unwilling to confront him directly.

## Goals and Motivations

- Conduct trade, diplomacy, and negotiation throughout the land
- Maintain his standing with various powers, including [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and the forest masters
- Operate under his personal philosophy: wealth increases luck

## Connections

- [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) — his people

- [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) — functional but non-loyal relationship
- [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) — familiar with the forest masters' rules

## Key Events

### Session 17
Arrived by carriage as [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) was forcing the party into a duel with [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}). By offering to duel in the party's place, he caused [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) to abandon the confrontation entirely. Transported the party to the Vistani encampment in his carriage, sharing extensive knowledge about the forest's laws and Vistani customs along the way. Cursed [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) with vulnerability to cuts for approximately 2 nights in exchange for a magic item. Agreed, at [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) request, to speak to the forest masters about framing the party's rule-breaking as a misunderstanding. Advised the party to seek out **Tolmul** in [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) for a more complete map, and mentioned they should tell Tolmul that [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) sent them. Appears in [Session 17]({{< ref "/sessions/session-17" >}}).

### Session 18
Encouraged the party to celebrate at the Vistani campfire. Sold two magic items to the party: a scimitar that glows in the dark to [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), and a hammer that converts Divine Smite into ally healing to [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}). At the end of the evening, informed the party that [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) would be challenged to a duel the following morning, and that the duels would likely be fought in pairs across two bouts. Winning would make the party forest masters and clear their price on their heads. Appears in [Session 18]({{< ref "/sessions/session-18" >}}).

### Session 19
Called the party to combat for the Forest Master duels. When [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) appeared uninvited at the dueling ground, [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) noticed and remarked: *"That's not great."* After the party's victories, formally recognized [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) as a Forest Master. Appears in [Session 19]({{< ref "/sessions/session-19" >}}).
