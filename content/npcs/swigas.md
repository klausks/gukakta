## Overview

Swigas is a mysterious figure held captive at the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}). The party overheard a conversation in which Swigas was being compelled to search for something on behalf of an unknown lord. When the party called out to Swigas, they responded with an urgent exclamation: "THE BOOK!" before being silenced, presumably gagged or restrained.

## Personality

Unknown. The only response recorded from Swigas is the frantic exclamation about "the book," suggesting they possess knowledge of an important tome.

## Goals and Motivations

Swigas is being forced to search for something—likely a book—either by the mysterious voice giving commands or by whoever controls the portal. The full nature of this compulsion is unknown.

## Connections

- **Rahadin** — another voice heard in the conversation, possibly an authority figure or overseer
- [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}) — location of captivity
- **The Martikovs** — mentioned as having "given up again" in the conversation with Swigas

## Key Events

### Session 45

The party, exploring the basement of the winery, heard a magical conversation in which Swigas was being compelled: *"You failed with your lord, the Martikovs must have given up again. It's your turn Swigas, go, discover again."*

When the party called out to Swigas, a response came: *"THE BOOK!"* 

Swigas was then silenced, and a portal opened. The full circumstances of Swigas' fate remain unknown. Appears in [Session 45]({{< ref "/sessions/session-45" >}})
