## Overview

An unnamed woman who appeared in response to [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) use of the Forest Master's bell. She carries her own small bell and appears to serve as a conduit between those who ring the Forest Master's bell and a broader network of aid. When she rang her own bell during the encounter, [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) bell did not resonate in return, suggesting her bell contacts a different — and possibly greater — being.

## Personality

Composed and cooperative. She engaged calmly with [Pepino Falstaff's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) telepathic communication while the party was stranded in the Ethereal Plane, described the plane back to him to confirm she understood, and showed visible alarm at the appearance of the black horse wreathed in flames.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown. She appears to act as a messenger or intermediary rather than an independent agent.

## Connections

- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) — summoned her via the Forest Master's bell

## Key Events

### Session 25
Appeared near the mill in response to [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) bell. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) communicated with her telepathically from the Ethereal Plane, relaying the party's situation. She rang her own small bell — [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) bell did not resonate in reply. [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) challenged her to a duel; [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) warned him off. She visibly reacted with fear to the black horse with flaming parts. After the party returned to the normal plane, she confirmed she had called upon a greater being when she rang her bell, and attempted to cancel the request at [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) request — outcome unknown. Appears in [Session 25]({{< ref "/sessions/session-25" >}}).
