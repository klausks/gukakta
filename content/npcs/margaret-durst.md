## Overview

Margaret was the nurse who cared for [Walter Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}), the son of the Durst family. She manifested as a spirit within the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}), bound by her duty and anguish.

## Personality

Dedicated and sorrowful. Her primary concern was [Walter Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) music box, which was broken. She desperately wanted to hear it play one more time.

## Goals and Motivations

- Hear [Walter Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) music box play

## Connections

- [Walter Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) — the child she cared for

## Key Events

### Session 2
Encountered by the party during their exploration of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). Before her defeat, she revealed crucial information: [Walter Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) was the "last sacrifice" made by the Durst couple — their own son. Defeated by the party in [Session 2]({{< ref "/sessions/session-2" >}}).

### Session 3
The party discovered what appears to be Margaret's physical body — bloodied — locked inside a chest in the attic of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). A letter was found alongside the chest. See [Session 3]({{< ref "/sessions/session-3" >}}).
