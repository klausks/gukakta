## Overview

[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) is a member of [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) known to have the ability to cure curses. She resides at the Vistani encampment in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) and is described as a blue-eyed old woman worthy of trust. She paid [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) to rescue the party, suggesting she has been watching them from afar and considers their safe arrival at the encampment important. She also took in [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and foretold that his destiny is intertwined with the party's.

## Key Events

### Session 13
Mentioned by [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) as someone capable of curing curses. [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) suggested [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) could seek her out if he wished to be freed from his demonic curse. No further information provided. Appears in [Session 13]({{< ref "/sessions/session-13" >}}) (referenced only).

### Session 17
Revealed to reside at the Vistani encampment in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). She arranged and paid for the party's rescue by [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}), described by him as being "rich in wisdom." She has taken in [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and foretold that his destiny is intertwined with the party's. The party entered her cabin at the end of the session to learn more about their mission and future. Appears in [Session 17]({{< ref "/sessions/session-17" >}}).

### Session 18
Performed a five-card [Tarot reading]({{< ref "/lore/tarot-reading" >}}) for the party, laying out their likely path ahead. After the reading, she met privately with each party member; all fell asleep upon entering her tent and woke with no memory of the conversation, though each felt deeply at ease afterward. When [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) returned to express discomfort at his memory loss, she reassured him that when the time came to act, instinct would guide him. She also revealed that [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), like [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), possesses a bond with something greater than himself. Appears in [Session 18]({{< ref "/sessions/session-18" >}}).

### Session 22
Spoke to [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) twice through visions on the road. In the first, she warned him that his tongue will betray him, his luck can quickly turn to a curse, his superstition alone will not save him, and that two attempts on his life are already set in motion—predicting he will die in the worst way. In the second vision, she told him there is a way to reverse death through deception: *"Lie only to evil, do not betray the good."* She then raised the subject of someone named Mikaela. Appears in [Session 22]({{< ref "/sessions/session-22" >}}).
