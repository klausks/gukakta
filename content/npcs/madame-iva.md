## Overview

[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) is a member of [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) known to have the ability to cure curses. She resides at the Vistani encampment in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) and is described as a blue-eyed old woman worthy of trust. She paid [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) to rescue the party, suggesting she has been watching them from afar and considers their safe arrival at the encampment important. She also took in [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and foretold that his destiny is intertwined with the party's.
