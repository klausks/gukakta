## Overview

Bluto is a fisherman who lives near [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}), just north of the [Vistani camp near Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vistani-camp-vallaki" >}}). He had abducted the Vistani girl [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) and was keeping her locked inside a sack in his home. [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) described him as "crazy." Bluto was absent when the party entered his house.

## Personality

Described by Arabelle as unstable. His motives for abducting her are unknown.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown.

## Connections

- [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) — the Vistani girl he held captive

### Session 53
