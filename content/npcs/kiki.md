## Overview

Kiki is a trained crow given to the party by [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) as a personal link to the Martikov family's spy network. The party chose the name Kiki. Like all Martikov crows, she can be used to send messages and as a distress signal. Members of the Martikov family are able to see through their crows' eyes, but only by consciously focusing on a specific bird — they do not passively watch. The party can ask Kiki to step away when they require privacy.
