## Overview

Viktor Vallakovich is the 16-year-old son of [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}). He practices ancient occult magic of Fey origin in secret in the attic of the [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}) in Vallaki, using scrolls, enchantment circles, and dolls.

## Personality

Anxious and territorial. Viktor is protective of his private studies and immediately suspicious of strangers in the attic, assuming they were sent to capture his father or dispatched by the Wachter family. He can be reasoned with, however, and showed enough pragmatism to suggest the party hide until nightfall to leave undetected.

## Goals and Motivations

- Protect his secret magical research
- Keep his studies hidden from his father and from Vallaki's politics

## Connections

- [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) — father
- [Stella Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/stella-wachter" >}}) — former romantic involvement
- [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}) — where he lives and studies

## Key Events

### Session 35
Halik stated that Stella Wachter once courted Viktor and that the relationship ended badly. Fiona described Viktor as cruel and suggested he is initiated in magic. Appears in [Session 35]({{< ref "/sessions/session-35" >}}).

### Session 47
Confronted part of the party after they arrived through the magical portal in his attic. Demanded they not touch his things and suspected they were agents of the Wachter family or sent to capture the Baron. After the party convinced him they had arrived by accident through a portal searching for their companions, Viktor confirmed no one else had appeared and suggested they remain hidden until nightfall to leave quietly. Appears in [Session 47]({{< ref "/sessions/session-47" >}}).

### Session 48
Spoke at length with the full party during their debrief at the [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}). The party considers him a potential rational ally against [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and untainted by Vallakian politics, which he has no interest in. Acknowledged that his father, despite being insufferable, has managed to keep Strahd out of Vallaki. Refused to accept full responsibility for [Stella Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/stella-wachter" >}})'s mental breakdown. Expressed open hatred of [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and accused her of practicing necromancy and maintaining a cult in the basement of Wachterhaus. Helped the party leave the mansion without detection. Appears in [Session 48]({{< ref "/sessions/session-48" >}}).
