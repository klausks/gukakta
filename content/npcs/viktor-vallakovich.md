## Overview

Viktor Vallakovich is the 16-year-old son of [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}). He practices ancient occult magic of Fey origin in secret in the attic of the [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}) in Vallaki, using scrolls, enchantment circles, and dolls.

## Personality

Anxious and territorial. Viktor is protective of his private studies and immediately suspicious of strangers in the attic, assuming they were sent to capture his father or dispatched by the Wachter family. He can be reasoned with, however, and showed enough pragmatism to suggest the party hide until nightfall to leave undetected.

## Goals and Motivations

- Protect his secret magical research
- Keep his studies hidden from his father and from Vallaki's politics
