## Overview

The Abbot is the presiding figure at the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) in [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}). He is notable for apparently **not aging**. [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}) suspects he is a **fallen angel**. Crows — including those of the [Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) spy network — do not land at the abbey. Krezk's residents are unusually secretive about him when encountered outside their village.

Despite his apparent fall and corruption, [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) has heard rumors — from adventurers who visited the abbey years ago and were never seen again — that the Abbot may still be capable of good works. These reportedly include **resurrection** and the **restoration of lost limbs** for those he chooses to help.
