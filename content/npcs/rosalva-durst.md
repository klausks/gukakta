## Overview

Rosalva Durst, known as "Rose," is the spirit of a young girl bound to the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). The party first encountered her and her brother [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) standing outside the house, where they appeared to be living children. She informed the party that they were in Barovia and that the surrounding forest was the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}).

In Session 3, the party found the children's spirits in the attic of the house, revealing that they are ghosts.

## Personality

Frightened and sorrowful. She sought help from the party and trusted them enough to share how to reach the basement.

## Goals and Motivations

- Wants the monster in the basement dealt with.
- Hopes the party will return for them after the monster is slain.

## Connections

- [Thornboldt Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) — her brother
- [Elizabeth Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/elizabeth-durst" >}}) — appears linked to her (name on mirror)

## Key Events 

### Session 1
Provided the party with their initial orientation to Barovia and the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}).

### Session 2
[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) discovered a broken mirror in the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) with her name written behind it — crossed out, with [Elizabeth Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/elizabeth-durst" >}}) name not crossed out nearby.

### Session 3
Encountered in the attic of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) as a spirit, along with her brother [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}). The party promised to deal with the monster in the basement and return to support the children. She revealed how to access the basement — through a staircase from the attic. The party's dog, Lancelot, was left with the spirits for company. Appears in [Session 3]({{< ref "/sessions/session-3" >}}).
