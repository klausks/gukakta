## Overview

Rosalva Durst is a young girl who, along with her brother [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}), was found standing outside the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) when the party arrived. She informed the party that they were in Barovia and that the forest they had passed through was the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}).

## Personality

Appears frightened. Sought help from the party rather than retreating.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown; scared of the house and its contents.

## Connections

- [Thornboldt Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) — her brother
- [Elizabeth Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/elizabeth-durst" >}}) — appears linked to her (name on mirror)
