## Overview

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/npcs/igga-bevneros" >}}) is a lyre-playing individual—possibly a drow—encountered at the Vistani encampment in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). Notably, he is not a Vistani himself but has been taken in by [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}). According to [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}), his destiny is intertwined with the party's.

## Personality

Eager and enthusiastic. Upon meeting the party, [Igga]({{< ref "/npcs/igga-bevneros" >}}) was visibly excited, greeting them with energy and announcing the connection between their fates without hesitation.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown; follows his destiny as foretold by [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}).

## Connections

- [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) — his benefactor and the one who revealed his connection to the party's destiny
- The Party: [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}})

## Key Events

### Session 17
The party met [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/npcs/igga-bevneros" >}}) upon arriving at the Vistani encampment. He greeted them enthusiastically, carrying his lyre, and told them that [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) had informed him their destinies are connected. Shortly after this introduction, the entire group entered [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) cabin together. Appears in [Session 17]({{< ref "/sessions/session-17" >}}).
