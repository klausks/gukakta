## Overview

Johma is a fire spirit who served as the mentor of [Yakov]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}) and acts as an intermediary for [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}). He is able to inhabit and emerge from trees, and can carry magical objects between realms. He was first observed at the Forest Master duel wearing fine red armor.
