## Overview

Donavich is the priest of the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), serving as the spiritual leader and performing religious duties for the community. He assists with funeral rites and burial ceremonies, as well as providing spiritual guidance in a cursed land. He carries a profound tragedy: his own son has become undead, a fate he struggles to reconcile with his faith.

## Personality

Compassionate and dutiful, yet haunted by personal tragedy. Despite the darkness that surrounds [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), Donavich performs his religious duties with dedication. His grief over his undead son manifests in physical signs—blood on his cloth, a weariness that betrays his internal struggle.

## Goals and Motivations

- Provide spiritual comfort to the people of [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})
- Perform proper burial rites for the deceased
- Maintain faith and hope in a cursed land
- Reconcile the fate of his undead son with his faith
