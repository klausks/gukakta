## Overview

Donavich is the priest of the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), serving as the spiritual leader and performing religious duties for the community. He assists with funeral rites and burial ceremonies, as well as providing spiritual guidance in a cursed land.

## Personality

Compassionate and dutiful. Despite the darkness that surrounds [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), Donavich performs his religious duties with dedication.

## Goals and Motivations

- Provide spiritual comfort to the people of [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})
- Perform proper burial rites for the deceased
- Maintain faith and hope in a cursed land

## Connections

- [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) — his congregation
- [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) — assisting with funeral arrangements for [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) father
