## Overview

Koselina is the youngest of the mill sisters. She appeared friendly when the party arrived at the mill — warm, wearing a flour-dusted apron and welcoming the party inside. She identified her sisters as **Bela** and **Morgantha**. She claimed the pies the sisters make are prepared with wolf meat.

## Personality

Initially appeared welcoming and guileless. Whether this was genuine or a facade is unknown.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown, though she is part of the mill operation which is connected to capturing people and harvesting body parts.

## Connections

- [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) — older sister
- [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) — sister (named directly by Koselina)
