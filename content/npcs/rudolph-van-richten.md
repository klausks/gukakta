## Overview

Rudolph van Richten is a monster-hunter referenced by the party in connection with a book about monsters and creatures that contains an attached note. The foreword of the [Monster Hunter's Handbook]({{< ref "/lore/monster-hunters-handbook" >}}) also identifies him as the author of that field guide.

## Personality

His writing presents him as grim, practical, and deeply distrustful of monsters and anyone he believes serves them.

## Goals and Motivations

To hunt monsters before they can prey on innocents.

## Connections

- [Androm]({{< ref "/npcs/androm" >}}) — the book is noted while reviewing stock at his shop

## Key Events

### Session 38
A book about monsters and creatures connected to Rudolph van Richten is noted for containing a note. Appears in [Session 38]({{< ref "/sessions/session-38" >}}).

### Session 52
[Tolmul]({{< ref "/npcs/tolmul" >}}), a Vistani merchant, reveals he recently sold a unique map to an unknown adventurer and speculates it may have been made by or once belonged to Van Richten. The map's current location is unknown. Referenced in [Session 52]({{< ref "/sessions/session-52" >}}).
