## Overview

Udo Rikalova is the son of [Willemina Rikalova]({{< ref "/npcs/willemina-rikalova" >}}). He recently underwent a dramatic behavioral change: he stopped attending church and began leaving his home at night wearing dark cloaks. He appears to have developed some connection to the [Wachter family]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}). He was arrested by the Baron's guards after being found with a sign that read the Burgomaster should be thrown to the wolves.
