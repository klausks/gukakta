## Overview

Tolmul is a Vistani merchant who operates out of the camp near Vallaki. [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) first pointed the party toward him as a source for a better map; [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) later confirmed he would arrive with rare goods and an item intended for [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}). When the party met him, he proved a reliable trader — delivering on his promises, dealing in equipment, and pointing them toward camp leadership for further needs.

## Personality

Efficient and businesslike. Tolmul honoured prior commitments without hesitation and dealt with the party in a straightforward manner.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown beyond maintaining his trade and operating within the Vistani camp.

## Connections

- [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) — recommended Tolmul as a contact for a better map
- [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) — claimed Tolmul will arrive near Vallaki soon
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) — delivered a promised item; also traded armor with him
- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) — sold him a retractable armblade as a prosthetic combat solution
- [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}) — the camp leader; Tolmul directed Jimmy to him regarding hand restoration
- [Rudolph van Richten]({{< ref "/npcs/rudolph-van-richten" >}}) — recently sold a unique map that may have belonged to Van Richten

## Key Events

### Session 32
Vasili told the party Tolmul would arrive near Vallaki soon with rare goods, including an item intended for Monel. Appears in [Session 32]({{< ref "/sessions/session-32" >}}).

### Session 52
Met the party at his camp near Vallaki. Delivered the item promised to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), traded armor with him, and sold [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) a retractable armblade to serve as a combat replacement for his severed right hand. Revealed he had recently sold a unique map — possibly once belonging to [Rudolph van Richten]({{< ref "/npcs/rudolph-van-richten" >}}) — to an unknown adventurer. Directed Jimmy and Monel to [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}), the camp leader, for information on restoring Jimmy's hand. Appears in [Session 52]({{< ref "/sessions/session-52" >}}).
