## Key Events

### Session 35
Received the party at Wachterhaus and offered to store their belongings. During a fight with an [Invisible Fiend]({{< ref "/creatures/invisible-fiend" >}}), Halik struck the creature with a distinct dagger. Appears in [Session 35]({{< ref "/sessions/session-35" >}}).

### Session 36
Was present as the party dealt with the captured fiend at Wachterhaus. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) suspected the creature served Halik; the fiend stared at him and repeatedly insisted, “I serve the Devil.” Appears in [Session 36]({{< ref "/sessions/session-36" >}}).

### Session 38
Receives Igga and Agares at Wachterhaus, arranges for Agares’ horse to be taken to a transport stable, and hosts them with cocktails while Fiona is resting or praying. Offers to store Agares’ belongings.

Shows guest rooms (including a room said to belong to Nikolai Wachter) and introduces household staff. Escorts the party to the area where the captured fiend is kept, and later carries Stella away when she becomes agitated. Appears in [Session 38]({{< ref "/sessions/session-38" >}}).
