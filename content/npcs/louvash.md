## Overview

Louvash is a Vistani at the camp near Vallaki and the brother of [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}), the camp leader. His daughter [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) went missing near [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}), prompting the party to search for her.

## Personality

Unknown beyond his distress over his missing daughter.

Unknown beyond the safety of his daughter.
