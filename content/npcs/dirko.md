## Overview

Dirko is a person of unrevealed background who serves as a "secret" partner or associate of [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), the Burgomaster's son. His exact role and motivations remain largely unknown to the party.

## Personality

Unknown; minimal direct interaction with the party.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown; appears to be aligned with [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) interests and the party's mission to escort [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to safety.

## Connections

- [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) — his associate/partner
- [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) — connected through [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}})
- The Party: [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}})

## Key Events

### Session 10
Met by the party as [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) "secret" partner during preparations to leave [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). Was present with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) after the encounter with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) to assess damages. Appears in [Session 10]({{< ref "/sessions/session-10" >}}).

### Session 11
At the inn in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), had a private conversation with [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) before departing to scout the perimeter of the village. [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) explained that [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) had volunteered to conduct reconnaissance around the village, mapping potential threats and escape routes. The party agreed to delay departure until morning to allow [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) time to complete his survey. Appears in [Session 11]({{< ref "/sessions/session-11" >}}).

### Session 12
Returned from his perimeter reconnaissance during or shortly after the undead assault on the inn. **[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) was noticeably wounded** but could not identify what attacked him. Consumed a healing potion to stabilize his condition and generously offered additional healing potions to both [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) for their injuries. The nature of his wound and the identity of his attacker remain mysterious. Appears in [Session 12]({{< ref "/sessions/session-12" >}}).

### Session 13
[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) revealed his true allegiance and made a **shocking proposal to [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}})**. He offered to free [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) from his demonic curse—a burden that has tormented him since childhood—in exchange for [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) becoming **[Count Strahd von Zarovich's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) informant** within the party. The proposal left [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) in shock, and he neither accepted nor refused. [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) is now confirmed as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) agent/pawn by the party. His true role serving [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) rather than [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) fundamentally changes the party's assessment of their situation. Appears in [Session 13]({{< ref "/sessions/session-13" >}}).

### Session 14
During the party's journey through [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) administered a healing potion to the critically wounded [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) after an ambush. The potion's administration caused [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) condition to worsen significantly rather than improve. The party identified that the potion had been provided by [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) and concluded that the potion was deliberately tainted or corrupted. This incident definitively proves [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) active sabotage of the party's mission and his commitment to [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) agenda. The party now distrusts any provisions, potions, or supplies that originated from [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}). Appears in [Session 14]({{< ref "/sessions/session-14" >}}).

### Session 16
[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) was encountered deep within [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) alongside [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}). When the party expressed difficulty traversing the forest, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) claimed that he experienced no difficulty whatsoever and asserted that he knows everything about crossing [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). He revealed knowledge of [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}), describing him as strict in enforcing the forest's rules and laws. [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) also suggested that it would be beneficial if someone were to challenge [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}). His presence and effortless navigation of the forest raise questions about his true connections and loyalties within this cursed land. Appears in [Session 16]({{< ref "/sessions/session-16" >}}).
