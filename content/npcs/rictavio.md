## Overview

Rictavio is a traveling bard performing at Vallaki's Blue Water Inn. He claimed to be the party's neighbor and warned them they were being talked about in town.

He also told the party that Urwin wanted to speak with them and that Urwin had left them a paid round. Later, he sang a song about the "mad mage."

## Personality

Friendly and talkative.
