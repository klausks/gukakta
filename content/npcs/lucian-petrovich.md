## Overview

Father Lucian Petrovich is a priest in Vallaki associated with St. Andral’s Church. He shared information with Ireena about a hidden crypt beneath the church’s altar and an upcoming feast in St. Andral’s honor.

## Personality

Calm and confident (as reported).

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown.

## Connections

- [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) — spoke with her privately
- [Lydia Petrovna]({{< ref "/npcs/lydia-petrovna" >}}) — his sister

## Key Events

### Session 37
Told Ireena that St. Andral’s crypt lies beneath the altar but is not visible, and that a feast for St. Andral will occur the same day as the Baron’s public event. Appears in [Session 37]({{< ref "/sessions/session-37" >}}).

### Session 38
Spoke with Jimmy at St. Andral’s Church about the demon situation. Jimmy learned Lucian can provide holy rites and services including making holy water and performing spells such as *Gentle Repose*, *Iron Vision*, and *Purify Water*. Appears in [Session 38]({{< ref "/sessions/session-38" >}}).

### Session 39
Jimmy undertook a private test connected to the hidden crypt beneath St. Andral’s Church, involving a crystal, a phrase of release, and prayer. Appears in [Session 39]({{< ref "/sessions/session-39" >}}).
### Session 49
Confirmed to Monel and Agares that [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) had been sheltering at [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}) and spends her days praying in the crypt of Saint Andral. Was present when Ireena described her visions of enormous flaming arms, and noted that [Izek]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) is known to project fire from his demonic arm. Reminded the party about the Saint Andral feast and mentioned that his sister, [Lydia Petrovna]({{< ref "/npcs/lydia-petrovna" >}}), is married to the Baron, which facilitates holding the feast on the same day as Vallaki's festival. Appears in [Session 49]({{< ref "/sessions/session-49" >}}).

### Session 50
Met privately with [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) inside the church while the rest of the party waited outside. Also shared an additional detail about Ireena's visions: the creature has **red-haired dolls hanging from it**, and mentioned that a man in Vallaki named [Blinksy]({{< ref "/npcs/blinksy" >}}) makes cloth dolls. Appears in [Session 50]({{< ref "/sessions/session-50" >}}).

### Session 51
Performed the first stage of an exorcism on [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) in the crypt of St. Andral's Church using the Iron Vigil rite, with the saint's mortal remains nearby. Threw holy water on Jimmy to drive the demon from his head into his right hand, then advised Jimmy to sever the hand to break the bond. Warned that the demon was stronger than anticipated and would not leave without causing one final pain. When Jimmy cut his hand off, the demon partially manifested as black smoke and escaped, breaking the crypt gate from its axis as it fled. Father Lucian noted that Jimmy's body and soul had survived better than expected, and instructed Jimmy to continue the Iron Vigil for the coming days to clear residual corruption.

Afterward, he told Jimmy directly that he was forged to fight evil actively — not passively — and urged him to begin by hunting the escaped demon. He invited Jimmy to the [Saint Andral feast]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}). He also mentioned, unprompted, that [The Abbot]({{< ref "/npcs/the-abbot" >}}) of the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) — though fallen — is rumored to still be capable of good works, including resurrection and **restoration of lost limbs** for those he favors. He heard this from adventurers who had visited the abbey years ago; he has not seen them since. Appears in [Session 51]({{< ref "/sessions/session-51" >}}).
