## Overview

Arabelle is the young daughter of [Louvash]({{< ref "/npcs/louvash" >}}) and niece of [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}). She went missing near [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}) and was found locked inside a sack at the house of a fisherman named [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}}). She was recovered by [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and returned to her father.
