## Overview

Irmno is a Vistani from the camp near Vallaki. He was assigned to accompany [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) when they tracked [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) to [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}). He proved unhelpful and uncollaborative throughout.

## Personality

Reluctant and uncooperative. Provided minimal assistance during the search, though he did identify the light on the lake as likely belonging to the fisherman Bluto.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown.

## Connections

- [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) — the missing girl he was sent to help find
- [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}}) — recognized his light on the lake

## Key Events

### Session 53
Accompanied Jimmy and Monel on the trail toward Lake Zarovich. Identified the light seen on the lake as possibly belonging to Bluto, the fisherman. Contributed little else to the search. Appears in [Session 53]({{< ref "/sessions/session-53" >}}).
