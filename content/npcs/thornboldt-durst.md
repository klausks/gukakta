## Overview

Thornboldt Durst is a young boy who, along with his sister [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}), was found standing outside the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) when the party arrived. He and his sister explained that they were in Barovia, that the surrounding forest was the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}), and that they feared entering their home due to a monster their parents kept imprisoned in the basement.

## Personality

Appears frightened. Sought help from the party alongside his sister.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown; scared of the house and its contents.

## Connections

- [Rosalva Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) — his sister
