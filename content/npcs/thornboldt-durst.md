## Overview

Thornboldt Durst, known as "Thorn," is the spirit of a young boy bound to the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). The party first encountered him and his sister [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) standing outside the house, where they appeared to be living children. He explained that they were in Barovia and that they feared the monster their parents kept in the basement.

In Session 3, the party found his spirit in the attic, confirming he is a ghost. During this encounter, he made a demand of [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) — to take his hand — which Jimmy refused.

## Personality

Frightened and at times demanding. He trusted the party with the secret of the basement access but was not above using his influence to make demands.

## Goals and Motivations

- Wants the monster in the basement dealt with.
- Hopes the party will return for them after the monster is slain.

## Connections

- [Rosalva Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) — his sister
