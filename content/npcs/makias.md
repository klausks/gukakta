## Overview

Makias is a young, thin human encountered waiting outside a closed iron gate in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). He wore a Vistani-style hat and carried an unlit torch. He was anxious and unwilling to share information, but revealed he had been unable to pass through the gate and was waiting for the "old pie woman" to come by, believing the gate would open when she did. He also mentioned having friends who are magicians. He has since appeared in the Ethereal Plane while the party was stranded there, seemingly drawn to the mill by [Morgantha's]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) pies — entirely unaware of the party's presence in that plane.

## Personality

Nervous, anxious, and deliberately unhelpful when first encountered. He showed a provocative side when singling out [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) with a racial comment and was evasive with all questions. He also demonstrated an impulsive aggression when he challenged a stranger to a duel without provocation, only backing down after a direct threat to his life.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown. He was trying to pass through the iron gate but what lay on the other side—and why—is unclear.

## Connections

- His unnamed magician friends (unknown identity)
- [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) — the "old pie woman" he was waiting for at the gate

## Key Events

### Session 22
Encountered alone outside a closed iron gate in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). Claimed not to want another duel, could not pass through the gate, and was waiting for the "old pie woman" to open it. When the party used magic to retrieve the gate key, he remarked *"You're magicians, just like my friends."* Directed a provocation at [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}), asked [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) for her name (she gave a false one), then passed through the gate and disappeared into the forest. Appears in [Session 22]({{< ref "/sessions/session-22" >}}).

### Session 25
Was drawn to the mill solely to eat [Morgantha's]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) pies. Only [Pepino Falstaff's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) telepathic connection could reach him. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) recognized him as the man from the iron gate, noting his improved appearance. Upon noticing a female messenger arrive for [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), Makias challenged her to a duel without provocation. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) telepathically threatened him with death if he followed through; Makias backed down and left. Appears in [Session 25]({{< ref "/sessions/session-25" >}}).
