## Overview

Makias is a young, thin human encountered waiting outside a closed iron gate in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). He wore a Vistani-style hat and carried an unlit torch. He was anxious and unwilling to share information, but revealed he had been unable to pass through the gate and was waiting for the "old pie woman" to come by, believing the gate would open when she did. He also mentioned having friends who are magicians.

## Personality

Nervous, anxious, and deliberately unhelpful. He showed a provocative side when singling out [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) with a racial comment and was evasive with all questions.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown. He was trying to pass through the iron gate but what lay on the other side—and why—is unclear.

## Connections

- His unnamed magician friends (unknown identity)
