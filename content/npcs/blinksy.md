## Overview

Blinksy is an eccentric doll and toy maker in Vallaki who keeps a shop that stays open later than most establishments. He has a monkey companion named **Piccolo**. His wares include dolls depicting various creatures: vampires, skeletons, ghouls, and more.

Some customers have reported that Blinksy's dolls became protective **amulets** after purchase, though Blinksy himself cannot explain this. More troublingly, he mentioned that some of his dolls have **come to life** and escaped the shop on their own.

He shared the information that [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) killed all the women and children of the twilight elves, describing it as a great tragedy.

## Personality

Eccentric and somewhat oblivious to the darker implications of his own work. Speaks openly and without apparent guile.
