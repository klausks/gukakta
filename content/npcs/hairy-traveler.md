## Overview

A very hairy human traveler was spotted near the road to the Wizard of Wines Winery, involved in an incident with a “giant rat” carrying a beautiful scimitar. The traveler chased it into a grotto and was later found downed and bleeding.

In a later development, the giant rat was seen transforming into a humanoid, suggesting the encounter may have been staged by two hairy, hunched humans working together.
