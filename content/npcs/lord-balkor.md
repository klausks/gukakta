## Overview

Lord Balkor is a mysterious and powerful entity who appears to exercise control over [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) and its laws. Little is known about him beyond his reputation as a strict enforcer of the forest's rules. He remains an enigmatic figure, with his true nature and origins shrouded in mystery.

## Personality

Unknown; no direct interaction with the party has occurred.

## Goals and Motivations

- Maintain strict control over the laws and rules of [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}})
- Enforce these laws with apparent rigor

## Connections
