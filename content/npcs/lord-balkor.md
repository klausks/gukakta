## Overview

Lord Balkor is one of the **forest masters**—mysterious and powerful entities who govern [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) and enforce its strict laws. He is one of several such masters, and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) formerly acted as his representative. He resides in a dimension accessible through a magical painting and communicates with forest masters through intermediaries such as [Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}). He appears as a bearded Vistani in dark robes, seated on a wooden throne in a chamber with two doors.

## Personality

Calm, magnanimous, and knowledgeable. He speaks with authority but without hostility, and appears genuinely willing to aid those who earn the Forest Master title. He possesses knowledge of powerful dark forces and their servants.

## Goals and Motivations

- Maintain control over the laws and rules of [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}})
- Recognize and empower worthy Forest Masters
- Guide Forest Masters in understanding their role and the world's greater forces

## Connections

- [Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}) — fire spirit intermediary; delivers messages and brings individuals into the painting
- [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) — formerly enforced [Lord Balkor's]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) demands on the party's behalf
- [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) — his former designated representative and dueling champion (deceased)
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) — recognized Forest Master

## Key Events

### Session 16
[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) first revealed the existence of [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) when the party mentioned their difficulties traversing [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) stated that [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) is rigorous with the forest's rules and laws. Appears in [Session 16]({{< ref "/sessions/session-16" >}}).

### Session 17
[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) invoked [Lord Balkor's]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) authority to force a duel upon the party, sending [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) as his champion. The terms were that a party loss would require surrendering [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}). The duel was averted by [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}), but the party still carries a price on their heads for breaking the forest's rules. Appears in [Session 17]({{< ref "/sessions/session-17" >}}).

### Session 20
[Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}) emerged from a tree carrying a magical painting and summoned [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to enter it. Inside, [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) formally granted [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) the full privileges of the Forest Master title: the party's debts were cleared, they would no longer be troubled by the mists, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) can hide inside any tree by striking it three times, and can summon forest informants by ringing a small golden bell (20–100gp tip). He also revealed that [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) was likely a failed attempt by a war entity to acquire a human servant, and promised that [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) would receive an item through an informant to make contact with this entity. Appears in [Session 20]({{< ref "/sessions/session-20" >}}).
