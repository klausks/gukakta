## Overview

[Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) is a member of [The Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}})—a race with innate supernatural abilities. [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) operates as a craftsperson in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), possessing tools and equipment useful for armor repairs and similar work. His exact dwelling and deeper background remain largely unknown to the party.

## Personality

Cryptic and observant. [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) speaks in measured, meaningful tones, making observations about others that suggest deeper supernatural awareness. He appears knowledgeable about matters beyond ordinary perception and maintains an enigmatic demeanor.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown; appears pragmatic in business dealings. Possesses knowledge about curses and their remedies.

## Key Events

### Session 13
[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) sought [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) to acquire armor repair tools and materials for [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})). During their encounter, [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) made several cryptic and revealing observations:

- Commented that [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) eyes have "seen much"—suggesting supernatural perception or a sight beyond the normal
- Revealed that all members of the [Vistanis]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) race possess inherent supernatural powers
- Expressed regret that [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) would only experience the "blessing powers" of the [Vistanis]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) peoples and not their curses
- **Critical Information:** Mentioned **[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}})** can cure curses—a potential solution to [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demonic possession

Provided the requested tools without apparent hesitation. Appears in [Session 13]({{< ref "/sessions/session-13" >}}).
