## Overview

An unknown druid is stationed at the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}). The party encountered this druid and incapacitated them before they could be fully interrogated. The druid possesses a staff of great magical power that extends far beyond their personal magic—possibly connected to a corrupted forest entity or ancient tree.

## Abilities & Attributes

The druid carries a **staff with a powerful magical bond** that appears to be tied to something greater than the druid themselves. This bond suggests the staff may be an artifact or connected to a higher force.

Before being subdued, the druid was heard chanting:

> "Oh great tree, resurrect... Come to us again..."

This indicates the druid is attempting to resurrect or summon a corrupted tree or forest entity, likely related to the blights plaguing the winery.

## Personality

Unknown. The druid was overtaken before meaningful interaction could occur.

## Goals and Motivations

- To resurrect or summon a great tree entity
- To maintain a conjuration effect that the party detected within the winery
- To support the forces hostile to the Martikovs
