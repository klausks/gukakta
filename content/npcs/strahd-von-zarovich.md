## Overview

[Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) is a vampire of ancient power and mysterious origins. He is the ruler of [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), a cursed land trapped in perpetual darkness and despair. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) has taken a personal and obsessive interest in [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), the sister of [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), creating a direct threat to her safety and liberty.

## History

Unknown.

## Motivations

- Maintain dominion over [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})
- Pursue [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) for unknown personal reasons
- Preserve his reputation with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}})
- Assert dominance and demand respect from those who would oppose him

## Personality

Powerful, commanding, and utterly committed to his will. His interest in [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) appears to be personal and obsessive, suggesting a depth of emotion beneath his undead nature. He is capable of pride, vanity, and the need for recognition. He respects bold action and will engage with those who prove worthy—though his mercy is cruel and dangerous.

## Abilities

- **Shapeshifting:** Capable of transforming into wolf form
- **Enchantment Magic:** Can enchant individuals to render them incapacitated
- **Unarmed Combat:** Possesses devastating physical strength; critical strikes deal extraordinary damage (1d8 + 4 + 4d6)
- **Legendary Actions:** Can resist magical effects as a reaction
- **Omniscience in Domain:** Maintains "eyes and ears" throughout [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})

## Limitations

- Will not enter a dwelling without an explicit invitation
- Values his reputation with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) and will not do anything to compromise it
- Operates within certain boundaries of social propriety

## Key Events

### Session 8
[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) spoke to the party of [Count Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) rule over [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) and his vampire nature (confirmed through rumor and apparent fact). Revealed his obsessive personal interest in [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), making her the unwilling target of the vampire's desire. Appears in [Session 8]({{< ref "/sessions/session-8" >}}).

### Session 10 - The Direct Encounter
[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) appeared directly to the party as they prepared to depart [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}). Manifested first in wolf form before transforming to humanoid shape. Demonstrated the following:

**Revelations:**
- Possesses omniscient awareness of all happenings in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) through his "eyes and ears"
- Expressed indifference to the party's escape with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), provided they do not damage his reputation with her
- Operates within boundaries of propriety; will not enter a dwelling without explicit invitation

**Combat Against [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}):**
- Enchanted [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) to prevent his intervention
- Issued a challenge to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) regarding respect
- Lured [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) into an abandoned house
- Demonstrated devastating unarmed combat proficiency with critical strikes dealing 1d8 + 4 + 4d6 damage, nearly killing [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) instantly
- Shifted focus to [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) when the party intervened, attacking with unarmed strikes
- Used legendary maneuvers to resist magical attacks
- Withdrew and departed when confronted with coordinated party opposition

**Tactical Lessons:**
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) will abandon a confrontation rather than escalate to lethal levels, suggesting constraints on his actions or willingness to engage the full party
- He respects bold action and decisive confrontation

Appears in [Session 10]({{< ref "/sessions/session-10" >}}).

### Session 18 (referenced)
[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) revealed to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) that [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), like [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) himself, possesses **a bond with something greater than himself**—a power beyond the mortal realm. The full nature of this bond is unknown. Additionally, the fifth card of the [Tarot reading]({{< ref "/lore/tarot-reading" >}}) revealed that the final confrontation with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) will take place at [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}) after one or two prior visits. Referenced in [Session 18]({{< ref "/sessions/session-18" >}}).

### Session 21
Left a magnificent carriage at the fork between the road to [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}) and the road to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), with two large black horses and an open door. Inside was a handwritten letter on high-quality paper addressed to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), and [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}): *"Come home."* The reverse carried a personal message to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), taunting him about the item promised by [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) and declaring that [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) awaits him. The party chose to ignore the invitation. Appears in [Session 21]({{< ref "/sessions/session-21" >}}).

### Session 22
Appeared briefly as an apparition at the top of a closed iron gate in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) when [Pepino Falstaff's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) mage hand reached for the gate key. He was crouched at the top of the gate, holding the key, and kicked it down to the party before asking [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) why he had not simply climbed the gate. He then vanished instantly. Appears in [Session 22]({{< ref "/sessions/session-22" >}}).

### Session 26
Arrived at the party's campsite atop a horse wreathed in flames — identified by [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) as an illusion. Stepped from invisibility and addressed [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) directly, expressing contempt for bards as people who write about life rather than live it. Gave [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) a single chance to save [Pepino Falstaff's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) life through performance — two attempts were made (one praising [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) courage, one invoking [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}})) and neither satisfied him. Declared his governing rule: **a blow for a blow**. After [Igga's]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) unarmed strikes (a punch and headbutt), [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) retaliated and left [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) near death. When [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) cursed him and struck in turn, [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) laid [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) unconscious and departed, declaring he had honored the rules. Appears in [Session 26]({{< ref "/sessions/session-26" >}}).
