## Overview

[Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) is a vampire of ancient power and mysterious origins. He is the ruler of [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), a cursed land trapped in perpetual darkness and despair. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) has taken a personal and obsessive interest in [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), the sister of [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), creating a direct threat to her safety and liberty.

## History

Unknown.

## Motivations

- Maintain dominion over [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})
- Pursue [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) for unknown personal reasons
- Preserve his reputation with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}})
- Assert dominance and demand respect from those who would oppose him

## Personality

Powerful, commanding, and utterly committed to his will. His interest in [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) appears to be personal and obsessive, suggesting a depth of emotion beneath his undead nature. He is capable of pride, vanity, and the need for recognition. He respects bold action and will engage with those who prove worthy—though his mercy is cruel and dangerous.

## Abilities

- **Shapeshifting:** Capable of transforming into wolf form
- **Enchantment Magic:** Can enchant individuals to render them incapacitated
- **Unarmed Combat:** Possesses devastating physical strength; critical strikes deal extraordinary damage (1d8 + 4 + 4d6)
- **Legendary Actions:** Can resist magical effects as a reaction
- **Omniscience in Domain:** Maintains "eyes and ears" throughout [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})

## Limitations

- Will not enter a dwelling without an explicit invitation
- Values his reputation with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) and will not do anything to compromise it
- Operates within certain boundaries of social propriety

Revealed his obsessive personal interest in [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), making her the unwilling target of the vampire's desire. ### Session 10 - The Direct Encounter
[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) appeared directly to the party as they prepared to depart [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}). Manifested first in wolf form before transforming to humanoid shape. Demonstrated the following:

**Revelations:**
- Possesses omniscient awareness of all happenings in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) through his "eyes and ears"
- Expressed indifference to the party's escape with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), provided they do not damage his reputation with her
- Operates within boundaries of propriety; will not enter a dwelling without explicit invitation

**Combat Against [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}):**
- Enchanted [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) to prevent his intervention
- Issued a challenge to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) regarding respect
- Lured [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) into an abandoned house
- Demonstrated devastating unarmed combat proficiency with critical strikes dealing 1d8 + 4 + 4d6 damage, nearly killing [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) instantly
- Shifted focus to [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) when the party intervened, attacking with unarmed strikes
- Used legendary maneuvers to resist magical attacks
- Withdrew and departed when confronted with coordinated party opposition

**Tactical Lessons:**
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) will abandon a confrontation rather than escalate to lethal levels, suggesting constraints on his actions or willingness to engage the full party
- He respects bold action and decisive confrontation
