## Overview

Arrigal leads the [Vistani camp near Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vistani-camp-vallaki" >}}) and is the brother of [Louvash]({{< ref "/npcs/louvash" >}}). He initially received the party with coldness due to the crisis involving his missing niece [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}), but warmed considerably after the party recovered her. He proved to be a knowledgeable and candid source of intelligence about Vallaki, Krezk, and regional politics.

## Personality

Sour and guarded when under stress, but capable of warmth and generosity when treated fairly. Candid about local politics and willing to share intelligence with those who have earned his trust.

## Goals and Motivations

The safety and wellbeing of his camp and family.

## Connections

- [Tolmul]({{< ref "/npcs/tolmul" >}}) — Vistani merchant in the same camp
- [Louvash]({{< ref "/npcs/louvash" >}}) — brother
- [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) — niece; recovered by the party in Session 53

## Key Events

### Session 52
Mentioned by Tolmul as the camp leader and a potential source of information for restoring Jimmy's severed right hand. Not yet met by the party. Referenced in [Session 52]({{< ref "/sessions/session-52" >}}).

### Session 53
Met [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) with visible sourness — his niece [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) had been missing for a day and a half near [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}). After the party recovered her from the fisherman [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}})'s house, Arrigal rewarded them with a detailed map and invited them to the camp's feast. Before the party departed, Arrigal answered their questions: he confirmed the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) in [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}) as the best path for restoring Jimmy's hand; described Baron Vallakovich as jealous and unstable and said no one in Vallaki is truly happy; stated that Krezk's burgomaster is saner and that the two towns trade covertly, primarily in wine; and noted that the Vistani know the Wachter family but maintain no relationship with them. Appears in [Session 53]({{< ref "/sessions/session-53" >}}).
