## Overview

Milivoj is a young man (roughly 16–18), simple and disgruntled, working as the gravekeeper at [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}) in Vallaki. He is a terrible liar.

## Personality

Bitter and wary.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown.

## Connections

- [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}) — Milivoj pointed him out in the church
- [Henrich Van der Hood]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}) — paid Milivoj to steal St. Andral's bones and bury 5 coffins

## Key Events

### Session 31
Met the party in the cemetery while digging a grave. Muttered that people call him "problematic" because he will not smile while the city collapses, and expressed fear of ending up in the Baron's hands (or worse, Izek — whom he called "Isak"). Appears in [Session 31]({{< ref "/sessions/session-31" >}}).

### Session 52
Confronted by [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) in the churchyard after the theft of St. Andral's bones was reported. Despite attempts to deny involvement, he quickly confessed: he accepted 10 gold pieces from [Henrich Van der Hood]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}) — 5 gp to steal the skull of St. Andral from the crypt and deliver it to Henrich, and 5 gp to bury 5 coffins that would be delivered to him that night at 10 PM. Appears in [Session 52]({{< ref "/sessions/session-52" >}}).
