## Overview

Offalia is one of the mill sisters. She was encountered by [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) on the second floor of the mill during combat, but quickly retreated to the third floor.

The tortured man in the cave identified his captors by initials — one starting with **"B"** and another with **"O"** — suggesting Offalia and [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) are responsible for his long imprisonment and the kidnapping of his daughters.

**Note:** [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) referred to her sisters as Bela and **Morgantha**. It is unclear whether Offalia and Morgantha are the same person or two different individuals.
