## Overview

Walpole is a person named in Ismark's letter. The letter's message only appeared when Ireena placed her hand on a handprint within it.

## Personality

Unknown.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown.

## Connections

- [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) — the letter's magic responded to her hand
- [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) — the letter's author

## Key Events

### Session 29
The revealed message instructed the party to present Ireena to Walpole. Ireena recognized the name and believed Walpole might be trustworthy. Appears in [Session 29]({{< ref "/sessions/session-29" >}}).

### Session 31
Found at St. Andral's Church in Vallaki. Recognized Ireena and promised a reward for escorting her safely to Vallaki, then led the party to his home to discuss terms. Appears in [Session 31]({{< ref "/sessions/session-31" >}}).

### Session 32
Paid the party for escorting Ireena safely to Vallaki (100 gp each), with [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) receiving a smaller share (50 gp). Suggested the party visit the [Vallaki Orphanage]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki-orphanage" >}}) and reassured them their adoption document was safe to carry. Ireena decided not to stay with Walpole yet and remained with the party longer. Appears in [Session 32]({{< ref "/sessions/session-32" >}}).

### Session 50
Visited by the party. Shared that he believes the abbot of the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) in [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}) is a fallen angel, and that Krezk's residents are very secretive about the abbey when encountered in Vallaki. Also mentioned that [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) has been visiting him regularly and the two have developed a friendly acquaintance; Vasili recently gave him a large sack of potatoes but kept his political allegiances vague. He has received no further news from [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}). [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) donated 2 gp; [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) arranged a paid meal for him at a tavern. Appears in [Session 50]({{< ref "/sessions/session-50" >}}).
