## Overview

Bela is the heavy-set, older of the mill sisters. She was asleep when the party arrived at the mill and awoke during their initial conversation with [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}). She possesses the ability to paralyze targets and issued a demand for the party to drop their weapons when [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) took [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) hostage.

The tortured man the party discovered in the cave identified his captors by the initial letters of their names — one starting with **"B"** and another with **"O"** — suggesting Bela and [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}) are responsible for his imprisonment and for kidnapping his daughters.

## Personality

Threatening and aggressive once her household was disturbed.

## Goals and Motivations

Unknown in full, but appears to be involved in capturing people and harvesting body parts in connection with the mill's operations.
