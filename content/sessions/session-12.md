## Summary

The party's respite at the inn in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) is violently interrupted by a coordinated undead assault. A horde of zombies and a Manticore attack the establishment, forcing the party into desperate combat. [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) awakens during the final moments of the battle, complaining of a headache. The aftermath reveals both critical intelligence about [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) connection to [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and signs of [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) vulnerability. The party agrees to extend their stay for a full long rest before continuing their journey.

## Events

### Sudden Attack

Before [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) could depart the inn to seek armor repair assistance, the establishment came under siege. A horde of zombies, accompanied by a **Manticore**, launched a coordinated assault on the inn. The attack caught most of the party by surprise, but [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) heard the tremendous noise and immediately rushed upstairs to defend the more vulnerable members of the establishment.

### Combat Report

The party engaged the undead horde in fierce combat:
- **Enemies Defeated:** Approximately 8 zombies and 1 Manticore
- **Notable Participants:** [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}), [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) coordinated the defense
- **Outcome:** The party successfully repelled the assault, though the inn sustained significant damage

### Ireena's Awakening

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) awoke near the end of the combat, complaining of a severe headache. The timing of her awakening—coinciding with the final stages of battle—suggested the enchantment may be impeding her ability to act independently during crisis situations.

### Critical Information About Ireena

Following the combat, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) pressed [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) for more information about [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) relationship with [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}). [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) revealed that **[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) had direct contact with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) when she was a child**, though she has no clear memory of these encounters. This suggests a long-standing connection between the vampire and [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) predating the current campaign.

### Dirko's Return and Condition

[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) returned to the tavern during or shortly after the combat. He was **noticeably wounded**, reporting that something had struck him during his perimeter reconnaissance but he could not discern what the attacker was. Recognizing the need for medical support, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) consumed a healing potion to stabilize his condition. He generously offered additional healing potions to both [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) for their wounds sustained during the battle.

### Post-Combat Actions

**[Arik]({{< ref "/npcs/arik" >}}):** The tavern keeper immediately took action, preparing food for the exhausted party and gathering cloths to begin cleaning and repairing the inn following the undead assault.

**[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}):** Took responsibility for removing the bodies of the defeated undead from the inn. While performing this grim task, he discovered **12 gold pieces** on the corpse of the Manticore.

**[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}):** Exhausted from combat, retired to sleep.

### Decision to Delay Departure

Despite [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) apparent desire to continue traveling toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party collectively agreed to **postpone their departure and conduct a full long rest**. The need for recovery from both physical injuries and magical exhaustion outweighed the urgency of continued travel. The group remains at the inn pending restored strength and tactical reassessment.

## Decisions & Consequences

- **Extended Stay:** The party will remain at the inn for a full long rest, delaying departure to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}})
- **Tactical Assessment:** The coordinated nature of the undead assault suggests [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) capability to direct minions even without direct presence
- **Ireena's Limitations:** Her childhold contact with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and the enchantment's effects on her consciousness present a strategic liability for the party
- **Resource Gain:** 12 gold pieces acquired from the Manticore corpse
- **Directko's Vulnerability:** An unknown force wounded [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) during reconnaissance, raising questions about what threatens the village perimeter
