## Summary

The party ventures deeper into [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) on their three-day journey to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}). After hiking for 30 minutes, two furry creatures ambush [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), claiming to challenge him to a duel under the forest's rules. The party suspects deception and engages in combat. Following the battle, they discover mysterious magical items in the forest—a golden sword and a beautiful hat—that appear to be linked to one another. The party establishes camp to rest and investigate these items.

## Events

### Departure from Barovia

[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) reinforces to the party that a sealed letter must only be opened once they reach [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}). [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) and [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) share a farewell before the party departs, along with the rest of the party members saying goodbye.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) continues to feel a persistent emptiness in his chest—the location where [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) struck him during their prior encounter.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) notices a thin cut across his pectoral, though it does not cause him significant concern.

The party establishes their travel order: [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) travel ahead, [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) travel in the middle, and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) travels in the rear. The journey is expected to take three days.

### Journey into the Forest

[Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) is not densely packed, with fog present but not suffocating. After approximately 30 minutes of travel beyond the departure point, [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) rolls a 12 on a 1d12 check.

Two furry creatures block the path after crossing a bridge. They declare "Parados aí!" (stop!) and demand that [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) engage in a duel with them, claiming to do so at the request of their chief and invoking "the rules of the forest."

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) mentally alerts [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to be prepared for interference. [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) remains unaware of the situation.

### The Message

During the confrontation, a raven bearing parchments in its talons flies overhead. The parchments resemble an open scroll held in its claws.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) accepts the duel on the creatures' terms. The raven releases two parchments and flies away. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) moves to intercept the parchments and quickly determines that the creatures are impostors, breaking the rules of the duel and engaging in combat.

### Combat and Aftermath

The party engages the two furry creatures and eliminates them. Ranged creatures—likely archers—strike the party from a distance with arrows before withdrawing from combat. The rangers' arrows cause significant damage to the party, particularly [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}).

[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) sustains critical wounds and nearly dies. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) offers [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) a healing potion. However, consuming it causes [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to worsen significantly. The party identifies that the potion was provided by [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}})—the revealed agent of [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}})—and determines they can no longer trust potions from him. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) mutters, "There is a traitor among us," though the group does not pursue the matter further.

### Exploration of the Fork

The party continues forward and encounters a fork in the road. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), following his intuition, identifies that the right path—which has less fog and more vibrant vegetation—is the preferable route.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) advances stealthily ahead to scout the path and discovers a bear feeding in a clearing. Behind the bear, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) observes:
- A golden sword with a swirling design etched into its hilt
- A beautiful hat

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) takes the hat and attempts to retrieve it, but the sword vanishes when the hat is removed from the clearing.

### Discovery and Investigation

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) returns to the party and relays his findings. The party agrees to proceed cautiously and avoids direct confrontation with the bear by taking a path that circumnavigates it. However, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) make excessive noise, prompting the bear to approach.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) uses dissonant whispers to frighten the bear into fleeing the area.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) return to investigate the bear's location further, while [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}), [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) maintain a distance and wait.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) encounters the sword floating in place. As he pursues it, he stumbles and the hat falls from his head. The sword immediately disappears. Upon placing the hat back on his head, the sword becomes visible again. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) theorizes that the hat grants visibility to the sword and requests the party to continue following the sword's trajectory, which aligns with the direction they were already heading.

### Rest and Research

The party establishes camp for a short rest. During this time, the group engages in discussion regarding magic items. [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) questions whether the party possesses knowledge of magic items and advises them that properties can be discovered by meditating while in contact with an item.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) meditates with the hat to discover its properties. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) analyze [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) dagger. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) discusses the properties of his silver sword.

The party remains surrounded by fog as they conclude their rest.

## Decisions & Consequences

- **Distrust of Dirko**: The party now knows without question that healing potions provided by [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) are unreliable and potentially harmful
- **Magical Items**: The party has acquired two mysterious magic items and is beginning to investigate their properties
- **Route Selection**: The party chose the right path based on [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) intuition, continuing toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}})

## Loose Threads

- What are the true properties and origins of the golden sword and beautiful hat?
- Who were the two furry creatures, and who sent them?
- What was contained in the parchments the raven dropped?
- Why did the bear appear in this location with the magical items?
- Will [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) condition improve, or will the effects of [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) potion linger?
