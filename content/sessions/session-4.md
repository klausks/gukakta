## Summary

The party descended to the basement of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) in search of the monster. They explored several chambers, discovering a hanging corpse with a suspected suicide note, a mysterious potion, and treasure around a well. They encountered the spirit of [Mr. Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}), who offered cryptic warnings before vanishing. As they ventured deeper, signs of undead activity became apparent, and the party was ambushed by three creatures — the encounter unresolved.

## Events

### Descent to the Basement

The party descended from the attic through a long staircase into the basement dungeon level of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}).

### Chamber of the Hanging Body

In one of the early chambers, the party discovered the corpse of a person hanging by the neck. Upon investigation, they found a letter in the body's pocket — a note that appeared to be a suicide message. The letter was recovered.

### The Contaminated Potion

Near the hanging body, the party discovered a flask containing a liquid resembling a healing potion. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) identified it but suspected the potion had been "blessed" (contaminated or cursed in some way).

### Encounter with the Spirit of Mr. Durst

In another chamber, the party encountered the spirit of [Mr. Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}), the father of the Durst family. He spoke of a grim pattern: people arrive in this place and die to the monster. When asked directly if the party would kill his wife, he provided no useful information and then vanished, leaving the party with more questions than answers.

### Treasury Around the Well

In a chamber containing beds and chests arranged around a well, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) opened all the chests and the party recovered:

- 11 gold pieces + 60 silver pieces
- 3 moss agates
- One black leather eye patch with a brown/caramel colored attachment
- One ivory brush with silver bristles
- One silver short sword (claimed by [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}))

### Undead Beneath the Earth

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) detected the presence of four undead creatures moving beneath the ground near the party's position.

### Ambush in the Corridor

As the party moved through one of the corridors, they were suddenly ambushed by three monsters. Combat ensued but was not completed before the session ended.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party recovered significant treasure and magical items.
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) acquired a silver short sword.
- The party learned that the monster claims lives regularly, according to [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) spirit.
- [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) cryptic warning about killing his wife hints at further family tragedy or danger.

- Is the potion truly cursed/blessed, or does it have another purpose?
