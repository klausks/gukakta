## Summary

In the aftermath of the duels, the party rests at the Vistani encampment, settles loose ends, and prepares to depart for [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}). Before leaving, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) is drawn into a mysterious painting by [Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}) and meets [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}), who bestows the full privileges of a Forest Master upon him and reveals unsettling truths about [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) past connection to entities of war. The party then sets out on the road to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), crossing an impressive stone bridge, where they are approached by two mice carrying strange dolls with human teeth.

## Events

### Aftermath of the Duels

Searching [Perwimple's]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) body, the party found: 3 white candles, 1 rag doll, and 1 dirty iron spoon.

The party agreed to return to camp to rest. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) praised [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) for his performance in combat.

### Pies and Temptation

When hunger set in, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) were reminded of the suspicious pies they had acquired earlier. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), exercising rare self-control, threw his two pie slices into the fire. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) could not resist and retrieved a piece directly from the embers.

### Preparations to Leave

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) expressed unease about remaining at the camp, urging the party to head for [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) as soon as possible. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) agreed on the route.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) discussed the wine shortage in Barovia. A Vistani named [Durena]({{< ref "/npcs/durena" >}}) overheard and approached, noting that the wine suppliers are located to the northwest and that supply has grown scarcer. She mentioned two possible explanations: excessive consumption by the Vistani themselves, or creatures that are opposed to drink. She added that planting crops in Barovia is difficult, despite [Esdras']({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) talk of luck.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asked [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) about what it means to be a Forest Master. [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) told him that [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) would be in contact soon, and that the meeting would be amicable.

The party sold several items to [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}): a Pike for 270gp ([Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})), Chain Mail for 70gp ([Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})), and a magic cloth for 315gp ([Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}})).

[Agares']({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) curse was lifted before the party departed. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) received a *Potion of Greater Healing* from the Vistani. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was given a silver coin by an unnamed lord as a symbol of hope and good faith.

The party chose to travel to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) via the route passing the mill, as it aligns with [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) prophecy. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) noted that when using the hat, its enchanted sword points toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

### The Painting and Lord Balkor

Shortly after leaving camp, the fire spirit [Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}) emerged from a tree, carrying a painting. He beckoned [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to approach. The painting was moving: it depicted a bearded Vistani in dark robes who spoke directly to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), commanding him to enter.

After alerting his companions, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) stepped into the painting. Inside, he found a room with two doors, where [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) sat upon a wooden throne.

[Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) conveyed the following:

1. The party's debts have been paid.
2. The party will no longer have trouble with the mists.
3. By striking any tree three times, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) can conceal himself inside it, becoming invisible.
4. By ringing a small golden bell, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) can request information from forest informants (a tip of 20–100gp is expected).
5. Duel challenges will decrease significantly. Companions of a Forest Master are rarely targeted; the Forest Master himself is the expected duelist.

[Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) then asked what [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) desired. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) mentioned, beyond his mission to escort [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) safely to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), his long-standing confusion about the war domain from his past.

[Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) explained that great forces exist in the world representing fear, despair, storm, and fury—and among them, entities of war. These immortal dark beings are still tormented by human needs, which they fulfill through human servants. [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) believes [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) was a failed attempt at becoming such a servant. He told [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) that he would receive an item through one of the forest informants that would allow him to make contact with this entity.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) returned to the party and relayed the Forest Master privileges. He then tested the tree-hiding ability—it worked.

### The Stone Bridge and the Mice

The party came upon an impressive waterfall and an ornate stone bridge lined with stone gargoyles. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) remarked on her fear of heights as they crossed.

Near the far end of the bridge, two mice approached carrying dolls made of string and sticks. Two of the dolls had human teeth tied to their feet. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast *Detect Magic*: both the dolls and the mice radiate magical auras. The school of magic has not yet been identified.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Agares']({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) curse has been lifted.
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) has received the full privileges of a Forest Master from [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}).
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) will receive a mysterious item through a forest informant to contact a war entity.
- The party is en route to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) via the mill road.
