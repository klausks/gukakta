## Summary

In a grotto off the road, the party’s pursuit of a giant rat with a stolen scimitar takes an unexpected turn when the creature trembles and transforms into a hairy, hunched humanoid—revealing the cave encounter as something closer to a staged play than a simple animal attack.

After withdrawing and regrouping, the party continues along the [Svalich Road]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-road" >}}). At a crossroads they find a sign pointing south toward [Berez]({{< ref "/locations/berez" >}}), and as they near the [Waschter Forest]({{< ref "/locations/waschter-forest" >}}) the mist thickens and the sounds of horses draw close.

Two wagons of mercenaries stop near the party’s hiding place and threaten Vedas’ owl with crossbows. Agares senses no supernatural presence, and the wagons eventually move on.

Soon after, the fog closes in and the road splits again: a sign points south toward the [Cabins]({{< ref "/locations/cabins" >}}) and north toward the Aligned Tree(s) of the [Svalich Road]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-road" >}})—just as three unknown creatures surround the party.

## Events

### The Grotto Without Mist
Inside the cave, there is no mist—only an odd, unidentifiable smell. The wounded hairy man lies with a bloodied mouth, babbling.

The giant rat continues deeper with the scimitar in its mouth, and the party hears stones shifting in the darkness.

### The Rat Transforms
The rat trembles and transforms into a humanoid figure, addressing the wounded man: *"Great brother, you performed well."* The pair offer to avoid conflict, claiming there is no need for everyone equally strong to kill each other.

Both appear human, but with more body hair and a slight hunch. The scimitar is described as unusually beautiful.

### A Failed Ambush
Agares hears movement in the bushes outside. The party steps out, and Jimmy bluntly declares they are back and that it was a waste of time. Igga remarks that it was a failed ambush.

### Crossroads Sign: Berez
At a crossroads, the party finds a sign with elegant lettering pointing south: Berez.

### Into the Mist Near Waschter Forest
Continuing along the Svalich Road near the Waschter Forest, the mist thickens. The forest is closed-in, with loud insects and frogs, and the party hears horses approaching.

### Mercenary Wagons and the Owl
Two wagons with horses stop near where the party is hidden. Mercenaries aim crossbows at Vedas’ owl, drink briefly, and then continue onward. Agares uses divine sense and detects nothing unusual.

### The Fork and the Sign
As the fog closes in, a fork in the road appears. A sign indicates two routes:

- South: Cabins
- North: Aligned Tree

At the same moment, three creatures surround the party.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party withdraws from the grotto without escalating the situation.
- The party stays hidden as the mercenary wagons pass, avoiding a direct confrontation.

## Loose Threads

- Who were the two hairy, hunched humans, and why did one appear as a giant rat?
- What is the significance of the beautiful scimitar?
- Who are the mercenaries on the wagons, and why were they so hostile to the owl?
- What lies in Berez, the Cabins, and at the Aligned Tree?
- What are the three creatures that surrounded the party at the fork?
