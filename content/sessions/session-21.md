## Summary

The party investigates the mysterious dolls from the bridge and discovers they are physically linked to [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), the two who ate the suspicious pies. After fleeing a large creature on the bridge, the party encounters and kills a white-skinned creature that drains vital energy. During their rest, they discover that [Igga Bevneros']({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) mending spell applied to a doll fully heals its linked bearer. The next morning, at the fork in the road between [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}) and [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party finds [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) carriage awaiting them with a personal letter. The group elects to ignore the invitation and press on to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

## Events

### Investigating the Dolls

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) confirmed the school of magic on the dolls is **enchantment** (though it initially felt similar to transmutation), and **divination** magic on the rats. The party is still on the stone bridge—high up, with a waterfall visible and mountains and forest stretching into the distance.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) picked up one doll and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) felt a strange sensation. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) then picked up the other doll, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) felt it instead. The group concluded that each doll is physically linked to one of them—pressure applied to a doll is felt by its bearer. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) proposed using them as a form of communication.

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) tested whether it was possible to speak through the dolls, but no voice transmission occurred—only pressure and touch sensations. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) suggested that only he and [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) received dolls because they were the ones who ate the pies. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) remarked: *"I told you so."*

Each party member agreed to be responsible for their own doll. Shortly after, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) heard [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) voice inside his head asking him to go ahead.

### Fleeing the Bridge Creature

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) spotted a glimpse of a creature's arm on the large bridge. When [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) heard the sighting came from the bridge, he urged the group to run. As the party sprinted, a faint tremor rumbled beneath their feet.

### The White-Skinned Creature

Further along the path, the party was surprised by a white-skinned creature that drained [Igga Bevneros']({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) vital energy. The party killed it; the creature dissolved into dust. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) collected the dust in a vial. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) detected no magical aura on the dust whatsoever, and laughed.

### Resting — The Dolls as Healing Anchors

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) found a suitable campsite and the party decided to rest immediately. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) mentioned that some Vistani had been returning to the encampment injured, reporting strange attacks from wild animals—rats, toads, and similar creatures. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was troubled by this and set a pile of food at a distance from himself before sleeping.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) noticed his doll appeared to be in a deteriorated state mirroring his own wounded condition. He suggested that [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast his mending spell on the doll. [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) did so, and [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) was fully healed. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) immediately requested the same to confirm the effect—it worked. The party now knows that **[Igga Bevneros']({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) mending spell applied to a doll fully heals its linked bearer**.

The party completed a long rest.

### Strahd's Carriage

The following morning, near the fork in the road between [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}) and [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party found a magnificent carriage stopped with its door open. Two large black horses stamped impatiently.

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) immediately advised retreating. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) withdrew with the group but used his Forest Master ability to conceal himself inside a nearby tree with a view of the carriage. From there, he confirmed the carriage was empty.

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was bitten by a rat.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) approached the carriage stealthily. Inside, on the front bench, lay a high-quality letter reading:

> *"Come home, Master Monel, Jimmy, and Ireena Kolyana."*

On the reverse:

> *"Master, what would you do if your item never arrived? I await you.*
> *— Count Strahd Von Zarovich"*

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) expressed displeasure at not being mentioned. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) explained the reference to the item, relaying what [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) had told him in Session 20.

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) wondered aloud whether [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) invitation was effectively an order. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) reasoned that duels are always explicit, and that even if it were, facing [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) now would be near-certain death—better to accept the consequences of declining than to walk into a slaughter.

The group agreed unanimously to ignore the carriage and continue to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

## Decisions & Consequences

- The dolls are confirmed as an enchantment-linked to [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), likely as a result of eating the pies. They mirror the bearer's injuries and can be fully healed via [Igga's]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) mending spell.
- The white-skinned creature is dead; its dust has no magical aura and was collected by [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}).
- [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) invitation was refused. The party continues toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).
- Wild animals appear to be attacking people in the area ([Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) reported, and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was bitten by a rat).
