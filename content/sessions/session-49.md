## Summary

The party's first full day reunited in Vallaki. The morning opens with a long meeting with [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) at the Blue Water Inn, during which the party collects their winery rewards, discusses Vallakian politics, and cements their alliance with the Martikovs — including receiving a trained crow named Kiki as a communication and distress tool. Agares reviews the accumulated evidence against [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and shares his conclusions with the group. In the afternoon, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) find [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) sheltering at [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}), where she has been experiencing disturbing visions. Meanwhile, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) schedule a breakfast meeting with the Baron for the following morning.

## Key Events

### Winery Rewards

Urwin gave the party their reward for the winery mission: two bottles of Champagne Du le Stomp wine and the staff that had been left behind at the winery. Separately, a Martikov couple arrived with their children carrying additional items: a horse, a white owl, one more Du le Stomp, and the staff — which had to be flown over Vallaki's palisade by crows, as it could not pass through the gates. Urwin already knew what had happened at the winery and expressed concern about the mission's next stages — recovering the missing gem and restoring production.

### Political Intelligence

Urwin confirmed the party's existing understanding of Vallaki's politics: [Baron Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) rules with an iron fist but keeps the city functioning; the [Wachter family]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) is influential, though rumors persist of strange gatherings at their manor. The party asked about [Izek]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}})'s demonic arm — Urwin had no information on its origins. He did mention that Izek had passed through the inn asking about a redheaded woman, which Urwin found unusual. He also revealed that Izek appears to be romantically interested in the noble [Rosemary]({{< ref "/npcs/rosemary" >}}), though the feeling is not returned.

### Cementing the Martikov Alliance

Agares spoke for the group: their objective is to destroy Strahd and all his allies. Urwin appeared moved but visibly unsettled by the scope of the goal. He reiterated that the Martikov spy network — operated via crows — is available to the party, and offered them a crow as a personal communication and distress tool. The party named the crow **[Kiki]({{< ref "/npcs/kiki" >}})**. [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) voiced concern about being surveilled through the crow, since Martikovs can see through their birds. Urwin explained that they do not passively watch — they must consciously focus on a specific crow to see through it — and that the party is free to ask Kiki to step away for privacy. He seemed briefly offended by the suspicion but reassured the group warmly. The conversation ended on good terms.

### Agares Reviews the Evidence

After the meeting, Agares shared his assessment with the party. He noted: Urwin mentioned that at a dinner she hosted, Fiona Wachter said something to the effect of *"I'd rather trust the devil than this baron"* — Urwin believed she was testing his political loyalties. Combined with the macabre cult rumors, her possession of a fiendish familiar, her apparent interest in Agares' infernal heritage, and Viktor's accusations from the previous session, Agares placed his suspicion of Fiona at roughly 98%. He announced he is working on a plan and will present it to the group.

### Finding Ireena

Monel and Agares went to St. Andral's Church. [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) confirmed Ireena is sheltering there, feeling strange, and spends her days praying in the crypt of Saint Andral. When called, Ireena came out and embraced them. She described feeling out of sorts but did not know the cause. The party did not tell her that Izek had been asking about redheaded women at the inn.

Father Lucian reminded the party about the Saint Andral feast, which falls on the same day as Vallaki's festival. He noted that his sister is married to the Baron, which has made coordinating both events more manageable.

### Ireena's Visions

Ireena described troubling recurring visions: enormous arms — far larger than any single humanoid's — that appear to tower over her. The arms are wreathed in flames. Father Lucian, present during this exchange, noted that Izek Stravni is known to project fire from his demonic arm. The implication that the arms in Ireena's visions might belong to Izek was left unspoken.

### Scheduling a Meeting with the Baron

Jimmy and Igga went to the Burgomaster's residence to request an audience with Baron Vallakovich. They were received by a secretary and scheduled a breakfast meeting for the following morning. Both then made their way to St. Andral's Church, where the full party regrouped.

### The Young Gravedigger

Outside the church, Igga and Jimmy encountered a young man — roughly 15 to 20 years old — who was visibly disgruntled, complaining about his life and about the church itself. He appeared to be employed there as a gravedigger.

### At the Inn

Back at the Blue Water Inn, the party noticed two young men dressed with unusual refinement, though unshaven and somewhat disheveled, drinking and talking with [Danika Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/danika-martikov" >}}). [Rictavio]({{< ref "/npcs/rictavio" >}}) was also present, as was [Ernst Larnak]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-ernst" >}}). At some point during the day, Jimmy closed up suddenly — appearing preoccupied or disturbed — but said he was fine and needed to think.

## Discoveries & Clues

- Izek has been asking about redheaded women at the Blue Water Inn — Urwin found this unusual.
- Izek appears to have a romantic interest in [Rosemary]({{< ref "/npcs/rosemary" >}}), though it is not returned.
- Fiona Wachter once told Urwin at a dinner that she would *"rather trust the devil than the baron"* — Urwin interpreted it as a political loyalty test.
- Ireena is sheltering in the crypt of Saint Andral and suffering recurring visions of enormous flaming arms — possibly connected to Izek.
- Father Lucian Petrovich's sister is [Lydia Petrovna]({{< ref "/npcs/lydia-petrovna" >}}), who is married to the Baron.
- The Martikovs have given the party a crow named **Kiki** as a communication link to their spy network.
- A young gravedigger was spotted outside St. Andral's Church.
- Jimmy Slit appears to be concealing something.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party chose to cement their alliance with the Martikovs and accepted Kiki.
- The group did not inform Ireena that Izek has been asking about redheaded women.
- Agares is developing a plan targeting Fiona Wachter.
- A breakfast meeting with Baron Vallakovich has been scheduled for the following morning.
