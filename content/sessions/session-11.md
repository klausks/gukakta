## Summary

The party takes shelter at [Arik's inn]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) to recuperate and plan their next steps. A critical discovery is made when [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demonic companion detects that [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) has been enchanted by [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), presenting two possible threats: she may be cursed to never wake from sleep, or she serves as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) remote eyes and ears. The party establishes security measures while preparing to depart in the morning.

## Events

### Supplies and Respite

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) purchased pies from a local vendor in the village. The party then made their way to [Arik's]({{< ref "/npcs/arik" >}}) inn to secure shelter and plan their departure from [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) offered the group wine as they settled in.

### Secret Conversation

[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) and [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) withdrew for a private conversation. Afterward, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) departed the inn, with [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) explaining that he had decided to scout the perimeter of the village. The party agreed to depart the following morning, allowing [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) time to complete his reconnaissance.

### Supernatural Detection

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) sensed an unusual chill emanating from [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}). [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) noticed this reaction and began observing [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) closely as she made notes in a notebook. 

[Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demonic companion manifested, urging him to touch [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to discern more about her condition. When [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) made contact with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) under the guise of checking on her wellbeing, the demon provided a troubling revelation: [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) has been **enchanted by [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}})**.

The enchantment presents two possible effects:
1. **Curse of Eternal Sleep:** She may be hexed to never awaken from sleep
2. **Remote Observation:** She may serve as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) eyes, ears, and nose from a distance—a conduit through which the vampire can observe the party remotely

### Security Protocol

Recognizing the threat level, [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) assigned [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) to watch the back door of the inn, which faces the forest and presents a likely entry point for potential attackers. [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) himself stationed himself at the front entrance to observe [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) return and guard against surprise attacks from the street.

### Nighttime Activities

[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) retired to her room. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) spent the evening attempting to repair his armor, which had sustained damage during the confrontation with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) in [Session 10]({{< ref "/sessions/session-10" >}}).

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) joined [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) to discuss the implications of his demonic companion's revelation and [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) possible curse. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) sought to validate the demon's reliability and observed [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to determine if there were signs of her inability to wake. His assessment: she appeared to be sleeping normally.

### Armor Repairs and Departure Plans

[Arik]({{< ref "/npcs/arik" >}}) mentioned that someone nearby possessed tools and equipment that could assist [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) in repairing his damaged armor. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) discussed this possibility with [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), who initially expressed uncertainty but ultimately agreed to allow [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) to leave the inn for two purposes: to locate the blacksmith or craftsperson who could provide armor repair assistance, and to encounter [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) during his perimeter reconnaissance.

## Decisions & Consequences

- **Accepted Enchantment Risk:** The party now understands that [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) may be compromised as either a sleeping curse victim or as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) remote observer—either way, escorting her may limit tactical secrecy
- **Security Measures Implemented:** Defensive positioning at inn entrances to prepare for night attack
- **Armor Repairs:** [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) will seek out a craftsperson to repair equipment damaged by [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}})
- **Departure Delayed:** The party remains in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) through the night, departing the next morning

## Loose Threads

- Can the party break or counter the enchantment before departing?
- Who is the craftsperson who can repair [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) armor, and what will they ask in return?
- What discoveries does [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) make during his perimeter reconnaissance?
