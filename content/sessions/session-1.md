## Summary

The party, already loosely acquainted from previous jobs, set out following a well-known legend: a high elf's chest of extraordinary value, said to be hidden inside an enormous tree deep within a forest. All who had gone before them were never seen again. Drawn by the promise of the reward, the party followed the trail toward the great tree — and found themselves pulled into something far beyond a simple treasure hunt.

## Events

### The Road and the Forest

Faced with a choice between a straight road stretching endlessly into the distance and the forest ahead, the party identified a massive tree in the distance that matched the legend's description. With night already upon them and their horses growing restless, they agreed to camp in the forest on their way toward the great tree.

### The Draining Ground

During the night watch, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) nearly fell asleep on duty and noticed that the ground near his companions was slowly pulling objects downward, as if being absorbed into the earth. He woke the rest of the party and most of their belongings were saved. Their horses, however, had vanished.

### The Mist and the Path

Once everyone was awake and alert, the party noticed that fog blanketed the entire area — except for a single clear path. As time passed, that path revealed a house in the distance.

### Arrival at the Durst House

Approaching the house, the party encountered two children: siblings [Rosalva Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}). The children informed the party that they were now in Barovia, and that the forest they had been travelling through was the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). The children also revealed they were standing outside because they feared a monster supposedly locked in the basement of the house — a creature their parents kept imprisoned there.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party chose to enter the forest rather than follow the main road, which led them directly into the mists of Barovia.
- Camping in the forest resulted in the loss of their horses.
- The party approached the Durst house and made contact with the Durst children.

## Loose Threads

- What is the monster locked in the basement of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}})?
- Where are the children's parents?
- What happened to all who previously sought the chest in the great tree?
- How does the party escape Barovia?
