## Summary

The party encounters a druid at the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}) and incapacitates him. While investigating the basement, they discover a hidden door concealed behind a movable bookshelf. Behind this door, they overhear a cryptic magical conversation about the Martikovs and a mysterious figure named Swigas, who is being compelled to search for something. A portal suddenly opens and closes, and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) manage to enter it before it seals, leaving the rest of the party behind.

## Key Events

### Encounter with the Druid

The party encountered a druid at the winery and knocked him unconscious. Before being subdued, the druid was heard mumbling an incantation:

> "Oh great tree, resurrect... Come to us again..."

### Magical Investigation

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast Detect Magic and sensed something strange in the room—specifically a conjuration effect. [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) commented that the effect likely came from summoned creatures. Igga, however, disagreed with this assessment.

Igga also noticed that the druid's staff possessed a magical bond that extended far beyond the druid's own personal magic—suggesting it was tied to a greater force or entity.

### Discovery in the Basement

While exploring the basement, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) identified that a bookshelf could be moved, revealing a hidden door behind it. The discovery of this concealed entrance suggested a secret chamber or passage within the winery.

### The Overheard Conversation

Beyond the hidden door, the party heard a strange magical sound, followed by a conversation in what seemed to be a magical transmission or summoning:

> "You failed with your lord, the Martikovs must have given up again. It's your turn Swigas, go, discover again."

Moments later, footsteps were heard, and another voice cried out:

> "Wait!"
>
> "Yes, Rahadin?"
>
> "Come back, come back now."

When [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) shouted, "Swigas, are you there?"—a response came back:

> "THE BOOK!"

Immediately after this exclamation, the voice was silenced, suggesting that Swigas was gagged or restrained.

### The Portal Opens

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) caught a glimpse of two **twilight elves** before a magical portal materialized in front of the party. As the portal began to close, Igga and Monel managed to enter it.

The rest of the party remained behind as the portal sealed.

## Discoveries & Clues

- The druid's staff is tied to something greater than his own power—possibly a corrupted tree or forest entity.
- A mysterious figure named **Swigas** is being forced to search for something and appears to be a prisoner or captive.
- The mention of **"The Book"** suggests Swigas was trying to communicate something important about a specific tome.
- **Twilight elves** are present at or near the winery—their connection to the druid or the portal is unknown.
- A **magical portal** exists within the winery's basement, suggesting a connection to other planes or locations.
- The Martikovs are mentioned in a context of failure and abandonment, hinting at a larger conflict.

## Decisions & Consequences

- Igga and Monel entered the portal and its fate is currently unknown.
- The remaining party is now separated and unaware of what awaits their companions on the other side of the portal.
- The hidden druid has not been interrogated or restrained—his current status and intentions remain unclear.

## Loose Threads

- What is the nature of the druid's connection to the resurrected tree?
- What role do the twilight elves play in the winery conflict?
- Where does the portal lead, and what will Igga and Monel encounter there?
- Who is Rahadin?
- How does this hidden chamber and portal relate to the stolen gems or the Martikov mission?
