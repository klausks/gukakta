## Summary

The party met with the merchant [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), only to discover he was an arrogant trader content only to deal with known associates. He demanded proof of the party's moral character, suggesting they clear zombies from a house—without compensation. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) wisely deferred this task. The party then proceeded to the [Burgomaster's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/burgomaster-mansion" >}}) where they met the Burgomaster's daughter [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) for the first time. [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) provided critical supplies and equipment, and the group made the decision to execute [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) escape on the very same day, immediately after the Burgomaster's funeral. The burial ceremony at the church was interrupted twice by disturbing whispered screams from behind a locked door.

## Events

### The Merchant Bildrat
The party arrived at the shop of **[Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}})**, a short, arrogant merchant. He was accompanied by his personal bodyguard, **[Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}})**.

[Bildrat's]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) Dismissal:
- He refused to appraise or trade the party's items from the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}), citing that he dealt only with known acquaintances.
- As a "test," he suggested the party eliminate the zombies infesting a house in the village as proof of their character.
- When [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) asked about compensation or discount, [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) flatly refused.
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) wisely suggested postponing this task, prioritizing the Burgomaster's burial instead.

### Return to the Inn
The party returned to the inn to meet [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) and prepare to transport the Burgomaster's coffin.

### The Burgomaster's Mansion
The party traveled to the **[Burgomaster's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/burgomaster-mansion" >}})** with [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}).

#### Meeting Ireena Kolyana
Upon arrival, the party was introduced to **[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}})**, the Burgomaster's daughter. She is described as:
- Beautiful
- Red-haired
- Wearing armor of excellent quality

[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) greeted the party warmly, offering them food and drink.

#### Supplies and Equipment
Within the mansion, [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) provided the party with supplies and equipment:

**[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}):**
- 10 arrows
- 2 flasks of oil
- 1 flask of poison
- 1 poison kit

**[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}):**
- 1 new shield

**[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}):**
- 1 new shield

#### Preparation for Escape
[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) and [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) decided to execute the escape plan immediately after the Burgomaster's burial ceremony.

**Disguise and Preparations:**
- [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) utilized [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) disguise kit.
- [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) donned the purple hooded cloak recovered from [Mrs. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mrs-durst" >}}) chamber in the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}).

**Supplies for Travel:**
- The party received a map of the region.
- The party received a sealed letter from [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), instructed to open it in [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) if the escort was successful.

### The Burgomaster's Funeral
The party accompanied [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) and the Burgomaster's coffin to the **church** in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}).

#### Disturbances During the Ceremony
As the funeral ceremony began, presided over by [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}), the proceedings were repeatedly interrupted:
- **First interruption:** Whispered screams emanated from behind a locked door
- **Second interruption:** The same disturbing whispered screams occurred again

The source of these sounds and what lies behind the door remain unknown.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party deferred the merchant's test (clearing zombies) to a later time.
- The party accepted [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) immediate escape plan, committing to escort her out of [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) on the same day.
- The party received critical supplies and a map for their journey to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).
