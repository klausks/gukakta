## Summary

The party prepares for departure from [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) secures armor repair tools from a mysterious [Vistanis]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) named [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}), who hints at Jimmy's supernatural perception and mentions a potential cure to his curse. A terrifying discovery emerges when [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) approaches [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) with an offer: freedom from his demonic burden in exchange for becoming [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) informant. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) discovers personal items in [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) backpack, revealing supplies and a mysterious notebook. The party learns critical intelligence about [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) true allegiance before departure, though uncertainty clouds how to respond to this revelation.

## Events

### Rest and Recovery

Following the agreed long rest, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) retired to sleep. [Arik]({{< ref "/npcs/arik" >}}) suggested he rest in [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) room, which [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) accepted. Upon lying down, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) discovered [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) backpack was half-open. He observed its contents without disturbing them:
- Wines and food supplies
- A healing potion
- A beautifully crafted lantern
- A notebook with a pen

[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) completed her rest and spent the morning in the center of the tavern conversing with [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}).

### The Vistanis and the Tools

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) traveled to the residence of a [Vistanis]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) named [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) to secure the armor repair tools for [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}).

During their encounter, [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) made cryptic observations about [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}):
- Remarked that [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) eyes have "seen much"
- Revealed that all [Vistanis]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) possess supernatural powers
- Expressed regret that [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) would learn only the blessing powers of the [Vistanis]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) peoples, not their curses
- **Critical Information:** Mentioned that **Madame Iva** is known to cure curses

[Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) provided the tools and equipment [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) needed for armor repairs.

### Dirko's Temptation

Upon leaving [Thork's]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) residence, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) was confronted by [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}). [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) made a **shocking proposal**: he offered to free [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) from his demonic curse—the very curse that has tormented him since his youth—in exchange for becoming [Count Strahd von Zarovich's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) informant within the party.

[Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) attempted to maintain his composure, but the offer left him in shock. He neither accepted nor refused, leaving the proposal in uncertain limbo.

### Morning and Preparations

The next morning, the elderly pie vendor appeared again at the inn. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) resisted her offer, but [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) purchased two pieces of her pies.

As the party discussed departure and breakfast, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) worked to reduce the weight of the tools and materials [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) had received from [Thork]({{< ref "/npcs/thork" >}}) the previous night.

### Urgent Warning

[Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) went upstairs to gather the remainder of their belongings before departure. Seizing the private moment, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) revealed [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) true nature and allegiance to [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}): **[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) is [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) agent/pawn**. The two discussed the situation but reached no definitive conclusion on how to proceed or whether to confront [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) directly.

As the party prepared to leave the inn, before [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) could rejoin the group and while [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) was out of earshot, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) moved close to [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) and whispered urgently: **"Strahd controla Dirko"** ("Strahd controls Dirko"), attempting to warn the leader of their true threat within the group.

[Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) slept through the night of [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) proposal and the tools acquisition, remaining unaware of these critical developments until the morning debrief.

## Decisions & Consequences

- **Potential Cure Identified:** [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) is confirmed as someone with the ability to cure [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demonic curse—a possible path forward for his deepest motivation
- **Betrayal Revealed:** [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) is confirmed as [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) agent, fundamentally altering the party's understanding of their situation and allies
- **Party Intelligence Shared:** [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) are now aware of [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) true nature, though a unified response strategy has not been determined
- **Jimmy's Temptation:** The offer of curse removal at the price of betraying the party presents a critical moral test for [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}})
- **Ireena's Supplies:** [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) discovery of the notebook and lantern raises questions about [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) preparation and knowledge
