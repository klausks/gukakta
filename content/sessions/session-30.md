## Summary

The party explores central [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) and witnesses a grim public punishment: wounded prisoners chained to a platform and left without food or water for up to two weeks, guarded by soldiers and watched by indifferent (or approving) townsfolk. Beside them is a tortured [Treant Sapling]({{< ref "/creatures/treant-sapling" >}}) — a "baby treant," as [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) identifies it.

The group commissions formal clothing from the tailor [Frederik]({{< ref "/npcs/frederik" >}}) in order to make a good impression when meeting the Burgomaster. There they meet a noblewoman, [Andreja]({{< ref "/npcs/andreja" >}}), who invites [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) to meet [Madame Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) at 11:00 the next day; Igga agrees to accompany him. To speed delivery and further impress, Agares and Igga pay an extra urgency fee.

With the order placed, the party begins deciding what to explore next, taking note of nearby shops, rumors, and potential allies.

## Events

### Toward the Town Center

Following the flow of foot traffic, the party reaches the town's central square. The Stockade draws attention immediately. Rumors circulate that a drunk named [Blut]({{< ref "/npcs/blut" >}}) has been missing for several days.

### The Stockade and the Treant Sapling

Near a small fountain and a wooden platform, several people stand chained and visibly injured — an apparent public prison. When questioned, a guard claims the prisoners are thieves, vandals, or troublemakers and says they remain there for one to two weeks. The guard adds that they are given no food or water so they "suffer more." A treant sapling is also kept there and tormented; the guard says it is tortured for no particular reason.

The surrounding crowd either enjoys the cruelty or ignores it; no one appears sympathetic.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) notes the center is full of opportunities for a thief: tense crowds, easy distractions, and plenty of hiding spots.

### The Tailor and the Invitation

Inside Frederik's shop, the party finds a refined noblewoman drinking fragrant tea in a private area. Frederik quotes prices for made-to-measure clothing:

- 25 gp — traditional tailored clothes
- 50 gp — elegant tailored clothes
- 100 gp — extremely refined tailored clothes

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), Jimmy, and [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) order elegant clothing at 50 gp each.

Agares and Igga pay 70 gp each to include an urgency fee for the meeting the next day.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) already owns noble clothing; Frederik considers it presentable.

Andreja introduces herself and reinforces her desire to introduce Agares to Madame Wachter at 11:00 the next day. She asks Agares to choose who will accompany him; the party agrees that Igga will go.

Andreja and Frederik also suggest bringing a gift when meeting the Burgomaster; Andreja says wine could be appropriate, but that the story attached to the gift matters most.

Frederik privately gossips to Igga about a romance between the Burgomaster's son and Madame Wachter's daughter (details unclear).

Andreja mentions a respected retired hunter, [Scran Ragnar]({{< ref "/npcs/scran-ragnar" >}}), who now sells leather goods.

### Shops and Leads

After leaving the tailor, the party identifies several businesses in the surrounding area:

- [Weiland]({{< ref "/npcs/weiland" >}}) — a forge focused on weapons
- [Anya]({{< ref "/npcs/anya" >}}) — a herbalist
- [Androm]({{< ref "/npcs/androm" >}}) — a bookshop
- Scran Ragnar — leatherworking
- [Konik Halflinger]({{< ref "/npcs/konik-halflinger" >}}) — wagons and woodcraft

They also note the presence of a potion shop, a church (not yet visited), a plush toy shop, and a coffin shop.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party committed gold to acquire presentable clothing for political meetings in Vallaki.
- Agares accepted an invitation to meet Madame Wachter at 11:00 the next day, with Igga accompanying him.

## Loose Threads

- What happened to Blut, and why is his disappearance being discussed?
- Why is Vallaki publicly torturing prisoners — and even a treant sapling — without mercy?
- What does Andreja want from Agares, and what will Madame Wachter ask in return?
- What is the truth behind Frederik's gossip about the Burgomaster's son and Madame Wachter's daughter?
