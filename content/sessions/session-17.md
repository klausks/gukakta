## Summary

Just as [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) forces the party into a deadly duel against [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}})—who represents [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}), one of the forest masters—a Vistani named [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) arrives by carriage and defuses the situation. Paid by a wealthy Vistani to rescue the party, [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) transports them safely through [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) to the Vistani encampment, imparting crucial knowledge about the forest's laws, Vistani culture, and the broader political landscape along the way. The party levels up. Upon arriving at the camp, they meet [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}})—a non-Vistani taken in by [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}})—who declares their destinies are intertwined. The session ends as the party enters [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) tent.

## Events

### The Forced Duel

[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) informs the party they must duel [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}), who represents [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}), one of the forest masters. The terms are stark: if the party loses, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) takes [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}). If the party wins, they go free.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) attempts a diplomatic solution—offering the magical hat to [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) in exchange for [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}) ordering [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) to duel [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) instead. [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) immediately counters this by ordering [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) to suffocate [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}), and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) obeys without hesitation.

### Esdras Arrives

A carriage approaches carrying a Vistani who introduces himself as [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}})—a visibly wealthy, athletic, and well-dressed man. He questions [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) directly, causing him visible discomfort.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asks why the duel must happen. [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) explains the party broke the forest's rules and must duel to appease [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}). [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) acknowledges the rules must be respected, but offers to duel in the party's place. [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}), unwilling to face [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}), withdraws from the duel entirely. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) is visibly annoyed that the fight has been averted; [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) is relieved and thanks [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}).

### The Journey to the Encampment

[Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) invites the party into his fine carriage—a vehicle with two separate cabins—and drives them toward the Vistani encampment. On boarding, the party hears the clanking of metal, which [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) dismisses as his merchandise stock. The mist parts ahead of them as they travel, with [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) noting it will be "gentle" with him. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) notices the path diverges slightly from the direction of the magical golden sword, though not in the opposite direction.

### The Forest Laws

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) asks [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) about the forest's rules. [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) explains what he knows:

- The forest masters despise weakness; refusing a duel is seen as weakness
- One must not interfere in duels
- Duels are fought to the death or until one side surrenders; surrendering means handing over all weapons and accepting defeat
- Winning a duel earns merit, powers, contacts, and rare items found in dungeons—duels serve to invigorate the otherwise dark region
- **The party currently has a price on their heads for breaking the forest's rules**

### Esdras' Wisdom and Observations

During the journey, [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) shares several important insights:

- He questions whether [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) is an ambitious man, noting he has seen three men before wear that same magical hat (the last was named "Leno")
- He believes strongly in luck and misfortune, stating that wealth increases one's luck and that **redheads attract misfortune**
- He advises the party to **trust the blue-eyed old woman**—[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}})—who lives at the encampment
- He warns the party to be cautious at the camp, as **some Vistani there may be spies**
- He notes the party's map is very incomplete and advises them to ask for **Tolmul** when they reach Vallaki, mentioning that [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) sent them
- As a merchant, diplomat, and negotiator, he maintains a **functional relationship with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}})** without being particularly loyal to him. He suggests the party consider giving [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) what he wants, noting he himself has passed some of [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) tests and gained benefits as a result
- He was paid by a Vistani at the encampment—described as "rich in wisdom"—to rescue the party

### Vistani Curse Mechanics

[Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) demonstrates and explains Vistani curses:

- All Vistani can curse, and each Vistani's curse is unique to them
- **Only the Vistani who placed a curse can remove it**; if that Vistani dies, the curse remains permanently
- [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) attempts to curse [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) but fails, stating "luck is not on Pepino's side" and that it was unusual. He asks [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) to choose someone else to receive the curse
- [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) selects [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}). [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) succeeds. He asks [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) to make a small cut with [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) dagger on his arm; [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) immediately notices heightened vulnerability to cuts
- The curse will last approximately 2 nights, given in exchange for a magic item ([Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) had asked [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) for help strengthening the party)

### Rest, Trade, and Leveling Up

The party uses the remainder of the journey to rest, trade items with [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}), and level up. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) asks [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) whether changing his identity could help evade the price on his head. At [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) request, [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) agrees to speak with the forest masters on the party's behalf, framing their rule-breaking as a misunderstanding.

### Arrival at the Encampment — Igga Bevneros

Upon reaching the Vistani encampment, the party is greeted by [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}})—possibly a drow—carrying a lyre and visibly excited. He informs the party that according to [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}), their destinies are intertwined with his. Shortly after, the party enters [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) cabin to learn more about their mission and what the future holds.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party avoided a potentially fatal duel thanks to [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}), but the party still has a price on their heads for breaking the forest's rules
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) is now cursed with vulnerability to cuts for approximately 2 nights in exchange for a magic item
- [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) will attempt to negotiate with the forest masters on the party's behalf, framing their actions as a misunderstanding
- The party has been warned that some Vistani at the camp may be spies
- The party has a new contact lead: **Tolmul** in [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}})
