## Summary

The remaining party completes their business at the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}), hands the staff and green gem over to the Martikovs, and collects their rewards. [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) discovers that the portal is opened by reciting a poem found in the book *Wines of Barovia*. Upon stepping through, the party finds themselves not with [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), but in the attic of the [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}) in Vallaki — where [Viktor Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/viktor-vallakovich" >}}) confronts them and, after some convincing, suggests they wait until nightfall to slip out undetected.

## Key Events

### The Portal's Secret

[Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) determined that the portal at the winery is activated by reciting a poem found in the book *[Wines of Barovia]({{< ref "/lore/wines-of-barovia" >}})*:

> *"Let this wine, sweet and bold,*
> *Warm our hearts and soothe our souls.*
> *With each sip, let us find delight,*
> *And banish all worries with its might."*

### Wrapping Up at the Winery

[Muriel Winchov]({{< ref "/npcs/muriel-winchov" >}}) declared she would keep custody of the incapacitated druid for interrogation and declined Vedas' offer of assistance, stating she had her own methods.

The party was unable to remove the green gem from the druid's staff. The Martikovs agreed to keep the staff and gem until the party could return to retrieve them.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) collected the party's rewards from the winery's main room:

- A **pouch** that appears to be magical, containing an unknown number of platinum pieces
- A **silver dagger**
- A bottle of **Champagne du le Stomp** (a vintage worth up to 200 pp — left with the Martikovs for collection later)
- A **scroll**
- A **strange potion**

[Davian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/davian-martikov" >}}) and the rest of the Martikov family arrived and thanked the party for their work. The party expressed anticipation for the coming partnership. The staff with the green gem was handed over to the Martikovs; wine and staff are to be collected at a later date.

### Into the Portal

The party recited the poem and stepped through the portal. They emerged in the **attic** of the [Baron's Mansion]({{< ref "/locations/baron-mansion-vallaki" >}}) in Vallaki. There was no sign of Igga or Monel.

### Viktor in the Attic

The attic was filled with **magical scrolls, enchantment circles, and dolls**. [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) identified these as ancient occult magic of **Fey origin**.

[Viktor Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/viktor-vallakovich" >}}) appeared and demanded they not touch his things. He was visibly anxious. He initially assumed the party had been sent to capture his father, or had been sent by the Wachter family. After the party explained they had arrived through a portal searching for their companions, Viktor admitted that no one matching their descriptions had appeared in the attic. He suggested they stay hidden until nightfall and then leave quietly.

## Discoveries & Clues

- The portal in the winery basement is opened by reciting the poem from the book *Wines of Barovia*.
- The portal does not lead to a fixed destination — Igga and Monel ended up at Castle Ravenloft, while the rest of the party arrived in the Baron's attic.
- Viktor Vallakovich practices ancient Fey occult magic in secret in the attic of his father's mansion.
- Viktor is wary of the Wachter family and paranoid about his father's safety.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The staff and green gem were left with the Martikovs — to be reclaimed when the party returns.
- The Champagne du le Stomp was also left with the Martikovs for later collection.
- The party is now inside the Baron's mansion in Vallaki without permission.
- Igga and Monel's whereabouts remain unknown to the rest of the party.
