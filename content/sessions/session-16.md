## Summary

Following their survival of a battle, the party continues deeper into [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) in search of the Vistani camp marked on their map. They discover a clearing with a peculiar long tree and a magical sword pointing toward unexplored paths within the forest. Choosing to follow the river instead, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) scouts ahead but disappears into the wilderness. When the party follows his trail, they unexpectedly encounter three familiar figures—[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}), [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}), and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}})—deep within the forest. The encounter raises troubling questions about their true allegiances and knowledge of this cursed land.

## Events

### The Sword and the Clearing

The party reaches a clearing containing a long tree and a magical sword. The sword points toward a path leading deeper into the forest's interior. Unable to discern the sword's purpose or danger, the party chooses to follow the river path instead, deliberately ignoring the sword's direction for the time being.

### Jimmy's Reconnaissance Gone Wrong

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) moves ahead of the group in stealth to scout the area but takes far longer than expected to return. The party grows concerned and decides to follow his trail into the forest, unsure of his fate.

### The Unexpected Encounter

Following [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) path, the party discovers [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}), [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}), and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) deep within [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). The party is shocked to encounter these three away from [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}).

### Discussion of the Forest's Challenges

The party mentions their difficulties in traversing [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}), seeking guidance or sympathy. However, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) responds with a chilling revelation: **he experienced no difficulty whatsoever crossing the forest**. Moreover, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) claims to know everything about traversing [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) and the journey through it.

### The Shadow of Lord Balkor

[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) makes a cryptic reference to **[Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}})**, describing him as a strict enforcer of the forest's rules and laws. The implication is that [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) governs the [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) with an iron fist, making the party wonder about the true nature of their challenges and whether they are random or orchestrated by some greater power.

### The Challenge to Perwimple

[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) makes an intriguing comment that it would be beneficial if someone were to challenge [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) to a duel. The exact meaning and intent behind this suggestion—whether a test, a threat, or something else—remains unclear.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party chose to follow the river path rather than the path indicated by the magical sword, delaying investigation of what the sword might be guiding them toward
- The party is now aware that [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}), [Bildrat]({{< ref "/npcs/bildrat" >}}), and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) are present deep within [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}), which raises questions about their origins and true allegiances
- The revelation that [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) controls the forest's rules introduces a new and powerful entity with unknown intentions toward the party

## Loose Threads

- Who is [Lord Balkor]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) and what are his true intentions toward the party?
- What is [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) ability to navigate [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) so effortlessly, and does it stem from [Lord Balkor's]({{< ref "/npcs/lord-balkor" >}}) influence?
- Why is [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) suggesting that someone challenge [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}})?
- What does the magical sword pointing into the forest's interior represent, and should the party investigate it further?
- What is the nature of the Vistani camp marked on the party's map, and will they reach it?
