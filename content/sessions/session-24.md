## Summary

The mill combat intensifies across multiple floors. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) kills [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) a first time, only for her to melt into the floor with a warning — *"Morgantha is coming."* After [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) kills [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}) on the upper floor, [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) reappears invisible on the ground floor. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) reveals her silhouette with slime, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) ultimately kills her a second and final time outside. Then [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) arrives. With a single clap, she destroys the voodoo dolls, dropping [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) unconscious. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) acts quickly — casting Mending to revive [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and Healing Word to revive [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) — as the session ends mid-combat.

## Events

### The Dolls Take Flight

During the upper-floor combat, [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) let out a scream. The party's voodoo dolls flew violently out of their bags. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) immediately ordered [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to hold the dolls and keep them under control. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) descended from the upper floor to help [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) manage the situation. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) created a containment to secure the dolls.

### Bela Killed — First Time

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) killed [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) on the upper floor. Rather than collapsing, her body melted into the floor, and she announced: *"Morgantha is coming."*

### Offalia Killed

With [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) apparently gone, the party turned focus to [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}) on the upper floor. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) killed [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}).

### Bela Returns Invisible

On the ground floor, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) spotted an invisible figure moving — [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) had reappeared. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) threw slime at the moving presence, making [Bela's]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) silhouette visible.

[Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) fled outside the mill. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) followed and encountered a giant toad outside. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) attacked [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}), and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) delivered the final killing blow, slaying [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) permanently.

The party then focused on the giant toad. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) killed it.

### Morgantha Arrives

A figure appeared outside the mill: **[Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}})**. She clapped her hands. The voodoo dolls split in two — [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) fell unconscious immediately. [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) then put [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to sleep.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) rushed inside to aid their fallen companions. [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast Mending on [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) doll, restoring [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to full HP. [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) then cast Healing Word on [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), reviving him to consciousness.

[Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) attempted to cast Ray of Enfeeblement on [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) — it failed.

The session ended mid-combat.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) was killed twice; she is now permanently dead.
- [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}) was killed by [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}).
- The giant toad outside the mill was killed by [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}).
- [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) is confirmed as a distinct and more powerful entity — not the same as [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}).
- The voodoo dolls were destroyed by [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}); their bearers fell unconscious.
- [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) has been put to sleep by [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}).
