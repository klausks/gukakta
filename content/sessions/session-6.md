## Summary

The party proceeded with the burial rites for the Durst family corpses. Using [Agares']({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) Divine Sense, they located [Mrs. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mrs-durst" >}}) body and retrieved it. During their search, they encountered a deeply evil statue that caused [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) to recoil in horror and [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demon familiar to warn them against touching it. Following the ritual chanting deeper into the basement, they were met by [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) spirit, who thanked them and delivered the repaired music box, suggesting it would be useful for calming spirits in the monster's chamber. The party now stands at the threshold of the final confrontation, debating strategy for both [Walter's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) burial and the impending battle.

## Events

### Burial of Mr. Durst
[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) retrieved [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) corpse and placed it in his tomb. The burial proceeded without complication.

### Convincing the Child Spirits
The spirits of [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) required persuasion to accept the burial rites, but the party successfully convinced them to accept their fates and allow the burials to proceed.

### Divine Sense and the Search
[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) used Divine Sense to locate the remaining bodies in the basement of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}).

### Encounter with the Evil Statue
While traveling south through the basement following [Agares']({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) Divine Sense, the party encountered a statue radiating extreme evil. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was overwhelmed by the malevolence and vomited. [Jimmy Slit's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demon familiar issued a grave warning: touching the statue would result in immediate death ("turning to dust"). [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was persuaded to move on and refocus on locating the bodies.

### Discovery of Mrs. Durst's Body
In a chamber to the south, the party found the body of [Mrs. Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/mrs-durst" >}}). Alongside her were the following treasures:

- 1 purple hooded cloak of excellent quality
- 1 meal kit
- 1 set of thieves' tools
- 1 yellow leather-bound book (claimed by [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}))

### Burial of Mrs. Durst
[Mrs. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mrs-durst" >}}) body was returned to her tomb and properly laid to rest.

### Encounter with Mr. Durst's Spirit
Following the ritual chanting deeper into the southern regions of the basement, [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) spirit appeared before the party. He expressed gratitude for their completion of the burial rites and presented them with [Walter's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) repaired music box, stating it would be effective for "calming spirits" in the chamber where the monster dwells.

### Arrival at the Monster's Chamber
The party reached the door to the monster's inner chamber.

## Decisions & Consequences

### Strategic Debate
Before entering the monster's chamber, the party entered into strategic deliberation about whether to prioritize [Walter Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) burial before engaging the monster.

**Monel's Proposal:** [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) would hold the monster in place while [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) retrieve and bury [Walter's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) body. (Risk: only two party members would be engaging a very powerful creature.)

**Pepino's Proposal:** Play the music box first to determine if it has any effect on the monster before committing to a burial strategy.

### Music Box Handoff
[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) transferred the music box to [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) for potential use. Though [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) suspects the music box may only have an effect on [Mrs. Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/mrs-durst" >}}) or [Margaret]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}) should they appear again, the party is open to experimental use.
