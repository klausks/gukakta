## Summary

After the ambush at the fork in the road, the party survives the confrontation and presses onward through the shifting mist along the [Svalich Road]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-road" >}}).

Vedas performs a ritual to understand the forces at work, perceiving three organized “windows” of evocation. The party continues to update their map and investigate the strange changes around the Aligned Tree(s), collecting a sample of black, poison-like liquid associated with beetles burrowing into the wood.

Along the way, they spot [Vallakian Mercenaries]({{< ref "/npcs/vallakian-mercenaries" >}}) finishing off creatures similar to the ones they fought. With the road growing stranger by the hour, the party follows the route toward a stone plateau and discovers a surprisingly clean [Mysterious Temple]({{< ref "/locations/mysterious-temple" >}}) sealed by a solid ancient door with a new padlock. They shelter inside for the night, establishing a watch order.

## Events

### The Ritual’s “Windows"
Vedas conducts a ritual in the aftermath of the fight. He senses three distinct, organized signatures—described as "windows"—of **evocation**.

### Aligned Tree(s) Changes
The party observes unusual changes in the trees near the Aligned Tree(s). Beetles bore into the wood, and a black liquid is gathered as a potential poison sample.

### Mercenaries on the Road
Wagons of mercenaries are seen finishing off creatures similar to those the party fought. Their armor looks Vallakian in style, and they wear necklaces: a silver, horned-creature symbol set with a gemstone. See: [Horned Silver Pendant]({{< ref "/lore/horned-silver-pendant" >}}).

### The Stone Plateau and the Temple
Following the route onward, the party reaches a stone plateau and discovers a temple/dungeon that appears cleaner than expected.

Vedas’ owl scouts ahead. A solid ancient door blocks deeper access—secured with a **new padlock**.

Inside, the party finds skeletons suggesting varied causes of death.

### Rest and Watch
The party sleeps inside the temple and sets a watch order:

1. Igga
2. Monel
3. Jimmy
4. Vedas
5. Agares

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party takes shelter inside the temple despite the unnerving signs (the new padlock and the dead).
- A sample of the black liquid is preserved for later use or analysis.

## Loose Threads

- What are the three evocation “windows” Vedas perceived, and what do they correspond to?
- Who are the Vallakian mercenaries, and what is the meaning of their horned-creature pendants?
- Why does an ancient temple door have a new padlock—and who placed it?
- What caused the varied deaths of the skeletons within?
