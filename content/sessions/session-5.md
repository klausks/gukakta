## Summary

The party completed their battle against the undead horde in the basement of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}), defeating Margaret's specter, a shadow, a ghoul, and a swarm of centipedes. After the combat, the spirit of [Mr. Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) appeared with crucial instructions: the bodies must be buried in their respective tombs, and the party could safely rest. He confirmed the existence of the monster they seek—his own bastard son. The party conducted a long rest and now must convince the spirits of [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) to accept the burials before preparing for the final confrontation.

## Events

### The Final Undead Combat

The party engaged and defeated the ambushed creatures:
- [Margaret Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}) specter
- A shadow
- A ghoul  
- A swarm of centipedes

### Mr. Durst's Appearance

After the combat concluded, the spirit of [Mr. Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) appeared before the party. He provided critical instructions and information:

- **Burial Directive:** The bodies must be buried in their respective tombs.
- **Sanctuary Confirmed:** He assured the party that the location was safe to rest.
- **Monster Confirmed:** He confirmed that a monster does indeed exist—and revealed it is his bastard son.

### Long Rest

The party conducted a full long rest in the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) basement, fully restoring their health and abilities.

## Decisions & Consequences

The party must now:
1. Convince the spirits of [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) (who remain in the attic) to accept the burial of the remains and to leave their post with [Lancelot]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}).
2. Proceed with burying the bodies in their designated tombs.
3. Prepare for the final confrontation against [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) bastard son.

## Loose Threads

- Identity and location of [Mr. Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-durst" >}}) bastard son—the true monster of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}})
- What role do the tombs play in the story?
- Will [Lancelot]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) be able to leave the attic with the party?
