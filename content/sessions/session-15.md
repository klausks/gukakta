## Summary

The party continues their journey through [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) following the magical sword and a body of water. They consult their map and identify a nearby Vistani camp said to wish them well. As the group travels and shares intimate details about their pasts, they reveal deeply personal stories—[Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) speaks of abandonment and loss, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) confesses to the demonic killing of his family, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) recounts a mysterious war-torn domain from which he inexplicably returned. Their moment of communion is shattered when another armored challenger appears, claiming the party has violated the forest's rules and demanding a duel with [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}). Recognizing the familiar pattern, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) initiates combat immediately without hesitation.

## Events

### Continued Journey and Armor Repairs

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) has examined the armor damaged by [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and now understands what must be done to repair it. The repairs will require two additional short rests to complete, and the outcome may be affected by rolls during the repair process.

### The Conflicted Hat

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) attempts to remove the hat from [Pepino Falstaff's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) head. The hat does not come off easily—it becomes stuck. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) responds with a slap to [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) hand and delivers a sharp warning: "Never try that again."

### Navigation and the Body of Water

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) becomes disoriented while trying to navigate through [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}). [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) suggests that the party simply follow the magical sword, which they do without further complications.

The party eventually hears the sound of a body of water ahead. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) proposes that they approach the water and follow it as a guide. The party agrees and opens their map to better understand their location.

On the map, they identify a point marked with a Vistani encampment. According to [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), this camp contains Vistani who wish the party well. The party also notes that the body of water they discovered corresponds to a location on the map. Observing that the magical sword appears to be floating in a direction that aligns with the Vistani camp, the party decides to follow the sword's trajectory toward that destination.

### Personal Revelations

As the party advances along the riverbank, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asks [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) about his origins.

#### Pepino's Story
[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) reveals that he began his life of adventure out of necessity—driven by the need for money. He is a half-elf orphan who believes he was abandoned by his parents. The loss that shaped his early life came when his sister died while he was still young, leaving him truly alone in the world.

#### Jimmy's Confession
[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) shares a darker truth. His father was deeply troubled—a chronic alcoholic who caused suffering throughout the household. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) reveals that he was responsible for killing his entire family. However, he survived by confronting his father directly, aided by "the being that exists within me"—a reference to the demonic entity bound to him through the cursed talisman he stole in his youth.

#### Monel's Burden
[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) opens up about his own past. He was born into a good family without major hardship. His father was a master brewer, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) spent much of his youth drinking and doing foolish things. At some point in his life, he enlisted in the military and adopted a peaceful, comfortable lifestyle.

However, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) then reveals a profound mystery: he once ended up in a domain consumed by war. He spent what felt like an infinite number of years in that domain, yet he never understood what was happening or discovered any explanation for his presence there. As suddenly as he was transported to that war-torn land, he was returned to the normal plane. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) asks [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) why this transportation occurred, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) responds with frustration—he never understood this episode and has no explanation for it.

### Moment of Comfort

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) offers wine to the group in an attempt to comfort them after their difficult conversations. She also mentions that the cloak provided to her offers protection—a detail the party notes.

### The Second Challenge

As the group shares this moment, they are suddenly confronted by a large armored man. The man demands that [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) engage in a duel, claiming that the party has violated the forest's rules—the same claim made by the two furry creatures in the previous encounter.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) immediately recognizes the similarity between this challenger's speech and the words of the first ambush. Having already learned that these "forest rules" are a deception, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) does not hesitate. He charges directly at the large armored man—identified as Hoge—and initiates combat without waiting for further dialogue.

## Decisions & Consequences

- **Armor Repairs**: [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) will require two short rests to repair his damaged armor
- **Hat Protection**: The hat appears to have protective properties that prevent removal; [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) sets a firm boundary with [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}})
- **Navigation**: Following the magical sword has proven more reliable than attempting to navigate the forest manually
- **Destination**: The party is moving toward a Vistani encampment marked on their map
- **Combat Initiative**: [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) quick recognition of the ambush pattern and immediate combat action prevented another false duel situation

## Loose Threads

- What is the true identity and purpose of Hoge, the armored challenger?
- Why does [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) use false duel challenges to ambush travelers?
- What protection does [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) cloak provide, and from what dangers specifically?
- What is the nature of [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) mysterious time in the war domain?
- How will the party's encounter with the Vistani camp unfold?
