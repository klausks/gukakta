## Summary

In [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party fails to negotiate an alternative to Jimmy's punishment: in this town, bail is treated as bribery. Jimmy is taken away by the guards under a black hood and brought before Izek and the Baron.

In private, Jimmy is questioned by [Izek]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) and [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) in a torture room. Meanwhile, the rest of the party returns to the Blue Water Inn and meets a traveling bard, [Rictavio]({{< ref "/npcs/rictavio" >}}), who warns that the party is being talked about in town and says [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) wants to speak with them.

Urwin reveals what happened at his family's winery: three magical gems once empowered the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}), but all three have been taken over time—one long ago, and two within the last month. With the vineyard overrun by blights, Urwin asks the party to help clear the winery, offering a reward and a letter of introduction to his father, [Davian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/davian-martikov" >}}).

By the end of the night, the party chooses a group name—**Gukakta**—and makes plans to meet Vallaki's nobles, respond to Urwin's request, and locate Jimmy.

## Events

### Jimmy Taken

The party tries to argue for a different punishment, but learns that bail is seen as bribery in Vallaki. Jimmy is hooded and marched away through quieter streets. When he protests his innocence, Izek strikes him with his unnatural red arm; the blow burns worse than it should.

### Before the Baron

Jimmy is brought to the Baron, who expresses displeasure at meeting under these circumstances and questions him while Jimmy is forced to kneel. The Baron suggests that outsiders are likely connected to "the Devil," but insists that festivals protect the town and that the party may still be useful.

Jimmy claims the group is fleeing the Devil; the Baron reacts angrily, then calms and asks which local nobles the party has spoken to. Jimmy mentions Vasili, Andreja, and Frederik the tailor. Jimmy is searched again and placed in a solitary cell; later he is given food and water, and Izek eventually removes his restraints.

### A Bard at the Inn

Outside the Blue Water Inn, a man tells a story about once owning a monkey. Inside, Rictavio introduces himself as a neighbor, says the party is being mentioned around town, and repeats that Urwin wants to speak with them. He says Urwin has left them a paid round. Later, Rictavio sings about the "mad mage." See [Mad Mage]({{< ref "/lore/mad-mage" >}}).

Danika serves the party a generous dinner of wolf meat. The children running through the inn are revealed to be Danika and Urwin's two sons.

### Urwin's Details

Urwin explains that the winery is magical: three gems were once set beneath it to make the magic work. The first gem was stolen around ten years ago. The second was taken about three weeks ago without signs of a struggle or witnesses. The third was taken about five days ago—this time with fighting, but the family could not hold.

With the vineyard choked by blights and plant growth, the family was forced to abandon the winery. Urwin asks the party to help clear it; he offers **100 gp** and a bottle of **Champagne Du le Stomp** (aged ten years, only made when all three gems were present). He also offers a letter of recommendation to introduce the party to Davian.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party adopts a group name: **Gukakta**.
- The party plans to speak to Urwin the next day about departing for the winery mission (and whether they need payment in advance).
- The party plans to pursue a meeting with the Baron (via his butler) after returning from the winery, with a festival expected in six days.

## Loose Threads

- Where are the winery's three gems now, and who took them?
- What does the Baron intend to do with the party once he "introduces" them to Vallaki?
- What will happen to Jimmy if Vallaki decides he is useful—or dangerous?
