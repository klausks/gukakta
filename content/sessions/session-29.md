## Summary

The party enters [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) and heads straight for the Blue Water Inn. In their rooms, they open [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) letter and discover it magically reveals instructions only when [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) places her hand on a handprint inside. The message directs them to present Ireena to [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}). To avoid Vallaki's strict enforcement, the group hides any documents that mention [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) (including the tome) under a loose floorboard, repaired afterward with magic.

The innkeeper, [Danika Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/danika-martikov" >}}), warns them not to meet the Burgomaster poorly dressed and offers guidance on local services. She also says her husband, [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}), wants to speak to the party privately that night. Meanwhile, [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) connects to his strange hat and hears a whisper promising the delivery of the floating sword — the voice claims to be nearby, at a camp outside the town.

## Events

### Through Vallaki's Streets

At the city entrance, the party sees many spears topped with wolf heads and trampled flyers on the ground. The flyers advertise a "Festival of War Against the Wolf," which has apparently already taken place. Vallaki feels alive and crowded, but townsfolk keep their distance; an elderly man shuts his door and shouts that he is not inviting anyone inside, followed by several other doors slamming shut nearby.

### The Blue Water Inn

The party finds the Blue Water Inn in a commercial district where many buildings have a shop on the first floor and living quarters above. Danika Martikov receives them as innkeeper.

### The Letter and the Hidden Cache

In the rooms, the party opens Ismark's letter. Inside is a handprint the size of Ireena's palm; when she places her hand on it, writing appears.

The message instructs the party to bring Ireena to Walpole. Ireena recognizes the name and suspects Walpole may be someone trustworthy — possibly involved in paying the promised gold.

A new festival flyer slides under the room door. The party finds a loose plank beneath a rug that is suitable for hiding sensitive documents and the tome. After hiding the material, [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) uses mending magic to repair the plank.

The party stores their belongings in a chest and splits the room keys: [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) keeps one key, Igga keeps the other, and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) keeps the key to a second room.

### Lunch and Local Advice

Downstairs, Igga buys beer for everyone in the tavern.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) asks about merchants and the orphanage. Danika says Vallaki has specialists of many kinds, including medicine, archery, smithing ("Wahlem's Hammer"), potions and alchemy, toys, carpentry, tailoring (a noble tailor named [Frederik]({{< ref "/npcs/frederik" >}}) who is known as a gossip), and leatherworking. She also suggests a seller of historical books.

Danika states that the town has a church that has protected Vallaki for years, devoted to the "Lord of the Morning," with a patron saint she names as "Undrow" (name uncertain).

Jimmy asks about a barber and receives a recommendation: [Mr. Urko]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-urko" >}}).

### A Private Meeting Requested

Danika says her husband, Urwin Martikov, wants to meet the party privately that night. She remains at the bar while Urwin usually stays in the kitchen.

### Pepino's Whispered Promise

Later, Pepino goes upstairs to connect with his hat. He hears whispers: "I'm close, okay? I'm going to deliver the sword to you." When Pepino asks if the speaker is in Vallaki, the voice says it is nearby — at a camp on the outskirts.

### Guards at the Inn

Town guards enter the inn and search Agares, but find nothing.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party hid or discarded written references to Strahd before fully settling in Vallaki.
- The party agreed (through Igga) to speak with Urwin Martikov privately later that night.
- The party plans to present Ireena to Walpole, per the letter's revealed instructions.

## Loose Threads

- Who is Walpole, and what role will he play in protecting Ireena or paying the party?
- Who (or what) is the voice speaking to Pepino through the hat, and where is the nearby camp?
- What is the new festival flyer that slipped under the door?
- Why do Vallakians react with fear to strangers and refuse to invite them inside?
