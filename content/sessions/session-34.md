## Summary

At the Blue Water Inn in Vallaki, Pepino spends the night reading a large portion of the tome. Jimmy is released from prison the next morning and rejoins the party at the inn.

After breakfast, the party speaks with Urwin, receives 100 gp and directions to the Wizard of Wines Winery, and is warned not to use anything involving fire at the winery. The group then splits to handle errands for the day, with Ireena accompanying Agares and Igga to the tailor, while Monel updates Jimmy and Pepino on the party's current plans and decisions.
