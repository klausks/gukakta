## Summary

The party explored the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}), discovering valuable items and dark family secrets. They learned of the Durst family's tragic fate, including the discovery that their own child Walter was the "last sacrifice" made by the parents. They encountered and defeated [Margaret]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}), Walter's spectral caretaker, but were unable to repair his music box — an object she desperately wanted to hear.

## Events

### Exploration and Discoveries

As the party explored the rooms of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}):

- **[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}})** picked up a piece of armor, immediately felt ill, and vomited. Both [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) asked him to drop it.

### The Mirror and the Names

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) found a broken mirror in a small room. Behind it were written two names:
- **Elizabeth Durst**
- **Rosalva Durst** (with her name crossed out)

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) informed the rest of the party of this discovery and kept the mirror in his backpack.

### Relics and Documents Discovered

The party recovered several items from the house:

- **3 Scrolls** (written in Common): *Blessing*, *Protection Against Poison*, and *Spiritual Weapon* — all kept by [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}).
- **2 Deeds**: One for the house, one for a windmill.
- **A Will**: Names of all people mentioned are crossed out except **Elizabeth Durst**. The heirs section is completely scribbled out and illegible.
- **3 Leather-bound Books**: No writing on the covers.

### Encounter with Margaret

The party encountered [Margaret]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}), the spectral nurse who had cared for [Walter Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}). She was distressed that his music box — an object she desperately wanted him to hear — was broken and non-functional. The party defeated the spirit in combat.

Before her defeat, Margaret revealed that [Walter Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) was the "last sacrifice" made by the Durst couple — their own son.

### Ongoing Investigation

The party believes it would be worth:
- Investigating other rooms in the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) in the same manner as before.
- Attempting to repair [Walter Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) music box.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) secretly pocketed valuable gems.
- The party defeated [Margaret]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}) but were unable to restore her peace by repairing the music box.
- The party learned of the Durst family's horrific secret: they sacrificed their own son.

## Loose Threads

- What is the purpose of the music box, and what will happen if it is repaired?
- What is the significance of [Elizabeth Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/elizabeth-durst" >}})'s name on the will and the mirror?
- Why was [Rosalva Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) name crossed out on the mirror?
- Are there other spirits in the house, and what are their stories?
- What is the windmill deed for?

## Rewards

- Defeated [Margaret]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}) (spirit)
- Acquired valuable gems, scrolls, deeds, and other documents
