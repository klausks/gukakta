## Summary

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) meets alone with [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) in the crypt of [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}), where the first stage of an exorcism is carried out. The demon is violently expelled — at great personal cost — and escapes loose into Vallaki. Jimmy reunites with the party on the road and briefs them. The group then keeps their scheduled breakfast meeting with [Baron Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}), gifting him wine and receiving a mission: investigate [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}).

## Key Events

### The Diagnosis

Father Lucian described a plan to expel Jimmy's demon in two stages. The first step is the **Iron Vigil** — a rite to be performed in the crypt of Saint Andral, in proximity to the saint's mortal remains. This would culminate in a direct confrontation with the demon. Jimmy described his urgency: the demon had to be expelled soon, or both he and others would die.

Sensing Jimmy's extreme vulnerability to the entity inside him, Father Lucian threw holy water over Jimmy's head. The effect was immediate: Jimmy's **right hand** — the one the demon had used to extract memories from people through touch — went numb, then blazed with pain. Jimmy began having visions of the hand rotting and turning gangrenous.

Father Lucian explained that the demon's main points of residence in Jimmy's body were the **head** and the **right hand**, but the holy water had driven it further into the hand. He stated plainly: *"The demon is stronger than I had anticipated. He will not leave without causing you one final pain."*

### The Severing

Father Lucian suggested that Jimmy sever the bond by removing the hand entirely. He spoke calmly and with gravity. The saint's remains were close. When he saw Jimmy's resolve waver, the priest demanded: *"Do you repent of your sins? If so — expel what no longer belongs to you!"*

Jimmy cut off his own right hand.

The severed hand swelled rapidly, then began to decay. From the decay, a **dark creature emerged as black smoke**, partially coalescing into a form that can only be described as somewhere between a demon and an aberration. It did not fully take shape — but what could be seen was terrifying. It stared at Jimmy as if the situation had spiralled beyond its own control, then escaped through the crypt gate, **wrenching it from its axis** as it fled.

Jimmy briefly lost consciousness.

### Aftermath

When Jimmy came to, his wrist was barely bleeding — **magically sealed**, as if the wound had been closed by something other than medicine. No trace of the severed hand remained on the floor of the crypt.

Father Lucian observed that Jimmy's body and soul had fared better than expected given what had just transpired. He explained that the soul and body are interconnected — *"like knitted cloth, threaded together"* — and that the demon had been partially lodged in both. Jimmy's body remained exhausted and tense, a state that would persist until a long rest.

The air in the crypt felt different to Jimmy for the first time: **empty of observation**. A constant subliminal sense of being watched — something Jimmy had grown so accustomed to that he barely noticed it — was gone.

Father Lucian informed Jimmy that the Iron Vigil would continue for the coming days to clear any residual corruption the creature had left behind.

### Father Lucian's Words

Looking directly at Jimmy without blinking, Father Lucian spoke:

> *"Unlike other brothers, you were forged to actively fight evil. Defeat evil one day at a time, until your last. At the end of your life — sooner or later — you will find the answer you seek about your existence. There is no easy way to repay a liberation from evil, except to perpetuate more liberation out there. Your allies will need you in this fight. There are great evils out there — and in here. Begin by hunting the creature that became entangled with your destiny. And do not forget to come to the feast of Saint Andral — we will need help to organize and celebrate. I hope that on your final day, you realize the good you have done by fighting daily against the evils of life."*

He then added, unprompted, that there is an abbot at the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) who fell — was corrupted — but that there are **rumors he can still perform good**: including resurrection, and in cases like Jimmy's, the **restoration of lost limbs**, for those he chooses to help. Father Lucian heard this from adventurers who had returned from the abbey years ago. He has not seen those adventurers since.

### Reuniting with the Party

After leaving the church, Jimmy headed toward the [Blue Water Inn]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) to regroup with the party. He encountered them en route. He described what had happened in the crypt: the exorcism, the severing of his hand, the demon's partial manifestation and escape. He told the group the demon was still loose somewhere in Vallaki. He outlined his immediate priorities: restore his hand, and track and kill the demon once and for all.

### Meeting with Baron Vallakovich

The party kept their previously arranged breakfast meeting with [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}). They brought him a bottle of **Champagne Du le Stomp** as a gift, earned from the Martikov winery mission.

The Baron tasked the party with **investigating [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}})**, his main political opponent in Vallaki.

During the meeting, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) spotted **[Majesto]({{< ref "/creatures/majesto" >}})** — Fiona Wachter's invisible imp familiar — peering in through a window. The imp was not acknowledged by anyone else.

## Discoveries & Clues

- Majesto, Fiona Wachter's invisible imp, was observed watching the party's meeting with the Baron through a window — suggesting Fiona already knows the Baron has tasked the party with investigating her.
- The demon has been **expelled** from Jimmy's body and is now **loose**, having escaped through St. Andral's Church crypt gate.
- The creature appeared as black smoke coalescing into a partial form — a mix of aberration and demon. It was not fully manifested but was already strong and needed only to **regenerate** the rest of its strength.
- The demon's primary anchor points in Jimmy's body were his head and right hand.
- The crypt gate at St. Andral's Church is now **broken from its axis**.
- Jimmy no longer feels the constant subliminal sense of being observed that had accompanied the demon's presence.
- Father Lucian stated the demon was stronger than anticipated.
- Father Lucian mentioned that [The Abbot]({{< ref "/npcs/the-abbot" >}}) of the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) — though fallen and corrupted — is rumored to still be capable of good works, including **resurrection** and the **restoration of lost limbs** for those he favors. Father Lucian heard this from adventurers who visited the abbey years ago and never returned.

## Decisions & Consequences

- Jimmy sacrificed his right hand to expel the demon.
- The demon is now free in Vallaki, partially manifested, and actively regenerating.
- Jimmy will undergo the Iron Vigil rite for several days to clear residual corruption.
- Jimmy's right hand — which could extract memories from people through touch — is gone.
- The party has accepted a mission from the Baron to investigate Fiona Wachter.
- Majesto witnessing the meeting means Fiona Wachter may already be aware she is being investigated.
