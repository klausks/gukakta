## Summary

The party woke well-rested and began their first full day in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). After breakfast and wine offered by their mysterious benefactor ([Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}})—the local burgomaster's son—the party learned of the village's true predicament. Ruled by the vampire [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), Barovia is cursed and trapped. Ismark's father was recently killed by wolves and requires burial. More critically, [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) has taken a personal interest in [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) sister [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), prompting him to request the party's aid in escorting her to safety. Before agreeing to any mission, the party sought to meet a merchant for item valuation and gather intelligence about the village.

## Events

### Morning in the Inn
[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) both experienced restful, pleasant dreams and woke feeling "Well Rested." [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) proved to be the sleepiest of the group and required waking.

### Meeting Arik
In the inn's common hall, the party became acquainted with **Arik**, the tavern's primary staff member who handles all operations. Arik is not the owner but manages the day-to-day affairs of the establishment.

### The Benefactor Revealed
The mysterious man who paid for the party's stay is revealed to be **[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}})**, son of the local burgomaster. He provided the party with:
- Breakfast
- Glasses of wine

### Ismark's Plea
[Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) shared critical information about the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) and its dire circumstances:

- **His Father's Death:** [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) father died recently in an attack by wolves and requires proper burial.
- **The Curse:** [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) is a cursed land, permanently trapped and ruled by [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), a vampire lord.
- **The Threat to Ireena:** [Count Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) has expressed a personal, obsessive interest in [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) sister, [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}).
- **The Request:** Fearing for [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) life and liberty, [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) formally requested that the party escort [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) to safety outside of [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}).

### Strategic Deliberation
The party agreed to help [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) but insisted on first:
- Gathering more information about the village and [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}})
- Meeting with a merchant to properly value their recovered items
- Assisting with the burial of [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) father
- Getting to know the village better before committing to the escort mission

### Meeting the Priest
Before accompanying [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) to transport his father's body to the burial site, the party encountered **[Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}})**, the village priest. [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}) was assisting [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) with the funeral arrangements.

### Merchant Visit
The party visited a merchant in the village to seek proper valuation of their recovered items, including:
- The purple hooded cloak of excellent quality
- The meal kit
- The set of thieves' tools
- The yellow leather-bound book
- Other treasures from the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}})

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party accepted [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) request to help escort [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) but prioritized gathering information.
- They agreed to assist with the burial of [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) father as part of their commitment to helping the village.
- The party chose not to immediately depart the village, instead choosing to establish themselves and understand the local situation.
