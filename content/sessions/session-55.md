## Summary

As the party continues pulling on the threads around [Wachterhaus]({{< ref "/locations/wachterhaus" >}}), [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}}) seem to realize they are being watched. An imp appears and speaks cryptically about **"invoking the truth"** and the **Blade of Truth**, reinforcing the sense that the Wachter household is aware of the party’s pressure.

The group then pivots back to the crisis at [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}). [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) explains that [St. Andral]({{< ref "/lore/st-andral" >}}) once drove Strahd from the sanctuary through faith, and that while the saint's protection remains in that holy place, those within are spared from evil. Following Milivoj’s lead, the party confronts [Henrich Van der Voort]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}), recovers the stolen bones, and discovers that five supposed corpses in the attic were in fact armed vampires waiting in ambush.

## Events

### Wachter Suspicion

- [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}}) appear to know the party is spying on them.
- An imp manifests and speaks about **"invoking the truth"** and the **Blade of Truth**.
- Jimmy notes that the five chairs around the pentagram in the secret room at Wachterhaus match the number of coffins tied to the church plot, but this remains only a suspicion.

### Questions for Father Lucian

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) question [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) about [St. Andral]({{< ref "/lore/st-andral" >}}) and Vallaki’s politics.

- Lucian explains that guards who had harmed Strahd’s forces once took refuge in St. Andral’s sanctuary.
- The Devil came to take revenge but was expelled.
- On Strahd’s third visit, St. Andral drove him back through the force of faith, and Lucian says that while the saint remains in that sanctuary, those inside remain free from evil.
- When asked whether [Baron Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) is truly a man of faith, Lucian answers plainly that he is not.
- Agares also asks about [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}) and whether [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) has been frequenting the church. Lucian mentions only a single occurrence.

### Milivoj's Lead and Henrich's House

[Milivoj]({{< ref "/npcs/milivoj" >}}) finds the party and confirms the arrangement to receive the coffins still stands. He gives directions to Henrich’s home, allowing the group to move on the final known link in the theft.

[Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) charms [Henrich Van der Voort]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}) and questions him. Henrich says there is nothing magical in the house, allows the party inside, and eventually reveals the key points of the plot:

- Vistani paid him **25 gp** for the coffins and burial arrangement.
- The same buyers offered **50 gp** for **St. Andral’s crown**.
- His description of the Vistani contact matches [Jimmy Slit’s]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) impression of [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}), though this is not confirmed.

Under the charm’s influence, Henrich shows the party where the stolen bones are hidden, and Jimmy places them in his backpack.

### The Coffins Open

[Kiki]({{< ref "/npcs/kiki" >}}) reaches the group but fails to communicate anything urgent clearly. Vedas decides the group has what it needs and could leave, but Jimmy insists on checking the bodies in the coffins. Vedas calls the rest of the party as a precaution through [Isha]({{< ref "/creatures/isha" >}}).

Henrich leads them to the attic. As the inspection begins, all five coffins erupt open. Instead of corpses, each contains a blood-hungry vampire:

- a blond vampire with a longsword
- a central vampire with a warhammer
- a woman with a dagger
- a distant man with a bow
- an old vampire with no weapon

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party recovers the stolen bones of [St. Andral]({{< ref "/lore/st-andral" >}}), though they are not yet returned to the church in these notes.
- Henrich is confirmed as more than a passive fence: he knowingly stored the relic and hosted the five coffins.
- The St. Andral investigation now points toward a Vistani buyer whose description resembles [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}), but this remains suspicion rather than proof.
- The attic ambush reveals that the coffin plot was an immediate combat threat, not just a burial arrangement.

## Loose Threads

- Was the Vistani contact truly [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}), or someone resembling him?
- What is the connection, if any, between the five coffins and the five-point ritual chamber in [Wachterhaus]({{< ref "/locations/wachterhaus" >}})?
- What did the imp mean by **"invoking the truth"** and the **Blade of Truth**?
- When will the recovered bones be returned to [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}})?
- Who placed the five vampires in Henrich’s attic, and what was their exact role in the larger plan?

## Rewards

- The stolen bones of [St. Andral]({{< ref "/lore/st-andral" >}}) were recovered from Henrich’s house.
