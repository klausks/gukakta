## Summary

In [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party splits errands: [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) head to [Androm]({{< ref "/npcs/androm" >}})’s bookshop to dig deeper, while [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}), [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}), and [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) hunt for distinctive rings and prepare for a visit to [Wachterhaus]({{< ref "/locations/wachterhaus" >}}) and [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}).

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) returns with news from [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}): beneath the altar at St. Andral’s Church lies the (hidden) crypt of [St. Andral]({{< ref "/lore/st-andral" >}}), and a feast in the saint’s honor will be held on the same day as [Baron Vargas Vallakovich’s]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) public event. Lucian appears confident things will be fine, and Ireena seems to be starting to accept the Baron’s assurances.

At [Frederik’s]({{< ref "/npcs/frederik" >}}) shop, the group runs into an uneasy [Andreja]({{< ref "/npcs/andreja" >}}) and meets a striking noblewoman named [Rosemary]({{< ref "/npcs/rosemary" >}}), who points them toward [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) as a source for fine rings. Vasili trades a pristine platinum ring for two secrets—one about Igga’s past and one about the party’s work in [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}})—and returns Agares’ money, satisfied.

## Events

### Splitting Errands
Vedas and Monel go to the bookshop to research further. Meanwhile, Igga, Agares, and Jimmy set out to find unusual rings and then head toward Wachterhaus.

### Ireena’s Church Intelligence
Ireena shares what she learned from Father Lucian:

- Under the altar is the crypt of St. Andral, who “faced evil and won,” but the crypt is not visible.
- A feast for St. Andral will occur on the same day as the Baron’s public event.
- Lucian’s sister, [Lydia Petrovna]({{< ref "/npcs/lydia-petrovna" >}}), is married to the Baron and is deeply religious.

### Frederik’s Shop and a New Noble
The party returns to Frederik’s shop to recover Agares’ armor and shop for platinum rings. Guards whisper as they pass, but do not stop them.

Andreja is present and seems unsettled, but Igga chooses not to press her. Rosemary, a red-haired noblewoman being measured, treats Andreja and Frederik with visible superiority and expresses curiosity about Agares’ magic. She suggests Vasili may be able to help with the ring search.

### Secrets for a Ring
Vasili provides a pristine platinum ring in exchange for two secrets:

- Igga once worshipped [Lolth]({{< ref "/lore/lolth" >}}); after killing in her name, he was imprisoned by [Duergar]({{< ref "/creatures/duergar" >}}) and bears scars from that captivity.
- Jimmy and the group entered the Durst family’s manor and put their spirits to rest.

Vasili returns Agares’ payment and gives them the ring, satisfied with the information.

### Remembering the Dursts
Jimmy recounts details about the Durst family and their estate:

- The Dursts were a noble family in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}).
- [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}) was visible from the manor.
- A cult operated inside the house.
- The party found letters from [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) there.
- The house’s fall did not seem very old.

Jimmy finds it unsettling that Vasili appears to know about the Dursts.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party agrees to trade secrets to secure a pristine platinum ring.
- Igga chooses not to probe Andreja’s apparent distress.

## Loose Threads

- Why is St. Andral’s crypt hidden beneath the altar, and what does it mean that it is not visible?
- Why did Vasili want these specific secrets, and how did he learn about the Dursts?
- What has unsettled Andreja?

## Rewards

- A pristine platinum ring, obtained from Vasili in exchange for secrets.
