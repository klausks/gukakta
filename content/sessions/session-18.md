## Summary

The party's session continues from within [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) tent, where she reveals the party's destiny through a five-card Tarot reading that lays out the path ahead. After private consultations with each party member (none of whom recall what was said), the group joins [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) and the Vistani at the campfire for a night of celebration. [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) sells the party two magical items before revealing that [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) will be challenged to a duel the following morning. The session ends with the group deliberating on dueling pairs and combat strategy.

## Events

### The Tarot Reading

[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) shuffles her Tarot deck and presents the party's fate through five cards. The order in which the cards were drawn is likely the order in which events will unfold. For the full details of each card, see the [Tarot Reading]({{< ref "/lore/tarot-reading" >}}) lore page.

After the reading, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asks which order they should follow. [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) says there is no prescribed order, but that they will likely follow the sequence in which the cards were revealed.

### Private Consultations

[Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) requests to speak with each party member individually. Every member has the same experience: they enter the tent, fall asleep, and wake up lying on the table surrounded by Tarot cards. No one remembers what was discussed, but all feel a deep sense of calm and ease afterward.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) is unsettled by the memory loss and returns to speak with [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) again. She reassures him that he should not worry about the uncertainty—when the time comes to act, the right action will come to him instinctively. She also reveals that [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), like [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), possesses a bond with something greater than himself.

### Evening Celebration

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) is found by the campfire, drinking and visibly happy—relaxed and carefree in a way the party hasn't seen before. She pulls [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) into a dance. The sight brings the party a sense of relief.

[Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) encourages the group to celebrate, then offers to show them his wares:

- **[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}})** purchases a **scimitar that glows in the dark**
- **[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}})** purchases a **hammer that converts Divine Smite damage into healing for a chosen ally**

### Conversation About the Cards

[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) asks about the meeting with [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}). The party shares what they know about the cards, noting that nothing specific about [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) was mentioned. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) expresses a desire to speak with [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) privately, but [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) is unable to summon her. They agree to speak with her the following day. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asks [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) to also inquire specifically about [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) role in their shared destiny.

### The Mill on the Path to Vallaki

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asks [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) whether he recognizes any of the places mentioned in the Tarot reading. [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) confirms he knows the **mill** referenced in the first card, and that it lies along the road to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

### The Duel Announcement

At the end of the celebration, [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) pulls the party aside to deliver important news: **[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) will be challenged to a duel the following morning**. He adds that the duels will likely be in pairs, with two separate bouts. Winning these duels would elevate the party to the rank of **forest masters**, restoring their honor and erasing the price on their heads.

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) explains that in paired duels, teams typically consist of a **primary combatant** and a **support combatant**, with the support role often being filled by a healer.

### Combat Strategy Planning

The party ends the session discussing dueling pairs and strategy. Current considerations:

- **Pair 1:** [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}})
- **Pair 2:** [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) with either [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) or [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}})

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party has a map of their likely destiny via the Tarot; their path will likely take them to a mill, a dragon's house, a small castle under a mountain, an abbey near the mists, and ultimately to [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}})
- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) has not yet spoken privately with [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}); this is deferred to the next day, with the added goal of asking about [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) destiny
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) learned that [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) also has a bond with something beyond the mortal realm
