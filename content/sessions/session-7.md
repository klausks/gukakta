## Summary

The party achieved victory over [The Durst Monster]({{< ref "/monsters/durst-monster" >}}). With the creature vanquished and all family bodies properly buried, the haunted magic infusing the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) dissipated utterly. The oppressive fog that had surrounded the house lifted. Exhausted and disoriented, the party emerged into the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), discovering a settlement of decadent, shuttered buildings. They obtained food, lodging, and rest for the night, with a mysterious benefactor paying for their stay. The party reached **Level 3**.

## Events

### The Final Battle
The party engaged [The Durst Monster]({{< ref "/monsters/durst-monster" >}}) in direct combat and emerged victorious.

### The Curse Lifted
With the monster defeated and all the Durst family bodies properly buried in their respective tombs, the haunted magic binding the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) dissipated. The oppressive fog surrounding the house lifted, revealing a path to the outside world.

### Emergence into the Village of Barovia
Exhausted from their ordeal, the party stepped out of the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}) and found themselves in the [Village of Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). The village presented a stark contrast to the house: decadent buildings, many shuttered and closed. The overall atmosphere was one of decay and abandonment.

### The Pie Vendor
The party encountered an **old woman selling pies** in the village streets. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) purchased 4 pieces and consumed 1. The pie was exceptional in quality and the consumption granted [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) a temporary increase in health. When the party inquired about lodging, the old woman directed them to a local inn.

### The Inn
Upon arriving at the inn, the party made direct requests for lodging and bathing supplies. A **man at the inn** (possibly another patron or benefactor) offered to pay for the party's entire stay to the innkeeper. 

In their room, the party received:
- A bucket with water
- Cloth for washing and cleaning

All four party members slept comfortably in the same room, each in their own bed.

## Decisions & Consequences

The party chose to accept the offer of lodging and rest after their harrowing ordeal, prioritizing recovery over immediate investigation of the village or the mysterious benefactor.
