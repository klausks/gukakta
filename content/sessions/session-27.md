## Summary

In the aftermath of [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) visit, the party is attacked by four wolves. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) interrupts his risky connection to the mystical tome to help in the fight. During combat, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) feels [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) watching from a distance on his flaming horse. As the wolves are defeated, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) shouts a challenge into the forest, vowing to destroy [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}). Suddenly, [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) enters a trance-like state and runs into the woods, declaring she will go to the castle with [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}). [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) stops her, and the trance ends, leaving her exhausted. Among the wolves' bodies, the party finds a gold ring wrapped in a letter written in Elvish by a tragic figure named [Hianag Darastrix]({{< ref "/npcs/hianag-darastrix" >}}), revealing deep lore involving Drow, a Dracolich, and centuries of servitude under [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}). Frustrated by a mocking addendum from the vampire lord, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) throws the gold ring away into the dark forest, and the party fails to find it.

## Events

### Wolf Ambush and Strahd's Watchful Eye

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) lay resting, feverish. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) continued attempting to connect with [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) tome, trembling from the magical strain as [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) attempted to stabilize him. 

Suddenly, the party realized they were surrounded by four wolves preparing to strike. Realizing the danger, [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) understood that a proper connection to the tome would require more dedicated time. He made a successful Intelligence saving throw to force himself out of the trance to assist the group.

The party engaged and defeated the four wolves. Throughout the fight, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) had the distinct impression that [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) was watching them from the background, still mounted on his illusory horse of fire. 

### Agares's Defiance and Ireena's Trance

Once the combat concluded, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) shouted a bold challenge, his voice echoing fiercely through the dark trees: *"Lord Strahd!!! My name is Agares Ariel, and I will destroy you!"*

Immediately after, [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) exhibited bizarre behavior. Appearing hypnotized and falling into a sudden trance, she bolted toward the deep forest. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) intercepted and restrained her. As he did, she shouted, *"Strahd, I will go with you to the castle!"* 

The trance broke abruptly. Disoriented and complaining of profound exhaustion, [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) was guided back to her tent by [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to rest.

### The Letter from Hianag Darastrix

Near the bodies of the slain wolves, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) found a gold ring wrapped in a parchment. The letter, hastily written in Elvish, revealed a tragic tale:

> *"Greetings, noble warrior. If you found this ring, it means I am dead and you are either born here or trapped in this land. Let me start from the beginning. My name is [Hianag Darastrix]({{< ref "/npcs/hianag-darastrix" >}}), a high elf born on the green island. Though born noble, I lived my entire life as a slave. As a child, I was captured and enslaved by a Black Dragon named Nithhogr Nightwing.*
>
> *Many decades later, a drow patrol rescued me from the dragon's lair. For the next century, I served as a slave of house Do'Urden, deep in the City of Spiders. My life in the underground of Menzoberranzan was as terrible as my stay with Nithhogr. Among other tasks, I was tasked with helping priestesses give birth, and assisting the matron mother in the sacrifice of male babies. My sanity was kept intact by the kindness of a drow—a male, ironically—who barely survived his own birth. His name was Drizzt Do'Urden, and he secretly taught me the way of the drow warrior. When the time came for me to be sacrificed to the spider queen, Drizzt pleaded on my behalf, pointing out my skills as a warrior to his mother, Matron Malice.*
>
> *I was spared that day, only to be sent days later to kill Nithhogr, alone. A death sentence. After 25 days of travel, as I approached my old prison, I realized something was wrong. I entered the lair and found the ancient Dragon already dead, its immense carcass completely rotted. I cannot describe what I felt. I thought I was finally free after 150 years of slavery, that I could finally see my people, my family. Leave the Underdark. Be the solar elf I was born to be.*
>
> *My joy lasted only seconds, interrupted by the sound of breaking bones. The massive winged skeleton rose before my eyes. I fought Nightwing, or whatever the beast had become, for three days in the tunnels. Gravely injured, I tried to find my way back but couldn't. It was as if the tunnels had changed. Had it not been for a man named [Strahd Von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), I would have died that day.*
>
> *Again I was rescued, again I became a slave. For the next 300 years, I served under [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) in his [War with Duke Gundar]({{< ref "/lore/war-with-duke-gundar" >}}). I also traveled the continent under his crest, killing creatures and men, with the promise that one day Lord [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) would let me return to my world, to my people. The year is now 650 in the Barovian Calendar. I have entirely lost track of my homeland's calendar."*

### A Mocking Addendum and a Lost Ring

A separate slip of paper, presumably added by [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}), fell from the parchment. It read:

> *"Be it a dawn elf or a drow. Die slowly. Sing while you can. Strahd von Zarovich"*

Having taken possession of the gold ring, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) grew furious at [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) mockery. In a fit of anger, he hurled the ring far into the dark forest. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) cursed him for throwing away what might have been a valuable or magical item. The party searched the forest floor but failed to recover the ring.

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}), familiar with drow lore, recognized the names from the letter. He mentioned rumors that Drizzt eventually abandoned his house, leading to a war, and suggested the ring might have belonged to Drizzt or [Hianag Darastrix]({{< ref "/npcs/hianag-darastrix" >}}). 

Exhausted, the party finally settled down to rest and recover.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) threw away the gold ring found with [Hianag's]({{< ref "/npcs/hianag-darastrix" >}}) letter. It is lost in the forest.
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) openly declared himself as an enemy intending to destroy [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}).
- [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) demonstrated a terrifying vulnerability, temporarily falling under [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) control and attempting to run to [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}).
- [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) concluded that properly decoding [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) tome will require dedicated, uninterrupted time.
