## Summary

The party pursues [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) into the Ethereal Plane, where they swiftly finish her off — [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) lands a critical hit and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) delivers the killing blow with a crossbow bolt to her throat. While recovering in the plane's strange, floating reality, the party discovers stone cubes bearing cryptic inscriptions about [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and a mysterious entity called the **[Mad Mage]({{< ref "/lore/mad-mage" >}})**. [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) appears, visible only to [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}})'s mental connection. A [Forest Master's Messenger]({{< ref "/npcs/forest-master-messenger" >}}) arrives in response to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})'s bell, and [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) attempts to challenge her to a duel — [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) talks him down. A black horse wreathed in flames then materializes, opens a portal, and a silent spirit approaches. The party crosses through and returns to the normal plane. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) retrieves a remarkable magical tome from the top of the mill. The party levels up.

## Events

### Morgantha Slain in the Ethereal Plane

The party entered a dimension of strange coloration and floating objects — the Ethereal Plane, a spiritual reality. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) warned the portal was closing behind them. [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) declared they would die lost in the eternal ethereal. The party acted fast: [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) landed a critical hit, and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) finished her with a crossbow bolt to the throat. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) fell just before the killing blow, and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) worked to stabilize him.

### Makias Appears

A figure approached. Neither [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) nor [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) could make physical contact with him. Only [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) telepathic connection reached him. The man explained he could not see them and was there only because of the pies; he found them on the ground floor of the mill and began eating desperately. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) recognized him as [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) — the man from the iron gate in [Svalich Forest]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-forest" >}}) who experienced a vision of [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) — though his appearance had improved.

### The Stone Cubes — Messages from the Mad Mage

The party discovered stone cubes floating in the ethereal space. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) floated upward and retrieved one. The inscriptions were in Subcommon and Primordial, signed by "the Mage." [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) read one cube aloud (in Primordial):

> *"Time does not exist for those who are in Barovia. We are in the infinite shackles of Strahd's evil. He himself forgot everything he has ever done. This may be what will end him. The Mage went mad because of him."*

Other cubes carried shorter warnings:

> *"Beware of the spiders."*

> *"Do not speak with the spirits."*

> *"The Mage — some call him the mad mage — but nobody knows my name."*

### Pepino Discovers the Tome

While the group discussed how to leave the plane, [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) floated through the mill and reached the top floor. Near the mill's mechanisms, he identified a magical aura and found a tome of excellent quality. As he was still in the Ethereal Plane, he could not yet take it.

### The Messenger Arrives — Makias Backs Down

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) had tried ringing his bell to summon aid; nothing had responded initially. Later, a woman approached the mill. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) felt his bell resonate and understood she was the messenger he had called for. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) established a telepathic link, informing her of the party's situation. She described the ethereal to confirm, then rang her own small bell — and notably, [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) bell did not resonate in response.

[Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}), noticing the woman, challenged her to a duel. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) connected with [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) telepathically and warned him bluntly that dueling her would mean his death. [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) stood down and left.

### The Black Horse and the Portal

A black horse with parts wreathed in flame approached. It passed through [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and opened a portal. Through the portal, the far side appeared to show the normal world. A spirit — or undead — emerged and stood watching the group in silence.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) attempted to ask the creature where the portal led. It gave no reply. The party chose to cross. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) went first, followed by [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}). [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) took [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) by the arm and followed. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) called for [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}). As [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) moved to follow, the creature raised its sword toward him. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) said in Infernal: *"We will meet again."*

### Return to the Normal Plane

Back in the normal plane, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) spoke with the messenger. She confirmed she had rung her bell to call upon a greater being for aid. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asked if it could be cancelled; she said she would try, and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) stood with her while she concentrated.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) suggested [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) return to the mill's top floor to retrieve the tome. [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) did so, and confirmed the tome is of extraordinary quality — clearly a very unique item.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) is dead, killed in the Ethereal Plane.
- The Forest Master's Messenger rang her bell to summon a greater being; it is unknown whether her attempt to cancel the request succeeded.
- [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) was in the mill for the pies and backed down from a duel after [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) threat.
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) addressed the silent spirit in Infernal, hinting at a future encounter.
- A mysterious black horse with flaming parts opened the portal that returned the party to the normal plane. Its nature and origin are unknown.
- The party retrieved a magical tome of exceptional quality from the top of the mill.
