## Summary

At [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}})'s home in [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party finally receives the promised reward for escorting [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) safely: 100 gp for each party member, with a smaller share set aside for Igga. Ireena decides she will not stay with Walpole yet, choosing instead to remain with the party for a few more days.

Outside Walpole's house, [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) arrives by carriage and privately warns Monel and Jimmy that town guards will search the party again soon. He advises them to dispose of any compromising documents and notes that Vallakians can be prejudiced against "twilight elves," making Igga more vulnerable to harassment. He also claims that a merchant named [Tolmul]({{< ref "/npcs/tolmul" >}}) will soon arrive near the city with rare goods, including an item meant for Monel.

Before leaving, Walpole reassures the party that their adoption document is not dangerous to carry and suggests they visit the [Vallaki Orphanage]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki-orphanage" >}}).

On the way back toward the Blue Water Inn, guards stop and search the party. They notice Ireena's dyed hair and record that detail separately. Jimmy's vial of poison is confiscated, and the guards state he will be taken to the Stockade for at least a day. The guards also relay that [Izek]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) (referred to as "Isac") wants to know the party's group name and expects them to choose one.

## Events

### Walpole's Payment

Walpole and Ireena speak privately, and Ireena returns carrying a heavy sack of coins. She distributes old, dusty coin purses to the party: Jimmy, Agares, Monel, Pepino, and Igga (Igga's purse is smaller). Ireena embraces each party member warmly.

### Vasili's Carriage

A fine carriage arrives outside Walpole's house, and Vasili invites Monel and Jimmy aboard. He shares three warnings:

- A merchant contact, Tolmul, will arrive near Vallaki soon with rare wares, including something intended for Monel.
- Guards will search the party again soon; compromising documents should be disposed of.
- Vallakians may be prejudiced against "twilight elves," putting Igga at greater risk.

He also suggests that Andreja and Fiona Wachter are "normal" people and that Agares's upcoming meeting should not be inherently dangerous.

### Search and Confiscation

After regrouping, the party is stopped and searched by guards. They demand the party choose a name for themselves (framing the warning as a courtesy), record notes about Ireena's hair separately, confiscate Jimmy's poison vial, and state Jimmy will be jailed in the public prison at the Stockade.

## Decisions & Consequences

- Ireena stays with the party longer instead of moving in with Walpole.
- The party is put on notice to choose a group name.
- Jimmy is taken for at least a day of imprisonment after his poison vial is confiscated.

## Loose Threads

- When and where will Tolmul arrive, and what item is intended for Monel?
- What does Vallaki's guard intend to do with Ireena's identifying details?
- How can the party protect Igga from prejudice and scrutiny in Vallaki?
- What will the party find at the Vallaki Orphanage?
