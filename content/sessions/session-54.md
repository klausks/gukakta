## Summary

At the end of the afternoon, the party prepares to infiltrate [Wachterhaus]({{< ref "/locations/wachterhaus" >}}). [Kiki]({{< ref "/npcs/kiki" >}}) is tasked with watching [Henrich Van der Voort]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}) while [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) sneaks into the mansion under invisibility magic from [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}). Meanwhile, Agares, Vedas, and Igga enter Wachterhaus to speak with [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) under the pretense of helping her daughter. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) stands guard outside with Agares’ horse, ready to intervene if the horse signals trouble.

Inside, Jimmy discovers signs of recent occupation in the basement and a hidden ritual chamber marked by a faint red magical aura. The conversation with Fiona turns political: she refuses outside intervention with Stella for now and demands proof of the party’s allegiance, implying that [Izek Stravni]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) must fall for the Baron to fall. As the group leaves, four masked-robed figures arrive to be admitted — likely cult members.

## Events

### Setup: Watchers and the Plan

- Kiki is set to observe Henrich Van der Voort.
- Monel remains outside Wachterhaus with Agares’ horse as a warning system.
- Agares, Vedas, and Igga enter to meet Fiona Wachter.
- Jimmy infiltrates Wachterhaus while invisible.

### Tea with Madame Wachter

[Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}}) receives Agares, Vedas, and Igga. He informs them Fiona is at afternoon tea.

When Fiona descends, she calls it an excellent night and orders Halik to bring her best wine. She asks what the party has been doing; the group mentions the Martikov mission, and Fiona criticizes the Baron and the festivals. The party agrees that Vallaki’s festival culture is crude.

When they offer to help with Stella, Fiona says her daughter has been improving slowly and receives daily prayers. She does not want interventions at the moment.

Fiona returns to politics, claiming she does not feel the party truly supports her — only the Baron. She demands evidence of loyalty and declares that Izek must fall for the Baron to fall as well, insinuating that the party could eliminate Izek to prove their support.

### Jimmy in the Basement

Jimmy enters the basement and lights a lantern. He finds several old beds that appear to have been used within the last few months, along with clothes and a [Blinksy]({{< ref "/npcs/blinksy" >}}) toy doll.

He discovers a secret door in the wall and hears the sound of fire on the other side. Inside is a ritual room: a pentagram drawn on the floor (the substance could be blood, but this was not confirmed) with a subtle red magical aura. At each point of the pentagram sits a chair, and the center appears to have a place meant to receive an item. Jimmy finds nothing else and leaves, closing the door behind him.

As his invisibility nears its end, he checks a staircase leading up from the basement. It goes directly to the second floor, ending at a door. He decides not to open it and withdraws.

### Isha’s Scouting

Vedas releases his familiar [Isha]({{< ref "/creatures/isha" >}}) — now in the form of a spider rather than an owl — to investigate other rooms.

Isha enters the library and searches the central table, finding a closed book titled **"The Devil we know"**. Later, Isha identifies a different book as the **Diary of Lovina Wachter** and realizes it is connected to a mechanism for a secret door.

### Observations Outside

Monel sees a diverse group of people walking slowly toward Wachterhaus. [Mr. Ernst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-ernst" >}}) appears and greets Monel but does not join the approaching group.

As the party exits, they find four individuals waiting to be called inside. They seem to recognize Agares and mention that foreign tobacco can be obtained through [Frederik]({{< ref "/npcs/frederik" >}}). Their names are **Dorian**, **Milan**, **Orloff**, and **Valeska**. Each keeps a hand in a pocket, which Agares suspects conceals a cult medallion.

Halik calls the four inside Wachterhaus.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party confirms a hidden ritual chamber exists beneath Wachterhaus, with an apparent item-slot at the center of a magically-tinged pentagram.
- Fiona Wachter explicitly demands proof of allegiance and floats the removal of Izek as the price of her support.
- The party identifies possible cult visitors arriving at Wachterhaus.

## Loose Threads

- What item belongs at the center of the pentagram, and what does the ritual chamber do?
- What is behind the second-floor door accessed from the basement stairs?
- What secret door mechanism is tied to Lovina Wachter’s diary?
- Who are Dorian, Milan, Orloff, and Valeska — and what role do they play in the cult?
- What will Kiki learn by watching Henrich Van der Voort?
