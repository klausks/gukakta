## Summary

The ambush in [Henrich Van der Voort's]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}) attic turns into a brutal but decisive fight. The party sends [Kiki]({{< ref "/npcs/kiki" >}}) to call the [Martikovs]({{< ref "/lore/martikov-spy-network" >}}) for aid, but destroys all five vampires before help returns. [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) devastates the field with repeated fireballs, completely incinerating one vampire and badly wounding the others, while [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) crushes another with divine smite. The remaining attackers fall through the party’s combined effort.

As flames begin to consume the coffin maker's house, the party drags Henrich out alive, throws the vampire bodies out the window, and seizes the high-quality weapons the creatures had been carrying. Outside, terrified neighbors gather around the burning scene until [Izek Stravni]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) arrives with guards and demands an account of what happened.

## Events

### Calling for Reinforcements

- The party sends [Kiki]({{< ref "/npcs/kiki" >}}) to ask the Martikovs for help.
- The requested aid does not arrive before the fight is over.

### The Five Vampires Fall

The attic battle continues immediately after the coffin ambush.

- [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) does most of the damage with a series of fireballs, burning one vampire to ash and heavily injuring the others.
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) destroys another vampire with a divine smite.
- The rest are defeated by the party working together.

### Escape from the Fire

Before the house is completely consumed by flames:

- the party removes [Henrich Van der Voort]({{< ref "/npcs/henrich-van-der-voort" >}}) from the building alive
- the vampire corpses are thrown out the window
- the party collects the weapons used by the vampires, which appear to be of excellent quality

### Guards on the Scene

Outside, frightened townspeople gather around the scene. [Izek Stravni]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) arrives out of breath with several guards and asks the party to explain the situation.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The five vampires hidden in Henrich's attic are destroyed before outside help arrives.
- Henrich survives the destruction of his house and can still be questioned or judged by Vallaki's authorities.
- The house itself is consumed by fire.
- The party leaves the scene in possession of the vampires' weapons.
- Izek and the guard are now directly involved in the immediate aftermath of the coffin maker incident.

## Loose Threads

- How will [Izek Stravni]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}}) and the guards respond to the party's explanation?
- Will the Martikovs still act on Kiki's request after the battle is already over?
- What is the true origin and significance of the vampires' high-quality weapons?
- What consequences will Henrich face now that his role in the plot is public and his house has burned?

## Rewards

- Five high-quality weapons taken from the vampires: a longsword, warhammer, dagger, bow, and one attacker carried no weapon.
