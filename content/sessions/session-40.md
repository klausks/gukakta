## Summary

Still unsettled by the strange events at [Wachterhaus]({{< ref "/locations/wachterhaus" >}}), Agares continues investigating until he hears [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}})’s voice through whispers: *"Go away. You are still raw. It will not be tonight that things happen."* He relays that the attempt has failed, and he and [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) stop searching.

In the morning, [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) is nowhere to be found, and the household staff are gathered in the guest quarters with Igga. The party eats, receives two rations from [Danika Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/danika-martikov" >}}), and prepares to leave [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}) under renewed scrutiny from the guards.

On the road, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) overhears an argument between a man and a [Vistani]({{< ref "/lore/vistani" >}}) about *treants* and a captured sapling—suggesting the forest may respond. Shortly afterward, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})’s Forest Master bell vibrates and a forest messenger warns of a dangerous [Troll]({{< ref "/creatures/troll" >}}) hunting carrion near key landmarks.

As the group heads toward the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}), they find a primordial inscription at a bridge, then follow a trail into a grotto where a wounded, [hairy traveler]({{< ref "/npcs/hairy-traveler" >}}) lies bleeding while a giant rat flees deeper into the cave with a beautiful scimitar clamped in its teeth.

## Events

### Halik’s Whispered Warning
Agares hears Halik whisper: *"Go away. You are still raw. It will not be tonight that things happen."* Agares tells Igga the investigation will not succeed tonight, and they return to sleep.

### Morning at Wachterhaus
The party wakes feeling physically well, but with clear memories of the prior night’s collective outburst. The household staff are gathered in the guest quarters with Igga, and Halik dismisses the party politely. Fiona is not seen that morning.

### Vedas’ Familiar
[Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) wakes first, prepares spells, and summons a white owl companion.

### A Drunk Noble Under Watch
Before departing, the party recognizes one of the nobles Vedas previously played dice with—sprawled drunk across a table with visible jewelry and a full coin pouch. Vedas remarks to Jimmy that the man is an easy target, but the party notices they are being watched closely.

### Leaving Vallaki
At the gates, guards question the party about where they are going, why, for how long, and by what route. They are warned that upon returning, they will undergo the same entry process as before.

### Treants and Vistani Curses
Just outside Vallaki, Jimmy stealths close enough to overhear a heated exchange between a man and a Vistani. The man complains about problems with treants, while the Vistani claims their group took a treant sapling and that the adults will come looking.

The Vistani also boasts that a curse from their people is "two touches." After the Vistani leaves toward a column of smoke, the party discusses what they know of treants and whether diplomacy might be possible.

### The Troll Warning
Monel’s Forest Master bell vibrates, and a forest messenger approaches to warn of a troll attacking travelers in the region—an estimated bounty of **800 gp**.

The messenger warns the troll is not attacking on the roads, feeds on carrion, and can be tracked by its strong, acidic, pungent smell. It has been reported near [Lake Baratok]({{< ref "/locations/lake-baratok" >}}) (south of [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}})) and at a crossroads near the [Raven River]({{< ref "/locations/raven-river" >}}), close to an [Abandoned Orchard]({{< ref "/locations/abandoned-orchard" >}}).

When asked, the messenger describes treants as generally neutral, and the Vistani as generally neutral but often in conflict.

### The Bridge Inscription
Approaching the winery bridge, the party finds a stone inscription in Primordial:

*"When the light of the sky touches what lies beneath these runes, the power sealed long ago shall be revealed."*

### A Rat with a Beautiful Scimitar
Scouting with Vedas’ owl, the party spots a very hairy human apparently struggling with a giant rat. The rat flees with something in its mouth as the man chases after it, shouting for the rat to return his magical weapon.

Following the trail into a grotto, the party finds the man downed and bleeding, while the rat retreats deeper into the cave carrying a beautiful scimitar. The man begins to babble incoherently.

## Decisions & Consequences

- Agares and Igga stop investigating Wachterhaus for the night after Halik’s warning.
- The party avoids acting against the drunk noble, recognizing they are under observation.
- The party proceeds toward the winery while keeping the troll threat in mind.

## Loose Threads

- What did Halik mean by saying the party is "still raw," and what was he preventing that night?
- Where was Fiona the next morning, and why were the servants gathered in the guest quarters?
- Is there truly a captured treant sapling in the area, and what will the adult treants do in response?
- Where exactly is the troll’s most active hunting ground (ask Zé), and who posted the bounty?
- What is the meaning of the primordial bridge inscription, and what is sealed beneath the runes?
- Who is the hairy traveler, and what is the scimitar the rat stole?
