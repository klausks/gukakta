## Summary

The party reaches a large iron gate they have never seen closed before, where they meet a nervous young stranger named [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) who is also waiting to pass. When [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) reaches for a key at the top of the gate with his mage hand, [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) briefly manifests and kicks it down before vanishing. The party passes through and continues toward the mill. Along the way, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) suffer unexplained psychic damage. Near the mill, the party finds a four-monolith structure, kills an undead and its insect swarm, and discovers a suspicious raven with anomalous properties. They descend into a passage beneath the central stone and find a dark cave containing a toothless skull with a magical red candle, and a narrow corridor from which a red thread and strong necromancy energy emanate.

## Events

### Madame Iva's Warning to Pepino

Before reaching the gate, [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) spoke to [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) through a vision:

> *"Mr. Falstaff, you managed to escape the clutches of [...]. I see luck in those who are superstitious. Your tongue will betray you from time to time. Your luck can quickly plummet into a curse. Your superstition alone will not save you. Two attempts on your life are coming. Already set in motion. You will die in the worst way."*

Later on the road, [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) spoke to him again:

> *"There is a way to reverse death, through deception. Lie only to evil, do not betray the good. Let us speak of Mikaela."*

### The Closed Gate

The party arrived at a tall iron gate they had never seen closed before, with a dense mist in front of it. A young, thin human sat alone nearby, holding an unlit torch. He wore a Vistani-style hat.

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) remarked he had never seen the gate closed. The stranger, anxious, asked who the party was and stated he had no desire to duel. He explained he could not pass through and intended to wait for the "old pie woman" to go by, believing the gate would open when she did.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) experienced flashes of children running and felt a tingling sensation in their feet, exchanging a glance.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) approached the stranger and offered a rope to help scale the gate. The man declined to share information but pointed out a key near the top of the gate, seemingly close to falling. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) offered to use his mage hand to retrieve it. The stranger, overhearing this, remarked: *"You're magicians, just like my friends."*

### Strahd's Apparition

When [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) mage hand touched the key, [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) appeared crouched at the top of the gate, holding the key. He looked down at the party, kicked the key in their direction, and asked calmly: *"Jimmy, why didn't you climb the gate?"* Then he vanished instantly. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) felt watched.

### Passing Through the Gate

The stranger introduced himself as [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) and asked [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) her name; she gave a false one. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) moved to claim the key, and [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) provoked him: *"Had to be the drow."* After the gate was unlocked, [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) passed through ahead and disappeared into the forest.

### Unexplained Psychic Damage

Shortly after passing through the gate, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) each suffered psychic damage of unknown origin, emerging with –6 temporary HP they could not account for. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) suspected the pies. The party debated whether to allow [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to eat any future pies and agreed they should be prevented from doing so, though cautiously—both might become violent or lose control. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) raised the question of whether overusing the mending-on-doll healing might itself be the cause of these ongoing effects.

### Tracks of the Pie Woman

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) recognized the path toward the old mill. On the way, the party noticed human footprints in the opposite direction of their travel, accompanied by two parallel drag lines—consistent with someone dragging heavy objects or a cart. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) identified these as likely belonging to the old pie woman.

### Hanging Dolls

Further ahead, the party found more dolls hanging along the path. After brief debate they decided to leave them alone and move on. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) used Divine Sense but detected nothing unusual.

### The Monolith Structure — An Undead Encounter

The party came upon a structure of four white monoliths surrounding a large central stone. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) detected an undead creature nearby with Divine Sense. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) mentioned that her father had told her these structures are places of great safety and that four white pillars make a good campsite.

The party killed the undead creature, which released a swarm of insects upon death. They killed the swarm as well.

### The Suspicious Raven

A raven that had been following the party began calling out desperately. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) cast *Command* to bring it down. The raven obeyed, confirming it understands spoken language—meaning it is likely not an ordinary bird. When it perched on [Agares']({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) arm, it was notably heavier than a natural raven and had a small golden circle on its leg.

### The Passage and the Skull

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) identified a hidden passage beneath the central stone of the monolith structure. [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) urged [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) to investigate. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) noted that the dolls in the area emitted divination magic—different from the enchantment aura on the party's own dolls—suggesting they may be used to observe the party. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) proposed destroying them; the party did so.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) entered the passage first. Inside, they found a dark cave. At the far end sat a **toothless skull** with a **lit magical red candle** on top—[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) confirmed the candle is magical, as no wax was melting. Beyond the skull, a narrow passage carries a back-and-forth breeze. From deeper in the cave, [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) detected a **strong necromancy energy**.

After the party gathered inside, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) advanced and noticed a thin red thread—like a vein—running from the skull into the narrow passage ahead. He warned his companions not to step on it.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party chose to ignore [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) apparition and make no comment.
- [Makias]({{< ref "/npcs/makias" >}}) passed through freely but shared no meaningful information.
- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) currently carry –6 temporary HP of unexplained psychic origin.
- The party is inside the cave passage beneath the monolith structure, preparing to advance past the skull.
- Divination-aura dolls in the area have been destroyed.
