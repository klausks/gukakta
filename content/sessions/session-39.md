## Summary

In [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), the party pushes for answers about the captured fiend **Majesto** ([Invisible Fiend]({{< ref "/creatures/invisible-fiend" >}})), intending to use truth magic and to question it away from [Halik]({{< ref "/npcs/halik" >}})’s influence.

When the interrogation begins, a crucial fact comes to light: Majesto is not a random intruder, but a **familiar tied to the Wachters**—a secret [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}) and Halik have concealed for political reasons.

Elsewhere, [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) makes a purchase at [Frederik]({{< ref "/npcs/frederik" >}})’s shop, and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) undergoes a private test of faith tied to the hidden crypt beneath St. Andral’s Church.

The session ends on uneasy, half-glimpsed events at [Wachterhaus]({{< ref "/locations/wachterhaus" >}}): masked gifts, whispered intrusions, and a sudden collapse at the door.

## Events

### Plans for a Truthful Interrogation
[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) plan to isolate Halik and interrogate Majesto with the help of truth magic, aiming to force clear answers.

### Majesto’s Secret
During the questioning, it is revealed that **Majesto is a familiar** connected to Fiona and Halik, and that the household has hidden this fact due to the political consequences it could carry in Vallaki.

Majesto is later described as **healed** and able to **fly while remaining invisible**.

### A Pearl at the Tailor
At Frederik’s shop, [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) buys a pearl.

### Jimmy’s Test Beneath the Altar
Jimmy undertakes a private religious test connected to the hidden crypt beneath St. Andral’s Church: a crystal, a phrase of release, and prayer—followed by a strange tingling sensation.

### Night at Wachterhaus
That night, several unsettling incidents occur:

- Igga discovers barrels associated with the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}) and hears a warning voice.
- Agares receives a mask and garments tied to identity.
- [Nikolai Wachter II]({{< ref "/npcs/nikolai-wachter-ii" >}}) collapses at Agares’ door.
- [Stella Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/stella-wachter" >}}) appears, whispers, and the scene ends with people seemingly asleep.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party agrees to keep what they have learned about the demon/fiend situation secret until they have left Vallaki.
- Fiona and Halik’s relationship to Majesto reframes the Wachterhaus “demon” problem as a political secret as much as a threat.

## Loose Threads

- What does Majesto truly want—and who is it loyal to?
- What are the political stakes in Vallaki if Majesto’s status as a familiar becomes known?
- What exactly happened in the night incidents at Wachterhaus (the barrels, the collapse, and the whispers)?
- What was the nature and outcome of Jimmy’s test in the hidden crypt beneath St. Andral’s Church?
