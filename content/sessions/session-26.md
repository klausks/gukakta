## Summary

The party departs the mill under escort from the [Forest Master's Messenger]({{< ref "/npcs/forest-master-messenger" >}}), who guides them to a safe campsite for 50 po. Before leaving, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) searches the mill and finds hidden valuables and a curious adoption document. On the road, the colorful leather pouch looted from [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) is opened — a spirit escapes and feeds [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demon, who delivers a brutal warning. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) privately reveals to each party member that the magical tome is [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) personal childhood diary. That night, [Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) arrives atop a horse wreathed in flames — detected as an illusion by [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) — and challenges [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) to save [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) life through performance. Both attempts fail. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) invokes his rule — a blow for a blow — and leaves [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) near death. When [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) curses [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and takes his turn, [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) strikes him down as well and departs, claiming to have kept the rules. The session ends with the sound of large creatures approaching from the darkness of the forest.

## Events

### Debrief and Departure from the Mill

The party filled in [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) on what had occurred while he was unconscious. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) questioned the [Forest Master's Messenger]({{< ref "/npcs/forest-master-messenger" >}}), whose name is a secret she would not share. She would only respond to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}). The party asked whether the Forest Master had any relationship with [Count Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}); she confirmed there is none. She offered to escort the party to a safe resting place for 50 po, and a second offer: there would be no duels directed at her. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) accepted, paid, and gave an additional 50 po as a tip — though it seemed she found it less than she expected.

Before departing, the group paused briefly to search the mill. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) remained outside with the Messenger while the others investigated.

### Jimmy's Finds in the Mill

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) located a small chest hidden under a bed. Inside he found:

- **6 pieces of jewelry** (low value)
- **200 gold pieces**
- **An adoption document** — partially smudged, but legible. It records the adoption of three children, one of whose names is **Kaselyn**. It references an institution operating under the name **"Valak"** — possibly an institution in [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}).

### The Colorful Pouch — Jimmy's Demon Feeds

On the road, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) handed [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) the colorful leather pouch looted from [Morgantha]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}). When [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) opened it, a spirit escaped from inside.

[Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demon immediately spoke: *"Ah, it's been a long time since I've eaten. Thank you, Jimmy."* He then added: *"The stronger I grow, the closer your death becomes."* [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) dismissed this. The demon, irritated at his lack of respect, dealt him **15 damage (4d8)**. The [Forest Master's Messenger]({{< ref "/npcs/forest-master-messenger" >}}) observed with visible unease and stated plainly: *"I am not involved in this."*

### Skeleton March in the Forest

On the way to the campsite, the group began hearing howls. The Messenger signalled the party to stop and stay silent. Moments later, a column of skeletons marched past — not wolf-hunters on patrol, she clarified, but something else entirely. The unearthly march passed within 20 metres and moved on without incident. The howling stopped. The Messenger relaxed, and the group continued.

### Ireena's Dreams and Foreboding

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) questioned [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) about [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}). He described [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) as a severe man — trouble should be avoided. He mentioned **Izek**, the Burgomaster's right-hand man, a knight (described in Infernal). He also mentioned **Fiona**, head of a powerful, politically ambitious family in the city.

[Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) expressed that she had been having **prophetic dreams** urging extreme caution upon entering [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}). She planned to hide her hair and conceal her face there. She mentioned experiencing similar dream cycles before — linked to other red-haired women — and suggested the dreams may have been previously interrupted by the noise of guards, possibly sent by [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}).

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) confirmed their position on the map and the direction toward [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), then paid the Messenger. As she bowed to leave, she offered a parting remark: *"I have heard there are fewer Vistani on the road these days..."*

### The Tome Is Strahd's Diary

After making camp and lighting a fire, [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) communicated privately with each party member through a mental link:

- **Monel**: the tome is [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) personal diary, from his childhood.
- **Jimmy**: the book belongs to the demon that cannot be named.
- **Agares**: the book must be kept completely secret — spoken words could reach unwanted ears.
- **Igga**: Pepino asked if Igga would consider **connecting** with the tome, since he cannot advance in reading it without a deeper magical link. [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) declined, warning of a possible curse. He reflected that this discovery validates [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) foresight — that knowledge of the enemy from his own words would give the party an advantage.

[Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) then said aloud: *"Absolute secrecy on this, agreed?"* The party agreed.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) cautioned the group about opening unexamined items in the future.

### Ireena's Headache and Strahd's Arrival

[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) complained of a headache similar to the one she felt at the inn in [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}). [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) initially suggested she not sleep; [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast *Detect Magic*. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) reminded [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) that [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) may be a conduit for [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) — his eyes and ears — which would explain [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) avoidance of her. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}), exhausted from combat, suggested she sleep anyway. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) deferred. [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) went to rest.

As soon as she was asleep, **a horse wreathed in flames** approached the camp. [Igga's]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) *Detect Magic* identified it as an **illusion**. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) moved to guard [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}); [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) took a battle stance; [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) moved toward [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}).

[Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) stepped out of invisibility.

### Strahd's Challenge — "A Blow for a Blow"

[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) addressed [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) directly, stating his contempt for bards: *"People who write about life instead of living it."* He declared that [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) had one chance to save [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) life through performance — through prose alone, not weapons.

- **First attempt**: [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) performed a piece celebrating [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) bravery. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) was unmoved.
- **Second attempt**: [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) spoke of [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}). [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) was still not pleased.

[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) then declared his rule: **a blow for a blow**. [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) struck first — a punch and a headbutt. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) barely registered the hits. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) said it was his turn; [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) removed his armor, declaring it the fairest exchange. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) struck and left [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) near death.

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) cursed [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and became the new target. He struck [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) with a punch and a headbutt. [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) retaliated, laying [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) unconscious, and departed — claiming to have honored his own rules.

[Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) crawled to [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) to attempt stabilization.

### Creatures in the Dark

The session ended with the sound of rustling leaves and the unmistakable approach of **large creatures** from the forest.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party now knows the tome is [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) personal diary — potential leverage as anticipated by [Madame Iva's]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}) [Tarot reading]({{< ref "/lore/tarot-reading" >}}).
- [Jimmy's]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) demon has been fed by the spirit in [Morgantha's]({{< ref "/npcs/morgantha" >}}) pouch and is growing stronger — an escalating threat.
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) is unconscious; [Igga]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) is near death. The camp is dangerously exposed.
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) demonstrated again that he governs interactions with rigid personal rules and enforces them without hesitation.
- The fact that [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) headache preceded [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) arrival further supports the theory that she is a conduit for his senses.
