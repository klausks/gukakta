## Summary

The session opens with an addendum to [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) visions: the enormous creature she described has **red-haired dolls hanging from it**, a detail that points directly toward a local doll-maker. [Father Lucian Petrovich]({{< ref "/npcs/lucian-petrovich" >}}) and [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) meet privately at [St. Andral's Church]({{< ref "/locations/st-andrals-church" >}}). Outside, the rest of the party is approached by [Willemina Rikalova]({{< ref "/npcs/willemina-rikalova" >}}), a desperate mother whose son has been imprisoned by the Baron. The group then visits [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}), who shares new intelligence about the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) and his unexpected acquaintance with [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}). Finally, the party visits [Blinksy]({{< ref "/npcs/blinksy" >}}), Vallaki's eccentric toy-maker, where disturbing dolls — and a disturbing revelation about the twilight elves — await.

## Key Events

### Addendum: Ireena's Visions and the Doll-Maker

Father Lucian shared an additional detail about [Ireena's]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) recurring visions: the enormous creature that towers over her has **red-haired dolls hanging from it**. Father Lucian mentioned that there is a man in Vallaki who makes cloth dolls — his name is **[Blinksy]({{< ref "/npcs/blinksy" >}})**.

### Jimmy and the Priest Meet Privately

Jimmy Slit and Father Lucian withdrew to meet alone inside the church. The rest of the party waited outside.

### Willemina Rikalova

While waiting outside the church, the party was approached by a woman named [Willemina Rikalova]({{< ref "/npcs/willemina-rikalova" >}}). She explained that her son, [Udo]({{< ref "/npcs/udo-rikalova" >}}), had recently changed his behavior: he stopped attending church, began leaving home at night wearing dark cloaks, and developed some connection to the Wachter family. He was recently arrested by the Baron's guards for carrying a sign that read the Burgomaster should be thrown to the wolves. She offered **50 gp** to anyone who could rescue him from the Baron's custody.

### Visit to Walpole

The party visited [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}) at his home. Two pieces of information came up:

- When asked about the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}), Walpole said he believes there is a **fallen angel** serving as the abbot — a figure who does not age. He noted that people from [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}) are very guarded about the abbey when in Vallaki, making information scarce.
- [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) has been visiting Walpole regularly and the two have developed a friendly acquaintance. Vasili recently gave Walpole a **large sack of potatoes** but was vague about his political allegiances.
- Walpole has received no further news from [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}).

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) donated **2 gp** to Walpole. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) separately arranged a paid meal for Walpole at a tavern.

### Visit to Blinksy's Shop

The party visited [Blinksy]({{< ref "/npcs/blinksy" >}}), a doll and toy maker in Vallaki. Notable observations:

- Blinksy has a **monkey companion named [Piccolo]({{< ref "/npcs/blinksy" >}})**.
- His shop carries an assortment of dolls depicting creatures: vampires, skeletons, ghouls, and others. The shop stays open later than most establishments.
- He mentioned that some customers had ordered dolls and later returned to thank him — the dolls had apparently become a kind of **protective amulet** for the bearers, though Blinksy could not explain the mechanism.
- He mentioned, with apparent confusion rather than pride, that **some of his dolls come to life** and have escaped the shop.
- He told the party that **[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) killed all the women and children of the twilight elves**, describing it as a great tragedy that befell that people.

[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) commissioned a doll, intending it as a donation. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) commissioned a miniature doll in her own likeness.

### The Vistani Message and Von Richten's Book

A Vistani individual made contact with [Ernst Larnak]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-ernst" >}}), informing him they had something to deliver to [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}). In the same exchange, Monel **lent her copy of Von Richten's book** to Ernst.

## Discoveries & Clues

- The creature in Ireena's visions has **red-haired dolls hanging from it** — pointing toward Blinksy as a connection to whoever or whatever is behind the visions.
- Blinksy's dolls have reportedly become protective amulets for some buyers, and some have come to life and escaped.
- The [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) in Krezk is presided over by a figure who does not age; Walpole suspects he is a fallen angel. Crows do not land at the abbey.
- Krezk's residents are secretive about the abbey when encountered in Vallaki.
- Vasili von Holtz has been visiting Walpole and the two are friendly, but Vasili's political orientation remains unclear.
- Walpole has heard nothing further from Ismark.
- A Vistani has something to deliver to Monel via Ernst — contents unknown.
- Strahd is said to have killed all women and children of the twilight elves.
- [Udo Rikalova]({{< ref "/npcs/udo-rikalova" >}}) is currently in the Baron's custody, arrested for seditious speech; his connection to the Wachter family and sudden behavioral changes are unexplained.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party is now aware of a possible rescue mission for Udo Rikalova; no commitment made yet.
- Igga and Monel have placed orders with Blinksy.
- Monel has lent Von Richten's book to Ernst, whose interest in it is unclear.
