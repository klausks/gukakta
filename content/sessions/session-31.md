## Summary

In [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}}), [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) visits the barber [Mr. Urko]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-urko" >}}), who speaks cautiously about the town and warns that the forest can drive a man mad. Urko also comments that [Baron Vargas Vallakovich]({{< ref "/npcs/baron-vallakovich" >}}) is a severe man who must be respected.

The party regroups at the Blue Water Inn amid a lively tavern celebration and observes several local nobles, including [Nikolai]({{< ref "/npcs/nikolai" >}}) and a rigid, well-dressed man known as [Mr. Ernst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-ernst" >}}). With [Danika Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/danika-martikov" >}}) overwhelmed by work, the group agrees to coordinate their stories and present themselves as mysterious adventurers, while [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) prepares a cover story about settling down in Vallaki.

Later, [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) asks the party for help: his family operates the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}), but wine deliveries have been failing for weeks due to non-natural trouble—possibly druids and corrupted creatures, with suspected influence from [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}).

Following up on Ismark's letter, the party visits St. Andral's Church and is guided to [Walpole]({{< ref "/npcs/walpole" >}}) with the help of a grave digger named [Milivoj]({{< ref "/npcs/milivoj" >}}). Walpole recognizes Ireena and promises a reward for escorting her safely to Vallaki.

## Events

### The Barber

Jimmy gets a fresh haircut and beard trim from Mr. Urko. Urko remarks that his son is ambitious, warns that the forest can drive a man mad, and advises the party to respect the Baron.

Urko asked for only 1 sp, but Jimmy paid 2 sp; Urko seemed genuinely grateful. Jimmy also realizes the new cut and beard could help him pass as a minor noble if needed.

### The Blue Water Inn

The group returns to the inn to plan. A celebration is underway in the common room, and the party spots nobles playing dice. Nikolai introduces himself, and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) joins the dice game. The party agrees on a social approach: be mysterious, share little beyond being adventurers, and coordinate their stories.

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) fields questions from the nobles during the dice game.

Upstairs, Pepino mentions he intends to connect with the tome that night. The party swaps room assignments so Pepino and Jimmy are not in the same room as Ireena during the reading.

Mr. Ernst is also present—well dressed and unusually rigid—and later is seen outside reading a book about kings.

### Urwin's Request

Urwin calls the party aside and urges them to ignore the nobles in the common room, saying they are "all of the house." He explains that his family owns the Wizard of Wines Winery and that wine is currently in short supply for reasons that are not natural. He asks the party to travel there (about 8 hours from Vallaki) to investigate and help restore deliveries.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) are present for Urwin's request.

Urwin also clarifies that the tortured tree at the Stockade was brought in by an adventurer and may not be connected to the winery troubles.

The party does not commit immediately, but plans to give an answer by the next day.

### St. Andral's Church and Walpole

The party goes to the church to find Walpole. In the cemetery, they meet Milivoj—an unusually handsome young man digging a grave—who mutters bitterly about being considered "problematic" for not smiling while the city crumbles, and fears ending up in the Baron's hands (or worse, [Izek]({{< ref "/npcs/izek" >}})—whom he calls "Isak").

Inside the church, Walpole is found speaking with a child. He recognizes Ireena and embraces her. When asked about the promised payment for escorting Ireena safely to Vallaki, Walpole leads the party to his humble but well-kept home to discuss terms.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party agreed on a coordinated social story to better navigate Vallaki's politics.
- The party deferred answering Urwin's request until the next day.

## Loose Threads

- What is disrupting the Wizard of Wines deliveries, and why has it been happening for weeks?
- Who in Vallaki is receiving (or expecting) the winery's wine?
- Why does Milivoj fear Izek so intensely?

## Rewards

- Walpole promised **80 gp per person** (except Igga) for escorting Ireena safely to Vallaki.
