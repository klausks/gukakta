## Summary

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) explore a forgotten domed room inside [Castle Ravenloft]({{< ref "/locations/castle-ravenloft" >}}), encounter a hostile spirit, and flee before midnight. A carriage awaited them outside and brought them near Vallaki, where [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) saw them arrive. After some difficulty with guards, they enter the city and reunite with the rest of the party at the Blue Water Inn. [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) shares two alarming pieces of news: [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) left the inn the day after the party departed, and [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) came asking about a redheaded woman. The full party gathers in a private room to share what happened and debrief each other — including Viktor's revelations about [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}}). The session ends with the group turning to the Tarot to plan their next move.

## Key Events

### The Domed Room

Monel and Igga discovered a domed room in Castle Ravenloft that appeared to have not been visited in years. Inside they found:

- A **spirit**, which attacked them
- A **mace**
- An artifact of **divination nature**

Unarmed and without the full party, they chose to flee rather than engage the spirit. They exited the castle before midnight.

### The Waiting Carriage

Upon leaving the castle, the pair found a carriage waiting for them. It transported them to the outskirts of Vallaki.

As they exited the carriage near the city, [Vasili von Holtz]({{< ref "/npcs/vasili-von-holtz" >}}) spotted them. He offered a theatrical bow — as if acknowledging them in a stage performance — then disappeared.

### Re-entry into Vallaki

The pair had some difficulty convincing the guards that they had simply become separated from the rest of their group. They eventually succeeded and entered the city.

### Urwin's News

At the Blue Water Inn, [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) shared two pieces of information:

- **Ireena left the inn** the day after the party departed for the winery.
- At some point, **Ismark came to the inn and asked if there was a redheaded woman staying there**.

### Ernst at the Door

As the party gathered in a room upstairs and checked for surveillance before entering, they heard a knock. [Mr. Ernst]({{< ref "/npcs/mr-ernst" >}}) appeared at the door and asked how the mission had gone. Monel deferred, explaining the group had been separated and needed to catch up among themselves. Ernst accepted this and left. He appeared noticeably more inebriated than in prior encounters.

### The Debrief

Behind closed doors, the full party exchanged accounts of what had happened since the portal split them.

Monel asked [Vedas]({{< ref "/players/vedas" >}}) whether anything in the tome he has been reading related to what Monel and Igga had experienced at Ravenloft. Vedas said the contents did not appear directly relevant but shared more of what he had read. He committed to rereading sections to provide more complete information later.

The other half of the party — Agares, Vedas, and Jimmy — recounted their own return through the portal into [Viktor Vallakovich's]({{< ref "/npcs/viktor-vallakovich" >}}) attic. Viktor made several significant disclosures:

- He is a **capable magic practitioner** and the party considers him a potentially rational ally for planning actions against Strahd.
- He shows **contempt for his father** but acknowledges that, whatever the Baron's faults, he has managed to keep Strahd out of Vallaki — a result Viktor attributes partly to his father's methods.
- He **denies full responsibility** for [Stella Wachter's]({{< ref "/npcs/stella-wachter" >}}) mental breakdown, implying shared or uncertain culpability.
- He claims to **hate [Fiona Wachter]({{< ref "/npcs/fiona-wachter" >}})** and leveled serious accusations against her: that she **practices necromancy** and maintains a **cult in the basement of her home**.
- He helped the group leave the mansion **without raising suspicion**.

### Tarot Planning

With the debrief complete, the party turned to the Tarot to discuss and plan their next steps.

## Discoveries & Clues

- There is an unvisited domed room in Castle Ravenloft containing a spirit, a mace, and a divination artifact.
- The carriage waiting outside was not arranged by the party — its origin and purpose are unknown.
- Vasili von Holtz appeared near Vallaki and acknowledged the party with a theatrical bow before vanishing — his awareness of their movements is unexplained.
- Ireena's whereabouts are now unknown; she left the inn shortly after the party did.
- Ismark is actively searching for Ireena in Vallaki.
- Viktor accuses Fiona Wachter of practicing necromancy and running a cult in her basement.
- Viktor denies full responsibility for Stella Wachter's mental condition.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party chose to flee the domed room rather than confront the spirit unarmed.
- The group is now fully reunited at the Blue Water Inn.
- Ireena's disappearance is now a pressing concern.
