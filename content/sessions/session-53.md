## Summary

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) return to the [Vistani camp near Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vistani-camp-vallaki" >}}) and meet [Arrigal]({{< ref "/npcs/arrigal" >}}), the camp leader, whose niece [Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}}) has been missing for a day and a half. The party tracks her to [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}), where a fisherman named [Bluto]({{< ref "/npcs/bluto" >}}) had been keeping her locked in a sack. Arabelle is recovered and returned to her father [Louvash]({{< ref "/npcs/louvash" >}}). The camp rewards the party with a detailed map and invites them to their feast, during which Arrigal shares valuable intelligence about Vallaki, Krezk, and the abbey.

## Key Events

### Meeting Arrigal

Arrigal received Jimmy and Monel with visible coldness. He explained that under ordinary circumstances the Vistani are festive and welcoming, but his brother [Louvash]({{< ref "/npcs/louvash" >}})'s daughter **[Arabelle]({{< ref "/npcs/arabelle" >}})** had been missing for a day and a half. The man being publicly punished with whipping in the central tent was the guard responsible for watching her — he had failed in his duty. Arabelle was last seen near [Lake Zarovich]({{< ref "/locations/lake-zarovich" >}}), a short distance north of camp.

### Tracking Arabelle

Jimmy and Monel followed a suspicious trail, accompanied by a Vistani named [Irmno]({{< ref "/npcs/irmno" >}}) who was assigned to guide them. Irmno proved unhelpful and uncooperative throughout.

Following the trail, the party spotted a **light on the lake**. Irmno identified it as likely belonging to **Bluto**, a local fisherman known to frequent the lake.

### Bluto's House

The party entered Bluto's illuminated house and found **Arabelle locked inside a sack**. Arabelle described Bluto as "crazy" and mentioned having had conversations with [the Mad Mage]({{< ref "/npcs/mad-mage" >}}) during her captivity. The nature of these conversations is unknown. Bluto himself was not present when the party entered the house.

### Return to the Camp

Arabelle was brought back to the camp and reunited with her father Louvash. As reward, the party received a **detailed map** of the region and were invited to the Vistani feast. They attended briefly before leaving early in response to a call from [Isha]({{< ref "/creatures/isha" >}}), Vedas' owl.

### Intelligence from Arrigal

Before departing, Jimmy and Monel asked the camp leaders several questions:

- **Jimmy's hand:** Arrigal confirmed that the best path toward restoring a lost limb is the [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) in [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}).
- **Baron Vallakovich and the festivals:** Arrigal described the Baron as jealous and unstable. He stated that no one in Vallaki is truly happy — the public celebration is a performance, not genuine.
- **Krezk:** Arrigal said Krezk's burgomaster is considerably saner than Vallaki's. The two settlements maintain a relationship primarily to **smuggle wine** between them.
- **The Wachter family:** The Vistani know of the Wachters but have no relationship with them.

## Discoveries & Clues

- Arabelle mentioned having had conversations with [the Mad Mage]({{< ref "/npcs/mad-mage" >}}) while held at Bluto's house. The identity and significance of this mage are unknown.
- The [Abbey of Saint Markovia]({{< ref "/locations/abbey-of-saint-markovia" >}}) is confirmed by Arrigal as the most viable path for restoring Jimmy's severed hand.
- Krezk's burgomaster is described as notably saner than Baron Vallakovich. Krezk and Vallaki trade covertly, primarily in wine.
- No one in Vallaki is genuinely happy, according to the Vistani — the festival culture is not organic.
- The Vistani know the Wachter family but maintain no active relationship with them.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party now possesses a detailed regional map acquired as a reward from the Vistani.
- Jimmy has a confirmed destination for hand restoration: the Abbey of Saint Markovia in Krezk.
- The party left the feast early due to Isha's summons — the nature of that call is unresolved.
