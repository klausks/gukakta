## Summary

Advancing through the cave, the party discovers a limbless man suspended by ropes who reveals he has been held captive for years by three sisters, who repeatedly sever his regrown limbs. At his own pleading, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) ends his suffering. The party then proceeds to the mill and is welcomed inside by the youngest sister, [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}). After discovering severed arms in sacks upstairs, [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) takes [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) hostage, and combat breaks out with the elder sisters [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) and [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}).

## Events

### The Tortured Man in the Cave

Advancing deeper into the cave, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) asked [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) whether he too felt pain — [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) confirmed an unease.

At the far end of the cave, the party found a naked man suspended from ropes. His arms and legs were severed, his body hairless, eyeless, and still — though [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) detected shallow breathing and slight lip movement with scarring around the mouth. Buckets lay beneath him, the source of a strong smell. The red thread from the skull connected to the man's chest.

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) struck the man in the stomach. The thread snapped, the skull filled with melted red wax, and the magical candle extinguished. The man began to writhe and cry for help.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) offered to cut him down, but the man insisted he only wanted to die in peace. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) used a mental connection to ask who had done this to him. The man revealed that his daughters had been kidnapped by the same women who imprisoned him. He had been there for years — his limbs continually regrow, only to be severed again by the same captors, who are not always present but keep returning. He said the name of one captor began with **"B"**, another with **"O"**.

The man begged repeatedly to be killed. [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) performed the mercy kill. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) suggested burning the body; all agreed. The party debated their next move — ambush, shelter among the monoliths, or pressing on to the mill — and chose to continue to the mill. [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) asked [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) to set a poisoned dart trap inside the cave before leaving; [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) agreed and set it.

### Approach to the Mill

The party moved through a section of forest before emerging onto a road without cart tracks. As they neared the mill, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) noticed the raven that had been following the party overhead, apparently trying to warn them of something. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) noted the party had no way to communicate with it. [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) golden sword did not point toward the mill.

[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) proposed the party enter through the front while he climbed to the top of the mill's onion-dome roof. He asked [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) to maintain their mental link. [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) found the upper entry point unsealed — unlike the shuttered windows around the mill's exterior — and signaled the others that entry was possible.

### Inside the Mill — Koselina

Before the party could act, the mill door opened. A very young woman in an apron, dusted with flour, greeted them. She introduced herself as [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}). The scent from inside was described as wonderful.

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}), [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) were invited inside. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) asked [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) for her sisters' names; she answered **Bela** and **Morgantha**. When [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) probed about the origin of the pies — mentioning he had trouble refusing them — [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) said they were simply made with wolf meat.

Meanwhile, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) discovered bags upstairs containing severed arms. At the same moment, a large, older sister who had been sleeping awoke and called out to [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) asking who was there.

### Combat Begins

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) opened the mill door to let [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) and [Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) inside.

[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) seized [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) as a hostage, holding a blade to her throat and ordering her to say everything was fine.

The older, heavy-set sister — [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}) — threatened that the party must drop their weapons or their friend would be killed. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) immediately moved upstairs. Combat erupted across multiple floors:

- **[Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}})** — the heavy-set older sister — was on the lower floor and paralyzed [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}).
- **[Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}})** — was encountered by [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) on the second floor, but quickly ascended to the third.
- **[Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}})** — was enchanted by [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and sent back to making pies, neutralizing her as a threat.
- **[Ireena Kolyana]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}})** — remained on a lower floor fighting an unseen creature and had not yet reached the upper floors.

The session ended mid-combat.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The tortured man was killed as an act of mercy; his body was burned.
- A poisoned dart trap was set in the cave by [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}).
- The mill sisters are identified: [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}), [Offalia]({{< ref "/npcs/offalia" >}}), and [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}). [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) referred to her sisters as Bela and Morgantha — it is unclear whether Offalia and Morgantha are the same person or different individuals.
- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) has been paralyzed by [Bela]({{< ref "/npcs/bela" >}}).
- [Koselina]({{< ref "/npcs/koselina" >}}) has been enchanted by [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and is neutralized.
- Combat is ongoing across multiple floors of the mill.
