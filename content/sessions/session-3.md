## Summary

After a long rest inside the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}), the party climbed to the attic, where two grim discoveries awaited: the bloodied body of [Margaret Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}) locked inside a chest, and the spirits of the two children — [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) — who were revealed to be ghosts. The party promised the spirits they would deal with the monster below, left the dog Lancelot as company, and learned how to access the basement. The descent proved the most personal for [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), who heard his inner demon's voice again — and paid the price for defying it.

## Events

### Long Rest

The party took a long rest inside the [Durst House]({{< ref "/locations/durst-house" >}}). During this time, [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) managed to repair [Walter Durst's]({{< ref "/npcs/walter-durst" >}}) music box, though only barely. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) was the only party member who did not complete the rest.

### Discovery of the Attic

[Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) recalled having seen, from outside the house, what appeared to be a third floor — an attic. The party searched for and found the access, then climbed up.

### Margaret's Body

In one of the attic rooms, the party opened a chest and found the bloodied corpse of a woman. The body appeared to be that of [Margaret Durst]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}). A letter was found alongside the chest.

### The Spirits of Rose and Thorn

In another attic room, the party encountered the spirits of the two children: [Rosalva ("Rose")]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt ("Thorn")]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}). The party made a promise to the spirits: they would deal with the monster imprisoned in the basement and return to support them.

The child spirits also shared how to access the basement — through a staircase descending from the attic itself.

The party left their dog, Lancelot, with the spirits to keep them company during the wait.

### Jimmy's Demon

Before leaving the spirits, [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) heard voices again — the familiar demands of the demonic entity bound to him. [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) instructed Jimmy to take his hand. Jimmy refused, and suffered damage as a consequence.

Shortly after, [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) chose to reveal to the rest of the party that he carries a demon that makes demands of him — and that their relationship is not one of trust.

### Descent to the Basement

Armed with the children's directions, the party began their descent through the attic staircase toward the basement. The children warned that the monster is too powerful even for all four of them.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party promised [Rosalva]({{< ref "/npcs/rosalva-durst" >}}) and [Thornboldt]({{< ref "/npcs/thornboldt-durst" >}}) they would deal with the monster and return.
- Lancelot the dog was left in the attic with the spirits.
- [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) defied his demon's demand and took damage as a result.
- The party now knows [Jimmy]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) harbors a demon — a secret he had kept until this moment.

## Loose Threads

- What is written in the letter found next to [Margaret's]({{< ref "/npcs/margaret-durst" >}}) body?
- Can the party fulfill their promise to the child spirits?
- What is the monster in the basement, and can the party survive the encounter?
- What are the full extent and limits of Jimmy's demonic bond?
