## Summary

Following the Burgomaster's funeral, the party prepared for their departure with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) from [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}})—but their escape was intercepted by [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) himself. The vampire appeared in wolf form before transforming into a humanoid shape. He revealed his omniscience regarding their plans and, despite his indifference to their escaping with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), challenged them to prove themselves. In a calculated act to protect the group, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) accepted [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) challenge and entered an abandoned house for combat. Though [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) fought valiantly, [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) power overwhelmed him. The party rallied, with [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) intervening to protect their companion and drive [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) away. The encounter provided critical insight into the vampire's strength and psychology, but left the party battered and shaken.

## Events

### The Funeral's Conclusion
The Burgomaster's funeral ceremony in the [Barovia Church]({{< ref "/locations/church-barovia" >}}) concluded without further interruption.

### Discovery of Donavich's Tragedy
[Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}) observed that [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}) wiped sweat from his brow with a cloth bearing signs of blood. Upon inquiring with [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) about the priest, [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) revealed that [Donavich]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}) lost his own son, who remains in a state of undeath—likely the source of the whispered screams heard during the funeral ceremony within the church.

### Departure Preparations
The party prepared to leave [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}).

### Meeting Dirko
The party encountered **[Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}})**, revealed to be [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) "secret" partner or associate.

### The Appearance of Strahd
As the party prepared to depart, [Count Strahd von Zarovich]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) suddenly appeared in **wolf form**, then transformed into a **humanoid shape** before the party's eyes.

#### The Enchantment of Ismark
[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) cast a magical enchantment on [Ismark]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}), rendering him unable to act.

#### The Vampire's Discourse
[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) engaged the party directly, focusing his attention on [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}):

- He questioned [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) about the party's leadership structure.
- When asked about their plan to remove [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}) from [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}), [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) attempted to deflect, but [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) remained incisively direct.
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) revealed his omniscience: he possesses "eyes and ears" throughout [Barovia]({{< ref "/locations/village-of-barovia" >}}) and knows of all that transpires.
- He expressed indifference to the party's escape with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), stating they may do as they wish—**provided they do not damage his reputation with her**.
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) issued a challenge: he would respect [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) more if the party would enter an abandoned house so they could "speak privately."

#### Monel's Acceptance
Recognizing the danger to the group, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) made the calculated decision to accept [Strahd's]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) challenge. He invited the vampire to enter the abandoned house with him.

### The Combat in the Abandoned House

#### First Exchange
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) attacked [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) twice, dealing mediocre damage on one occasion.
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) responded with a devastating **critical hit** using unarmed strikes (1d8 + 4 + 4d6 damage), nearly killing [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) instantly.

#### The Party's Intervention
- [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) was the first to reach the house. He **kicked down the door** and drew his dagger.
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) turned his fury upon [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}), striking with unarmed attacks.
- [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) **provoked** [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) and attempted **Shocking Grasp**.
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) used a **legendary maneuver** to resist the magical attack and grabbed [Pepino's]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) arm.
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) intervened to protect [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}).
- [Pepino]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) provoked again and used **Misty Step** to escape the danger zone, earning his inspiration point.

#### Strahd's Departure
[Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) turned his back to the party and **walked away**, ending the confrontation.

### The Aftermath
- [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) rushed to [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) aid to prevent his death.
- [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}), [Jimmy Slit]({{< ref "/players/jimmy-slit" >}}), and [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) joined the party to assess damages and plan next steps.
- The full implications and discussion of the session's events remain ongoing.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) sacrificed his own safety to protect the group from greater harm.
- The party gained critical insight: [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) will not pursue them beyond certain boundaries if they maintain his reputation with [Ireena]({{< ref "/npcs/ireena-kolyana" >}}).
- [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) demonstrated that homes can serve as safe spaces—he will not follow into a dwelling he is not invited into.

## Loose Threads

- What is the nature of [Donavich's]({{< ref "/npcs/donavich" >}}) undead son?
- What is [Dirko's]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) role and why is he [Ismark's]({{< ref "/npcs/ismark" >}}) "secret" partner?
- What lies ahead on the journey to [Vallaki]({{< ref "/locations/vallaki" >}})?
- Can the party's injury be fully healed before continuing?
- What other powers or servants does [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) command?

## Rewards & Lessons Learned

- **Tactical Insight:** The party discovered that [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) operates within certain boundaries of social propriety and will not enter a home uninvited.
- **Psychological Insight:** [Strahd]({{< ref "/npcs/strahd-von-zarovich" >}}) values respect and can be provoked, but is far more powerful than any individual party member.
- **Survival:** The party survived a direct encounter with a vampire lord, achieving a tactical withdrawal.
- **Character Development:** [Monel's]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) selfless decision to protect the group demonstrated leadership under pressure.
