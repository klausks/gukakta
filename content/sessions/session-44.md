## Summary

At the hidden Martikov camp near [Uncle Billy’s Orchard]({{< ref "/locations/uncle-billy-orchard" >}}), the party is welcomed with wine and food as preparations are made to receive them. The presence of children lifts the mood, even as [Davian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/davian-martikov" >}}) and [Muriel Winchov]({{< ref "/npcs/muriel-winchov" >}}) appear to be discussing something serious.

[Adrian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/adrian-martikov" >}}) explains why [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}) no longer supports them, outlines the threats inside the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}), and reveals details about a [green gem]({{< ref "/lore/three-gems >}}) powering the attackers. The party also learns that the Martikovs maintain a raven-based spy network and can offer it as part of the mission reward.

With the winery reconnaissance mapped out—including entrances, likely dangers, and the best route to avoid vine blights—Vedas’ owl [Isha]({{< ref "/creatures/isha" >}}) completes an aerial sweep, giving the party an edge for infiltration.

## Events

### Reception at the Camp
The party is served wine and food while the camp organizes their reception. Children bring a livelier tone to the place.

Davian and Muriel are seen in serious conversation.

Muriel feeds ravens with rats—and also eats from them. Others in the camp eat roasted wolf.

Two ravens deliver papers to Muriel; she opens them, reads, and keeps them.

### Adrian’s Briefing
Adrian shares several key points:

- **Krezk no longer helps them**, despite longstanding tradition; Krezk is stricter with outsiders than Vallaki, and it also has burgomasters.
- The most persistent threat has been **blights**—spiky, root-like humanoid pests that try year after year to drain the life from the vines. They do not speak; they attack at range or restrain with thorns. See: [Vine Blights]({{< ref "/creatures/vine-blight" >}}).
- There are also **forest humanoids/creatures** associated with the blights, speaking a strange language, and they have been seen inside the winery. See: [Forest Humanoids (Winery)]({{< ref "/creatures/forest-humanoids-winery" >}}).
- The party’s earlier road encounter mercenaries may be connected to the [Lords of the Silver Dragon]({{< ref "/lore/lords-of-the-silver-dragon" >}}).

The party drinks a special wine: **Purple Great Mash, No. 3**.

A story is mentioned about the Count and an inheritance passed to the Kreskovs, but the details remain unclear.

### Raven Spies
When Igga asks whether the ravens circling the camp are “their cousins,” Adrian confirms they are the Martikovs’ fastest spies.

He admits they have a **hybrid form** and are **lycanthropes**. See: [Martikov Spy Network]({{< ref "/lore/martikov-spy-network" >}}).

[Stefania]({{< ref "/npcs/stefania" >}}) and [Dark]({{< ref "/npcs/dark" >}}) consider traveling to Vallaki, fearing the town will soon run out of wine.

### The Situation at the Winery
The party is told the following:

- Inside the winery, the forest humanoids are supported by a **swarm of small, spiky creatures** (vine blights).
- Adrian wants to know the forest humanoids’ true objective.
- A [**green gem**]({{< ref "/lore/three-gems" >}}) empowers the creatures. It is currently with **two wild women** and **a bearded man** carrying a **staff**; they came from [Berez]({{< ref "/locations/berez" >}}), from the swamp.
- Muriel is attempting to charm one of them to learn more about their origins.
- The staff is considered the most important objective; taking it would be a strong affront to [Baba Lysaga]({{< ref "/npcs/baba-lysaga" >}}), described as an ancient old woman who lives in the swamp and is an ally of Strahd.
- The party is expected to **interrogate** one of the forest humanoids to understand their relationship to Strahd.
- A **dawn elf** adventurer-merchant is said to be trapped inside the winery. See: [Dawn Elf Merchant]({{< ref "/npcs/dawn-elf-merchant" >}}).

### Rewards and Reconnaissance
The Martikovs offer:

- **250 gp** reward
- **Access to and use of the spy network**

Adrian suggests a reconnaissance of the winery:

- There are **five** entrances/exits.
- There is **no** entrance/exit via the stable, despite it being attached to the winery.
- Left of the stable is a door leading to the **storage**.
- To the south, there is a door that is **unlocked and intact**.
- The **storage** is on the upper part of the winery.

Isha performs an aerial sweep of the property. The party expects an advantage on stealth/evasion and identifies a better route to avoid vine blights among the vines.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party agrees to proceed toward the winery with reconnaissance information rather than rushing in blind.
- The party accepts interrogation of a forest humanoid as a key mission objective.

## Loose Threads

- What is the Count/Kreskov inheritance story that Adrian alluded to?
- What is the green gem’s origin and why does it empower the attackers?
- Who is the dawn elf merchant trapped inside the winery?
- What is the true goal of the forest humanoids inside the winery?
