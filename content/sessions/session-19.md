## Summary

The party faces two back-to-back duels at the Vistani encampment. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) and [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) fight [Yakov]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}) and his flaming magic skull, emerging victorious. [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) then face [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) and [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}), who crash the event, and defeat both opponents. [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) is recognized by [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) as a Forest Master.

## Events

### Early Morning at the Encampment

The party woke in the early hours of the morning. As they gathered at the center of the camp, an elderly Vistani—apparently the leader of the group—approached them to ask about the duel. He appeared visibly apprehensive.

While eating and talking amongst themselves, the party overheard another Vistani remark: *"Wine in Barovia is running out. Maybe that's a good way for me to stop suffering from so many hangovers."*

### The Call to Combat

[Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) called the party to combat. [Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}}) cast *Aid* on himself, [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}), and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}) in preparation.

At the dueling ground, the party observed archers and figures that appeared to be druids positioned around the perimeter. Standing nearby was a figure in fine red armor who moved with notable agility—[Johma]({{< ref "/npcs/johma" >}}), revealed to be [Yakov's]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}) mentor.

### The First Duel — Monel & Igga vs. Yakov & the Flaming Skull

[Yakov]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}), a dark-haired human, was the challenger who had demanded the Forest Master title. Before combat began, he dramatically threw a cloth into the air to reveal his second combatant: a **flaming magic skull**.

Moments before the fight, [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) recalled a phrase from his private session with [Madame Iva]({{< ref "/npcs/madame-iva" >}}): *"Battle is my destiny. Pain, my guide. Victory, my truth."*

As the duel began, [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) appeared uninvited at the edge of the dueling ground. [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) noticed and remarked: *"That's not great."*

- **[Igga Bevneros]({{< ref "/players/igga-bevneros" >}})** destroyed the flaming skull with a devastating *Vicious Mockery*.
- **[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}})** killed [Yakov]({{< ref "/npcs/yakov" >}}) with a shield bash to the face.

**Rewards from the first duel:**
- Pike +1
- Chain Mail
- Gold (amount to be determined), 7 platinum pieces, 32 copper pieces
- A mystic cloth

### The Second Duel — Agares & Pepino vs. Perwimple & Dirko

With [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) present, a second duel was initiated between them and [Agares]({{< ref "/players/agares" >}}) and [Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}}).

- **[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}})** killed [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) with *Shocking Grasp*. His body melted in a bizarre and unnatural manner.
- **[Pepino Falstaff]({{< ref "/players/pepino-falstaff" >}})** killed [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) with a *Chromatic Orb* (fire).

The rewards from the second duel were not yet collected.

### Aftermath

[Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) was formally recognized by [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}) as a **Forest Master**.

## Decisions & Consequences

- [Dirko]({{< ref "/npcs/dirko" >}}) and [Perwimple]({{< ref "/npcs/perwimple" >}}) are dead.
- [Monel]({{< ref "/players/monel" >}}) is now a Forest Master, recognized by [Esdras]({{< ref "/npcs/esdras" >}}).
- The party has still not collected the loot from the second duel.
