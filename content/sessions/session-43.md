## Summary

Within the [Mysterious Temple]({{< ref "/locations/mysterious-temple" >}}), the party uncovers a hidden mechanism behind a dwarf statue and—after resting—pushes deeper into sealed catacombs.

They dispatch a group of dwarf zombies and discover a chamber of tombs centered around the grave of [Thargrim Dûrvold]({{< ref "/npcs/thargrim-durvold" >}}), "the Stone Guardian." The tombs hold lingering magic: abjuration light, transmutation auras, and a strange test that seems to judge the opener’s nature.

Back on the [Svalich Road]({{< ref "/locations/svalich-road" >}}), the party notices dangerous, spore-heavy mushrooms and reaches a new fork: a northern archway leading into the forest, snowy mountains to the south, and the road onward toward [Krezk]({{< ref "/locations/krezk" >}}) and the [Wizard of Wines Winery]({{< ref "/locations/wizard-of-wines-winery" >}}).

Choosing the winery route, they arrive at [Uncle Billy’s Orchard]({{< ref "/locations/uncle-billy-orchard" >}}) and are guided to a hidden camp. Agares introduces the party "in the name of" [Danika Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/danika-martikov" >}}), which eases the camp’s tension. The elder introduces himself as [Davian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/davian-martikov" >}}), and his son [Adrian Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/adrian-martikov" >}}) offers food and drink while promising to explain the situation.

A crow lands, transforms into a woman, and explains that the camp can hear them and that [Urwin Martikov]({{< ref "/npcs/urwin-martikov" >}}) and Danika sent them days ago. She introduces herself as [Muriel Winchov]({{< ref "/npcs/muriel-winchov" >}}) before leaving.

## Events

### The Dwarf Statue and the Hidden Door
In the room Jimmy opened, the party finds a statue of a dwarf that does not resemble anyone they recognize. Agares notices thin cracks behind the statue and smells undead beyond.

Vedas and Jimmy identify an opening mechanism built into the statue. The group agrees to rest and continue the next day.

### Zombie Dwarves
After solving the mechanism, the party opens the way and finds undead: zombies—each of them dwarves. The fight is short and one-sided.

### The Tomb Chamber
Beyond the zombies lies a chamber with six tombs and one central, principal tomb.

On the principal tomb rests a magical sword. Light from within the tomb reads as **abjuration** magic. Agares lifts the sword and nothing happens; he sets it down again. Monel examines the weapon and determines it is at least a **+1 sword**.

The principal tomb bears an inscription: **Thargrim Dûrvold — the Stone Guardian**.

### The Uncorrupted Tomb
Among the six side tombs, the party finds that only one has not been corrupted. Inside, the body is barely decomposed. It wears a beautiful spiked armor and bears a battleaxe. The armor radiates an aura of **transmutation** magic.

### A Nature-Tested Opening
Vedas determines that "nature" (described by the party as a matter of disposition) affects who can open Thargrim’s principal tomb. He suggests Monel do so.

Monel opens the tomb and retrieves a war horn. Inspecting the heavily damaged armor inside, he notices the source of the tomb’s magic is a pair of ribs. He removes the ribs and feels a spirit pass through him, receiving a vision of a dwarf watching over him and a sense of safety.

### Back to the Road
Returning to the road, the party notices many mushrooms. Vedas identifies them as dangerous and highly spore-bearing.

At a new fork, the party identifies three routes:

- **North:** An archway leading directly to a trail into the forest. Farther ahead, Isha identifies an isolated castle within [Lake Baratok]({{< ref "/locations/lake-baratok" >}}). The party hears a legend of a powerful hermit gone mad there. See [Isolated Castle (Lake Baratok)]({{< ref "/locations/castle-in-lake-baratok" >}}).
- **North:** An archway leading directly to a trail into the forest. Farther ahead, Vedas’ owl [Isha]({{< ref "/creatures/isha" >}}) identifies an isolated castle within [Lake Baratok]({{< ref "/locations/lake-baratok" >}}). The party hears a legend of a powerful hermit gone mad there. See [Isolated Castle (Lake Baratok)]({{< ref "/locations/castle-in-lake-baratok" >}}).
- **South:** Snowy mountains.
- **Along the road:** Toward Krezk and the winery.

### The Martikov Camp
The party follows the road toward the winery. At Uncle Billy’s Orchard, they receive a signal from a man who guides them to a clearing with beds, a campfire, and resting people.

Davian Martikov introduces himself as the patriarch. Adrian asks his father to let him continue the conversation, and he offers the party food and drink, promising to explain the situation.

Muriel Winchov arrives in crow form, transforms into a woman, and confirms the camp can hear them. She requests permission to leave and departs.

## Decisions & Consequences

- The party chooses to rest rather than force the statue mechanism immediately.
- The party allows Monel to open Thargrim’s principal tomb based on Vedas’ assessment.
- The party chooses the road onward to Krezk/the winery rather than taking the archway trail or heading south into the snow.

## Loose Threads

- Who built the statue mechanism, and why were the sealed tombs defended by dwarf zombies?
- What is the full nature of Thargrim’s tomb test, and what power resides in the ribs Monel removed?
- What is the origin and danger of the spore-heavy mushrooms along the road?
- What is the isolated castle in Lake Baratok, and who is the rumored mad hermit?
- What is the Martikov camp’s situation, and what will Adrian explain?
